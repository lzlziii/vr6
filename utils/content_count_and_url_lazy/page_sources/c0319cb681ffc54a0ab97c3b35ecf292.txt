<h2 class="h1">Privacy Policy</h2>

<p><strong>Updated December 14, 2022</strong><br>
We are Cyberspline Games Inc. (“Cyberspline” or “Developer” of VR game BoomBox) with a registered office in Canada, delivery address 905 Pender St W Fl 6, Vancouver, BC V6C 1L6, entered in the BC Registry Services Business Corporations Act under reference BC1235280.<br>
This document explains the use of data we collect from users who plays BoomBox and consists of two parts. By accepting this document, you acknowledge having understood the contents of both. The first part details the personal data we collect for the purpose of making the game BoomBox available to you, and how this data is used. The second part contains the full End-User License Agreement, which covers some additional topics.<br>
If you have problems understanding anything in this document, do not be afraid to contact us at boombox@cyberspline.com.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>COLLECTION OF INFORMATION</strong></span></p>
<p>We collect and store the player’s game platform username and platform account number (Steam, Meta Quest/Rift, Viveport, iQiyi, Pico, YVR, Pimax) with scores attributed to that username.<br>
We collect other anonymized information from your play of BoomBox.<br>
BoomBox does NOT collect the player’s name, physical address, contact information, or any geolocation information.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>USE OF INFORMATION</strong></span></p>
<p>We need to collect the user data described above (identification data and game use data) so that the game is functional, including leaderboards, multiplayer etc. We do not need your personal data in a form that enables your identification.</p>
<p><strong>GAME PLATFORM USERNAME</strong></p>
<p>Facilitate leaderboards for the player which also display their Steam &amp; Meta Quest/Rift &amp; iQiyi &amp; YVR &amp; Pico &amp; Pimax friends.<br>
Facilitate multiplayer games and player-to-player interaction.<br>
We restricted players’ interaction options to keep you safe, and the interactions are available strictly via a pre-defined set of friendly emojis and phrases which you can select when in multiplayer mode.</p>
<p><strong>AGGREGATE, DE-IDENTIFIED PLAY DATA</strong></p>
<p>We aggregate de-identified play data to:<br>
– analyze and improve our game.<br>
– support the internal operations of BoomBox.</p>
<p>We aggregate all information collected so that it cannot be used to identify any particular player.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>THIRD-PARTY INTEGRATIONS</strong></span></p>
<p><strong>TWITCH CHAT INTEGRATION</strong></p>
<p>This is an additional modding tool you can use with the game to access your Twitch chat directly in-game. You don’t have to use this tool to fully enjoy BoomBox, the tool was implemented to simplify Twitch streaming.<br>
To enable the tool, go to BoomBox Menu -&gt; Streaming and then enable the Twitch Chat there.<br>
We do not collect any personal or user information or chat history from this tool; all details of every library included in this tool are available on GitHub* codes we utilized (see links below this document).<br>
You do not need to install this tool to play BoomBox, and access to this tool is purely voluntary.</p>
<p>Read more about Twitch Terms of Service <a href="https://www.twitch.tv/p/en/legal/terms-of-service/">HERE</a></p>
<p><strong>FACEBOOK</strong></p>
<p>Third-Party Tracking Technologies: Social Features, Activity and Movement Data, In-Game Purchases, Gameplay Information</p>
<p>Playing BoomBox on the Oculus Quest platform with a Facebook-linked Oculus Account enables rich data sharing of your activity within BoomBox to Facebook for social features native to the Facebook platform. These include but are not limited to: gameplay data for dates, times, song and difficulty played, scoreboard information, challenge participation details, Facebook friends, and information required for in-app purchases, In addition, players with Oculus Move activated will activate a collection of spatiotemporal virtual reality tracking data, fitness performance history and distances traveled on the Oculus Platform. Please note that this information is not collected by BoomBox, for more information, please see the Facebook Privacy Policy.</p>
<p><strong>Walk Those Brackets! (WTB) INTEGRATION</strong></p>
<p><strong><br>
</strong>This is an additional application you can use with the game to organize tournaments directly in-game. You do not need to use this application to fully enjoy BoomBox, and access to this application is purely voluntary.</p>
<p>By logging on WTB website, they will collect:</p>
<ul>
<li>Discord ID</li>
<li>Discord Name</li>
<li>Discord Avatar</li>
<li>Discord Discriminator</li>
</ul>
<p>By using WTB in-game, they will collect:</p>
<ul class="mb-5">
<li>Tournament selected</li>
<li>Map played</li>
<li>Score</li>
<li>Accuracy</li>
<li>Cut count</li>
<li>Max combo</li>
<li>Total note count</li>
</ul>
<p>Read more about WTB Terms of Service <a href="https://wtb.omedan.com/boombox/legal/privacypolicy">HERE</a></p>
<p><strong><br>
DATA RETAINING PERIOD </strong></p>
<p>We retain the data described above for the time for which you play BoomBox and at the maximum until you delete your account on any platform which you used to purchase our game or until you ensure the complete erasure of BoomBox from your library on any given platform. We will also delete the data:</p>
<p>a. Whenever the User Data is no longer necessary to provide BoomBox experience or service to you;<br>
b. When requested by you (see the section below for more information);<br>
c. When you delete your account associated with BoomBox; NOTE: if you published a custom map and indicated your nickname on that map, that map and your nickname will still be visible on that map even after deletion of your account. If you would like to remove all your maps from our server, please contact us via email and we will remove them.<br>
d. When requested by Facebook;<br>
e. When required by law or regulation;</p>
<p><strong>DATA ACCESS BY OTHER PARTIES</strong></p>
<p>Apart from us, your personal data are also processed by the platforms through which you have purchased our game:</p>
<p>Steam</p>
<p>Oculus (Meta)</p>
<p>SideQuest</p>
<p>Pico</p>
<p>iQiyi</p>
<p>YVR</p>
<p>PIMAX</p>
<p>Your user data are also processed by Unity, which ensures the creation and management of statistics concerning our game as mentioned above. (Privacy policy available at: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy)</p>
<p><strong>YOUR RIGHTS</strong></p>
<p>You have the right to access the processed personal data concerning you. We will provide this data to you free of charge on the basis of your request sent to the above-given postal address or by e-mail to boombox@cyberspline.com.<br>
Under any circumstances, you have the right to demand that we erase your personal data and we will do so.<br>
If you think that your personal data are no longer needed for the purpose for which they have been processed; if you believe that your personal data are processed by Cyberspline Games Inc. illegally; or if Cyberspline Games Inc. process personal data of a child without any parental consent given for such processing, you may demand that we erase your personal data.</p>
<p><strong>SECURITY OF DATA</strong></p>
<p>Although Cyberspline Games Inc. has access to the Personal Data and Usage Data described above from your acquisition and use of the Game, as set forth above, protection of any personally identifying Usage Data or Personal Data is our highest priority. Such Data is shared only with our Partners and Platform Holders to support your use of the Game, as described above. No method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.</p>
<p><strong>CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY</strong></p>
<p>We may revise this Privacy Policy, for example, to reflect changes in the law, our practices, or the features of BoomBox. These changes will be effective immediately upon posting. As a result, you should periodically review the Privacy Policy so that you are up to date on our most current policies and practices. By playing BoomBox, you agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy, and, by continuing to play BoomBox after changes have been made, you agree to the changes.</p>

<p>If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us: <a href="mailto:boombox@cyberspline.com">boombox@cyberspline.com</a><br>
