<p><em>Last updated: September 28, 2021</em></p>



<p>Protecting your privacy is important to us. We hope that the following statement helps you understand how Proeminent Code SRL (“we, us, our”) collects, uses and safeguards your personal information in context of our games (“the games, our games”).</p>



<p>This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. You should consult this privacy policy regularly for any changes.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Who we are</h2>



<p>Our website address is: https://www.proeminentcode.com.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What personal data we collect and why we collect it</h2>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Game Data</strong></h3>



<p>We collect and maintain Player Account Data and High Scores Data in our server. These data are automatically collected for all players of our games. Player Account Data contains Player’s Game State and Player’s credentials. Player’s Credentials in Player Account Data may allow our server to identify a player as an existing player when the player logs into the game from a new device. This allows our players to play with the same Player Account from many devices. Player’s Game State in Player Account Data allows the player to continue game right from where he or she left previously. High Scores Data allows us to show global high score lists and friend highscore lists in the game.</p>



<p>Player Account Data in our server contains following information:</p>



<ul><li>Player ID that is allocated by us and uniquely identifies single Player Account.</li><li>Name that Player has entered to his or her Player Acccount.</li><li>Country that Player has entered to his or her Player Account.</li><li>Steam User ID Hash that identifies the player in Steam if Steam is enabled for Player’s account.</li><li>Oculus User ID Hash that identifies the player in Oculus Store if Oculus is enabled for Player’s account.</li><li>Player’s game state: amounts of games played, selected ball, Steam name, Oculus name, owned installations, throw results and such.</li></ul>
