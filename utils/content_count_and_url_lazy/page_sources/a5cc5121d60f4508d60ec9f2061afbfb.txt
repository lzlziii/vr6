        <li aria-level="1"><strong>Identity Data</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> refers to data submitted or collected, that relates to the identity of an individual.</span></li>
        <li aria-level="1"><strong>Personal Data</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> refers to any Identity Data or Usage Data that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.</span></li>
        <li aria-level="1"><strong>Service</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> refers to the Application or the Website or both.</span></li>
        <li aria-level="1"><strong>Service Provider</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> means any natural or legal person who processes the data on behalf of the Company. It refers to third-party companies or individuals employed by the Company to facilitate the Service, to provide the Service on behalf of the Company, to perform services related to the Service or to assist the Company in analyzing how the Service is used.</span></li>
        <li aria-level="1"><strong>Website</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> refers to TimeLooper, accessible from </span><a href="https://www.timelooper.com/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">www.TimeLooper.com</span></a></li>
        <li aria-level="1"><strong>Usage Data</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> refers to data collected automatically, generated by the use of the Service.</span></li>
        <li aria-level="1"><strong>You</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> means the individual using the Service, or the company, or other legal entity on behalf of which such individual is accessing or using the Service, as applicable.</span></li>
        </ul>
        <h2><strong>Your Personal Data</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Your Personal Data is comprised of two categories: </span><strong>Identity Data</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and </span><strong>Usage Data</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
        <h2><strong>Indentity Data</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">While using Our Service, you will </span><strong><em>never </em></strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">be asked to submit any identity data.</span></p>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Identity Data definitionally include, but is not limited to:</span></p>
        <ul>
            <li aria-level="1">First and Last Name</li>
            <li aria-level="1">Account Name</li>
            <li aria-level="1">Email address</li>
        </ul>
        <h2><strong>Usage Data</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">While using Our Service, Usage Data is collected automatically. Usage Data is generally anonymous, and does not include any personally identifiable data.</span></p>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Usage Data may include, but is not limited to:</span></p>
        <ul>
            <li aria-level="1">Areas of our Service that You use</li>
            <li aria-level="1">The time and date of Your use</li>
            <li aria-level="1">The time spent on those Areas</li>
            <li aria-level="1">Your Device's manufacturer, type, and version</li>
            <li aria-level="1">Your Device's identifiers and diagnostic data</li>
        </ul>
        <h2><strong>Disclosure of Your Personal Data</strong></h2>
        <p>Because we do not collect your personal data, we cannot, and do not sell, rent, or lease any of Your Personal Data to third parties.</p>
        <h2><strong>Removal of Your Personal Data</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If at any time and for any reason you have concerns regarding the status of your personal information please email us with your request at </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">privacy@TimeLooper.com</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
        <h2><strong>Personal Data from Minors</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our Service is intended for general audiences, and not for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information (as defined under the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act, also known as COPPA) from children under the age of 13 years old. We will not ask for the personal information of children under the age of 13 years old, and will only collect information from children for limited purposes that are consistent with applicable law. If We learn that a child under the age of 13 has provided us with personal information that is not subject to a COPPA exception, we will immediately delete the information upon receiving such notice.</span></p>
        <h2><strong>Links to Other Websites</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our Service may contain links to other websites that are not operated by Us. If You click on a third party link, You will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise You to review the Privacy Policy of every site You visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites or services.</span></p>
        <h2><strong>Dispute Resolution</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We are committed to resolving complaints about our collection or use of Your Personal Data.</span></p>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you have any questions or comments about our use of Personal Data collected through the use of our Service, or about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">privacy@TimeLooper.com</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">. We will use reasonable efforts to promptly investigate any complaint you have regarding our use of such information and to comply fully with the legal and regulatory authorities responsible for enforcing our adherence to the privacy principles stated above.</span></p>
        <h2><strong>Changes to this Privacy Policy</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. We will let You know via email and/or a prominent notice on Our Service, prior to the change becoming effective and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.</span></p>
        <h2><strong>Contact Us</strong></h2>
        <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, You can contact us by email: </span><a href="mailto:privacy@timelooper.com"><span style="font-weight: 400;">privacy@timelooper.com</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> or through our website: </span><a href="https://www.timelooper.com/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">www.TimeLooper.com</span></a></p>
