		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<p>This is an extreme VR experience that simulates sudden and dramatic acceleration, freefalling, twisting and rolling. <strong>Even with the 90 FPS frame rate, it is not for VR-sensitive players</strong>. Detached’s in-game helmet and HUD are both designed to counter VR nausea. Having said that, players will rotate and accelerate in all directions in the game.</p>
<p>For many, this will create a wonderfully intense VR experience. But for those more susceptible to VR nausea, this level of movement may prove uncomfortable. Although Detached has been calibrated to introduce players to the controls slowly, this should not be the first game you pick up in VR.</p>

Also all rotations are suppressed when there is no input.</span></li>
