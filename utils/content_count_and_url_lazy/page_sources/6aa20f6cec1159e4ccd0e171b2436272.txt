		<h2>Turtle VR</h2>
	</header>
	<div class="entry-content">
		<h1 style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/413750/Turtle_VR/">⇒ BUY TODAY ON STEAM ⇐</a></h1>
<p><strong>Turtle VR</strong> brings the Logo turtle into the 21st century. Use an interface similar to MIT Scratch, and Google Blockly, to program a turtle to draw your creations within various 3D worlds.</p>
<p><iframe title="Turtle VR Launch Trailer" width="598" height="336" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/j8kwOW6PL4k?feature=oembed" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen=""></iframe></p>
<p>Using a tablet style interface, combined with Google’s Blockly, you are able to drive a programmable turtle in real-time while in VR. Draw and explore geometry using the code blocks Turtle VR provides, and browse through the provided catalog of programs.</p>
<p>Block-based programming makes it easy for anyone to get started.</p>
