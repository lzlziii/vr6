							<p style="font-weight:normal; text-align:justify;"><strong>
We are a small but strong team based in Cannes on the French Riviera passionated about video games and new technologies. We've started some months ago by creating a free application called "A TIME IN SPACE VR", framing the time space. This one became quickly on the top of the VR downloads (over 380,000 downloads so far with a rate of 4.4/5).<br><br>
We then launched "A TIME IN SPACE 2" at the beginning of the year 2016 and rated 4.4/5 on Playstore, this unique experience in space is a much longer version and lively. It also offers a mini game where you need to go, through asteroid fields.<br><br>

In April 2016 our first Rollercoaster is launched, Find your way throught two long path in Fantasyland and Adventure Land, He's now became the best rated VR Rollercoaster all platform mixed with 4.5/5. (Available on Android, iOS, Gear VR, Oculus Rift and Viveport)<br><br>

In Summer 2017, we have launch "Bad Dream" on Android and Gear VR, this game allow you to move with your real Gesture in the game ! With amazing graphic and sound atmosphere he is allready the best Rated Horror VR app on Playstore. <br>

In 2018 We have launch "Happy World Land", an amazing Attraction park where you can play with your friend and "Excessive Speed", a racing game in Augmented Reality where you can play in real environnment.<br>

Our latest game "Darkness Rollercoaster - Ultimate Shooter Edition" is released on PSVR in 2019 in Europe and 2020 in North America, It's a rail Shooter game with many possibilities of movement and interactions.  <br><br>

Our little touch:<br>
Our applications are at the forefront of mobile optimization, we do regular important updates in order to make it look more attractive in time.
We also take great care &amp; enjoy answering our users comments.<br><br>

The following :<br>
We are currently developping BAD DREAM - First Contact for PSVR and Steam VR, a bigger version of our game released in 2017 on Mobile Platform.
We are also preparing a VR game, which will be revolutionary in the concept ... but it's still too early to talk about it...<br>
