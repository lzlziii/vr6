        <h1>ABOUT RENTO MONOPOLY GAME</h1>
        <p>
          Rento is online multiplayer monopoly board game.<br>
          The game is for 2 to 8 players and you can play it online versus friends, offline versus robots, on same phone with friends or on WiFi.<br>
          You can also invite your facebook friends to play.<br><br>
          In this family board game you trade lands, build houses, win auctions, go and most importantly - have fun. Your goal is to acquire monopoly in the game and to bankrupt the other players by upgrading your lands and getting high rentals.
          The game has lots of <a class="ul" href="#" onclick="$(&quot;html, body&quot;).animate({ scrollTop: $(&quot;#extras&quot;).offset().top }, 1000); return false;">different boards</a> on which you play, <a class="ul" href="#" onclick="$(&quot;html, body&quot;).animate({ scrollTop: $(&quot;#extras&quot;).offset().top }, 1000); return false;">custom pawns</a>, <a class="ul" href="#" onclick="$(&quot;html, body&quot;).animate({ scrollTop: $(&quot;#extras&quot;).offset().top }, 1000); return false;">custom dice</a>, other settings for different house rules and much more.

    If you go to <a class="lime" href="/Leaderboard">Leaderboard stats page</a> you can find all of Rento top players, ranked in 4 categories: Top 20 ever, Top Today, Top Weekly and Top Monthly.<br>
    These stats are updated every couple of hours (especially the daily rankings). <br>
    The daily, weekly and monthly top charts are calculated by the number of wins that each player has managed to score. <br>
    The "TOP 20 EVER" ranking is calculated with the use of the <a rel="nofollow" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elo_rating_system">ELO rating system</a>. 
    Each new player starts with 1000 ELO points. <br>The idea is that if you win against higher ranking player than you, you will get more of his ELO points, than what he will get from you if he beats you. Read more in Wikipedia to learn how your score is calculated.<br>

    <h3 id="tournamentsRegister" class="title">MONOPOLY TOURNAMENTS</h3>
    Every Sunday we are scheduling global Rento monopoly tournament in which thousands of players can register.<br>
    Tournaments are starting <p class="lime ul">EACH SUNDAY 1 PM NEW YORK TIME (UTC -05)</p><br>
    In the beginning of the tournament each player is joined to 4 players room in which the winner plays with 3 of the winners of the other rooms.<br>
    Each time you win in room, you play on the next level with other winners and this pyramid goes until only one winner remains<br>
    This means that to win in tournament you have to play between 3 and 7 games one after another<br>
    Each game is limited to 45 or 50 minutes in play to avoid prolonged games. In this case the winner is the player with most assets (capital money + property land cost)<br>
    You can register for the tournaments from the game itself or from our WEB portal <a class="lime ul" href="https://BoardGamesOnline.Net/Tournaments">Board Games Online - Tournaments</a><br>
    The top 20 tournament finishers are always winning something (Credits, Coins and VIP status for our WEB portal BoardGamesOnline.Net)<br>
