            <h1>Beach Body Bros</h1>
            
            <p>
                Beach Body Bros is a 4 minute room scale VR experience for the <a href="https://www.oculus.com/experiences/rift/1810750645607190/" target="blank">Oculus Rift</a> &amp; <a href="http://store.steampowered.com/app/575420/Chocolate/" target="blank">HTC Vive</a>.
                <br><br> 
                Presented by <a href="https://www.oculus.com/medium/">Oculus Medium</a>, Beach Body Bros sets you as a puny wimp marching on a mirrored beach world filled with encouraging bros.  
                As you march and pump iron your muscles quickly bulk up (like a weekend costco run) and you level up to heavier weights while rocking to a wicked 80s montage track.   
                The bros watch and cheer you on in amazement as you transform into a hulking pile of meat, glistening in the sunset.  
                <br><br>
                Beach Body Bros is a test case for using Oculus medium as a production ready sculpting tool. Read more about that <a href="https://developer.oculus.com/blog/developer-perspective-using-oculus-medium-for-3d-production/" target="blank">HERE</a>.
            </p>
