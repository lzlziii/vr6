<h2 id="-an-interactive-vr-game-" style="cursor: pointer;"><strong>+ An Interactive VR Game</strong></h2>
<p style="display: none;">Humble Pie is a room-scale interactive VR game that allows players to humbly deliver cherry pies directly to a ficticious President. This satire-driven therapeutic experience with a retro 8-bit soundtrack is a fun antidote to the relentless current headlines.</p>
<p style="display: none;"><img src="https://static1.squarespace.com/static/5a94aef431d4df77f27be3d0/5a985dc653450a6689c4eba9/5a985dc6ec212d62633e387c/1519934922291/2.jpg?format=2500w" alt="Humble Pie Game"></p>
<p style="display: none;">This FPT (first-person-tossing) game debuts a series that will include other fictitious high- profile individuals beyond politics, who could perhaps use a nice helping of humble pie.</p>
<h2 id="-the-studio-behind-humble-pie-" style="cursor: pointer;"><strong>+ The Studio Behind Humble Pie</strong></h2>
<p style="display: none;"><a href="http://sunnyboyentertainment.com">SunnyBoy Entertainment</a> is a full-service creative studio specializing in a new era of entertainment. After creating top-tier animation and VR experiences for blockbuster film franchises, SunnyBoy has been developing original AR/VR intellectual properties for global distribution. Humble Pie marks the debut of their first original interactive VR game. Their roster includes VR experiences for Warner Bros. and New Line Cinemas IT: Escape From Pennywise that debuted at Comic Con 2017, Annabelle Creation: Bees Room, The Conjuring 2: Experience Enfield and 20th Century Foxs The Greatest Showman: Come Alive 360, among others.  SunnyBoy continues to delve into the computer, mobile and video game world, with more exciting projects on the horizon.</p>
