<div class="anim-up delay-1">
<h2 class="page-title grunge-text" itemprop="headline"><span class="grunge-2 anim-fade delay-3"></span><span class="over-grunge">Can you banish evil together?</span></h2>
</div>
</header>
<div class="entry-content anim-fade delay-3">
<ul class="margin-top nom-bottom">
<li>
<h3 class="p large-text uppercase"><strong>1-4 player online co-op</strong></h3>
<p class="nom-bottom">Take control of up to 4 cult members in this unique online co-op experience where you must work together to stop possessed cultists dead set on taking you to hell with them.</p>
</li>
<li>
<h3 class="p large-text uppercase"><strong>Single Player Mode</strong></h3>
<p class="nom-bottom">For hardcore players only. In this mode, you'll be doing all the screaming yourself.</p>
</li>
<li>
<h3 class="p large-text uppercase"><strong>Exorcise the demonically possessed</strong></h3>
<p class="nom-bottom">Your goal is to break Azazel's hold on the cultists. On each map, work together in a race against time to find key ritual items – some of which might be alive and unwilling - to complete the banishment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<h3 class="p large-text uppercase"><strong>Player progression</strong></h3>
<p class="nom-bottom">Players earn XP with each playthrough, increasing their Cult Rank and earning Ritual Tokens. These are used to unlock valuable Perks, which can mean the difference between living to banish another demon or an agonizing death.</p>
</li>
<li>
<h3 class="p large-text uppercase"><strong>Full VR support</strong></h3>
<p class="nom-bottom">Ready to turn it up a notch? Play DEVOUR in VR without any additional purchase necessary.</p>
</li>
</ul>
