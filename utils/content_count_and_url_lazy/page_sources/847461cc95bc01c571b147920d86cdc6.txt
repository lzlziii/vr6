<h4 class="">information collection, use, and sharing</h4>
<p class="">We are the sole owners of the information collected from our websites and applications.</p>
<p class="">On websites, we only have access to/collect information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone. We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us.</p>
<p>Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.</p>
<p class="">Additionally, we record usage data while you play ProShot VR applications and connect to the internet. This data includes but is not limited to Oculus ID, times used, user customization settings, and game scores. This data is used as part of services such as leaderboards. Scores associated with Oculus ID may be made publicly available through leaderboards, but personal data like settings are not made publicly available. We will only share such personal data with third parties in aggregate or anonymized. We will not sell or rent individually identifiable information from recorded telemetry to anyone.</p>
