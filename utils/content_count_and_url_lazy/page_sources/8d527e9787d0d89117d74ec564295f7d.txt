    <h1 style="text-align:center;" class="in-left">Ball Knight VR Privacy Policy</h1>

<p>
I care about your data!
<br>
<br>
Whilst having fun the last thing you want to worry about is your personal data being used in a way you dont agree with.
<br><br>
My games may collect score data for the purposes of populating a leaderboard for fun and competitive gameplay. You have the option to submit your score anonymously or to tag it with your initials. I never keep data such as your address, phone number or other personal information that does not directly relate to the game you are playing.
<br><br>
The data that is kept in my database after you submit a score includes the following fields; Score, Initials, Username, Date, Rank.
<br><br>
The data I keep is kept safe using industry best practices and I never share or sell it to third parties.
<br><br>
If you wish to have your data removed from the leaderboard database, Please feel free to contact me via the contact me form on this website or directly at joel.samuel.wall@gmail.com
<br><br>
The leaderboard data is available on this website and links to the leaderboard may exist on other parts of the internet.
<br><br>
You are free to refuse the request for your personal information, with the understanding that I may be unable to include you on any leaderboards or future services.
<br><br>
Your continued use of my website and applications will be regarded as acceptance of my practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how I handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact me via the contact form.
<br><br>
This policy is effective as of 6 Jan 2023.
<br>
</p>
