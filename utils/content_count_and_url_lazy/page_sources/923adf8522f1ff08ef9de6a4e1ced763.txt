			<p>All of this and more can be solved by using <em>virtual whiteboards</em>, and that is is exactly what <span>Virtual Idea Area</span> provides! <span>VIA</span> lets you create a <em>practically infinite amount</em> of virtual whiteboards, for which you can freely choose the <em>size, color and position</em>. It even lets you have <em>patterned whiteboards</em>. The markers don't smell, never run out, and they as well are available in any color you want.</p>
