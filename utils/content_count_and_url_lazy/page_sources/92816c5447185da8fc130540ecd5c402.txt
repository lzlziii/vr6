<h2>What Information Do We Collect?</h2>
<ul>

  </li>
  <li>

    history for the Services.
  </li>
  <li>

    training and academic research purposes.

  <li>
    <strong>Internet or other similar network activity</strong>: [1] We may collect your activity
    history, through cache, cookies, and REST API calls. [2] We may collect usage data and history,
    including, but not limited to, search terms entered, pages viewed, and other usage behavior
    identified by analytics events.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Other categories</strong>: [1] We may collect information from the content that you
    create and share publicly in the Services. [2] We may collect your responses to surveys that
