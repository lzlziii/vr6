                <hr class="divider">
<p class="text-muted mb-5">
This policy relates to data collected and handled via usage of the application Space Docker VR (SDVR) by us (Cat Commandos Ltd). We take privacy and information security seriously and aim to collect only the data required to provide services to you and to improve the products that we offer.

<h2 class="mb-4">Information We Collect and How it is Gathered</h2>
<p class="text-muted">We collect information automatically when you play SDVR, including the following items:
</p><ul class="text-muted mb-5">
<li>Platform Username and ID (i.e. Steam ID, Oculus ID).</li>
<li>Platform ID of friends who also own SDVR.</li>
<li>Your IP Address.</li>
<li>Information about your hardware:
<ul class="text-muted mb-5">
<li>CPU Model and Frequency, GPU Model, Total System Memory.
</li><li>Operating System Version.
</li><li>Virtual Reality Hardware.
</li></ul>
</li></ul>

<h2 class="mb-4">How We Use Your Information</h2>
<p class="text-muted">
We use the above information in the following ways:
</p><ul class="text-muted mb-5">
<li>To provide functionality in SDVR through online leaderboards.</li>
<li>To generate anonymised statistics relating to player counts, coarse locality and system information.</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p class="text-muted">
Under the General Data Protection Regular (GDPR), the lawful basis we rely on for processing this information are:
</p><ul class="text-muted mb-5">
<li>Our contractual obligation to provide services within SDVR.</li>
<li>Our legitimate interest in improving SDVR and other products.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="mb-4">How We Store Your Information</h2>
<p class="text-muted mb-5">

Information We Share with Third Parties
We do not share your information with third parties.
