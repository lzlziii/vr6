Some information - such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, device characteristics and information about your current game state

What information do we collect? <br>
The data received does not reveal your specific identity, such as your name. We collect some information about your playthroughs 
to help us figure out which levels you like and where you get stuck.<br> 
We use the “GameAnalytics” services to collect statistics about your playthroughs. 
“GameAnalytics” collects device information like IP address and device identifiers, as well as events completed or actions taken within the game, 
including level, number of tries, time to complete level. The “GameAnalytics” privacy policy can be found here: <a href="https://gameanalytics.com/privacy/">https://gameanalytics.com/privacy/</a><br><br>

We use “Playfab” to provide highscores in our game. “Playfab” stores information about your device such as device identifier and IP address. 
The “Playfab” privacy policy can be found here: <a href="https://playfab.com/privacy">https://playfab.com/privacy</a>.
The game is built using the Unity engine and Oculus Unity Plugin, these may also collect data such as device identifiers and IP address, please see their respective privacy policies for more information:
<a href="https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy">https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy</a> and <a href="https://www.oculus.com/legal/privacy-policy/">https://www.oculus.com/legal/privacy-policy/</a>
