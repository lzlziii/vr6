<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
<h2>LunaBeat VR Games</h2>
<p>
The headset platforms &amp; game distributors may collect headset activity data by default, and display limited anonymous information to us as developers.
</p><p>
The data retained and shown to the developers can be, but is not limited to, the following: Units sold, keys redeemed, total unique users, total active users at any given time, headsets used, frame rates, headset battery burn rates, available headset memory, reasons for refunds, time spent in the game, frequency of play sessions, and crashes.
</p><p>
We never share or sell your personally identifiable information.  We do not have access to delete or modify this information.  On occasion, we may privately share the total number of units sold, revenue, number of active users, and anonymous information about play sessions.
</p><p> 
We do not collect or store any anonymous or personally identifiable information through our games.  We only have access to the data shown to us via the developer dashboards.
</p><p>
Headset platform &amp; game distributors may collect and store data about your devices, time playing the game, and/or other personally identifiable information, but we do not have access to delete or modify this information.
It may be shown on your player profile page and any reviews you post.  We, the developers, have no control over this.
</p><p>
Our systems are not designed to associate personal information with your activities (such as pages you view or things you click on or search for).
</p><p>
We do not knowingly contact or collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly obtain parental consent or remove the information.
</p><p>
We do not store or share your precise location.
</p><p>
We do not use or collect your precise geographic location.
</p><p>
You cannot request to see your personal data, as we do not have a way to identify any of the anonymous users or the data associated with them.  This data is part of the user records from storefronts and is not able to be deleted.
</p><p>
The headset platforms &amp; game distributors may keep data indefinitely.
</p><p>
We do not share personally identifiable information (such as name, address, email or phone) with other companies.
</p><p>
No ad companies collect data through our service.
</p><p>
You can ask privacy questions.
<br>
If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policies, please contact us: privacy@lunabeat.com.
</p><p>
No analytics or third party vendors are used to track players within the game.
</p><p>
We take reasonable steps to secure your personally identifiable information against unauthorized access or disclosure.  However, no security or encryption method can be guaranteed to protect information from hackers or human error.
</p><p>
Information headset platforms &amp; game distributors collects may be stored or processed on computers located in any country where we and they do business.
</p><p>
Special situations may require disclosure of your data.
</p><p>
To operate the service, we also may make identifiable and anonymous information available to third parties in these limited circumstances (although we do not have a way to identify users in our data): (1) with your express consent, (2) when we have a good faith belief it is required by law, (3) when we have a good faith belief it is necessary to protect our rights or property, or (4) to any successor or purchaser in a merger, acquisition, liquidation, dissolution or sale of assets. Your consent will not be required for disclosure in these cases, but we will attempt to notify you, to the extent permitted by law to do so.
</p><p>
This privacy policy was last updated on Jan 17, 2023. Our privacy policy may change from time to time. If we make any material changes to our policies, we will place a prominent notice on our website or application. If the change materially affects registered users, we will send a notice to you by email, push notification or text.
