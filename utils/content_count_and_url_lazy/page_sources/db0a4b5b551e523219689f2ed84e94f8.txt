<p><strong>PRIVACY STATEMENT –</strong>&nbsp;<strong>VERTIGO</strong> </p>



<p><em>Version 1.2 – April 2023</em></p>



<ol>
<li><strong>Vertigo</strong></li>
</ol>



<p>Vertigo Studios B.V. and affiliated companies (’Vertigo’)&nbsp;are Dutch&nbsp;companies.&nbsp;We are&nbsp;active&nbsp;in the European Economic Area (EEA) and we keep our data on servers in the EEA, unless stated otherwise below.</p>



<p>We process your personal data when you use our games, applications, websites and software and when we contact you or if we have a business relationship with you.&nbsp;In this privacy statement we summarize when and how&nbsp;we&nbsp;collect,&nbsp;use and secure&nbsp;your personal data.</p>



<ol start="2">
<li><strong>General</strong></li>
</ol>



<p>We may change&nbsp;provisions of this privacy statement from time to time.&nbsp;If&nbsp;we do that, we will inform you of the changes.&nbsp;However, we also advise you&nbsp;to check&nbsp;for&nbsp;yourself from time to time whether the privacy statement has been changed.</p>



<ol start="3">
<li><strong>Which personal data do we collect and for which purposes?</strong></li>
</ol>



<p>There are a number of ways in which&nbsp;we&nbsp;can&nbsp;collect&nbsp;your&nbsp;personal data.&nbsp;In this section we explain which personal data we&nbsp;may&nbsp;collect&nbsp;from you.&nbsp;The personal data is processed according to your role and to the different processing purposes.&nbsp;The data retention period also differs depending on the processing goal. This period will also be mentioned below. Note that should there be any legal changes to the possible data retention periods, these legal changes will take precedent over the periods mentioned in this privacy statement. Please note that, insofar possible, we may anonymise your personal data for statistical and analytical purposes. Once personal data is anonymized it is no longer personal data as it cannot be traced back to you in any way. The retention periods below do not apply to anonymized personal data.</p>



<p>3.1 If you are a player of our games</p>



<p>A. To be able to provide you the game and the intended experience, we process the personal data mentioned below. Our processing ground is the performance of a contract with you.</p>



<p>If you play our games, we process the following personal data up to 10 years after your last use of the game. Our purpose is to provide you the game.</p>



<ul>
<li>Your in-game progression data</li>



<li>Your in-game friends and relations&nbsp;</li>
</ul>



<p>If you have to authenticate with a Vertigo account, we process the following personal data up to 10 years after your last use of the Vertigo account:</p>



<ul>
<li>E-mail address&nbsp;</li>



<li>User-ID or platform user-ID&nbsp;</li>



<li>Password</li>



<li>Which Vertigo games you own</li>



<li>Your platform (Steam, Oculus etc.)</li>
</ul>



<p>To provide matchmaking services, we process the following personal data up to 2 weeks after the matchmaking:</p>



<ul>
<li>Username</li>



<li>IP-address</li>
</ul>



<p>B. To be able to secure our games, we process your IP-address up until 1 year after collection of the IP-address or 1 year after an incident in which your IP-address was involved has been handled. Our processing ground is our legitimate interest to secure our games.</p>



<p>C. To be able to improve our games, we process the personal data mentioned below. Our processing ground is our legitimate interest to improve our games. If you do not wish for us to process the information mentioned below, you can send us an e-mail at <a href="mailto:legal@vertigo-games.com">legal@vertigo-games.com</a>.&nbsp;</p>



<p>We use diagnostics and analytics software of Sentry.io to process the following personal data up to 90 days after collecting the personal data:</p>



<ul>
<li>GPU and GPU driver</li>



<li>The VR software development kit that you use</li>



<li>The (VR) platform that you use</li>



<li>The VR headset that you use and its serial code</li>



<li>Username (pseudonymized)</li>



<li>IP-address</li>



<li>Used peripherals</li>



<li>Game version</li>



<li>Game settings</li>



<li>Owned DLC</li>
</ul>



<p>Sentry.io may store this personal data on its servers in the United States. Sentry.io is bound to the standard contractual clauses as approved by the European Commission. The privacy policy of Sentry.io can be found here: <a href="https://sentry.io/privacy/">https://sentry.io/privacy/</a>.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Other analytical data that we process for up to 2 years after collecting it:</p>



<ul>
<li>User-ID or platform user-ID</li>



<li>Time, date, duration and general location of using our game</li>



<li>Time and date of connections with the matchmaking servers</li>



<li>In-game statistics (such as checkpoints reached, time spent per part)</li>



<li>Device information (such as CPU, GPU, memory size, screen resolution, OS, language, VR device)</li>
</ul>



<p>D. If you contact us as a player, we process the personal data mentioned below up to 5 years after our last contact moment. Our processing ground is our legitimate interest to be able to reply on your requests.</p>



<ul>
<li>Name</li>



<li>E-mail address</li>



<li>Communication and content of e-mails</li>
</ul>



<p>We may send our e-mail via Mailchimp. Mailchimp may store this personal data in the United States. Mailchimp is bound to the standard contractual clauses as approved by the European Commission. The privacy policy of Mailchimp can be found here: <a href="https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/">https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/</a>.</p>
