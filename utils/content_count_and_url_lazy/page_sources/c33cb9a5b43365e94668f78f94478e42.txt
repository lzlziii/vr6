<h2 class="container">Karnage Chronicles</h2>
</div>
<div class="boldgrid-section">
<div class="container">
<p class="" style="padding: 0em;"><strong><span style="color: #999999;">Built for VR from the ground up.</span></strong></p>
<p class=""><i>Every five thousand years the planets align and create a rift in the fabric of cosmos. It’s prior to such an event that this story unfolds. Play as a Murkwraith, an undying knight barred from Death’s domain, and cursed to eternal servitude for sins committed eons ago. Experience a rich story that takes you on a perilous journey through dark dungeons filled with monsters, traps, puzzles, treasure and more.</i></p>
<ul class="bb_ul">
<li>Play alone or together with friends. Karnage Chronicles supports 2-4 players in the same party, with auto-scaling of monster strength and number of spawns as people come and go. Full drop-in / drop-out support allows you to play and have fun while waiting for friends or randoms to join your game, even allowing you to change party size on the fly.</li>
<li>Karnage Chronicles is designed from ground up to fully immerse you in the magic of VR.</li>
<li>Choose between&nbsp;<u>several types of locomotion</u>&nbsp;by accessing settings in-game.</li>
<li>Take full advantage of an inventory system made specifically for VR.</li>
<li>Unparalleled anti-wiggle system for close quarter combat. In this game you cannot tickle your enemies to death as the velocity and distance traveled of your swing defines the damage you do to your enemies.</li>
<li>Customizable weapon presets allow for quick switching between weapons in combat, and there are quick slots for easy access to consumables like potions and food.</li>
<li>Parry incoming attacks with your weapon or block them with a shield. Take full advantage of room-scale VR to dodge and sidestep arrows or magic hurled at you from afar.</li>
<li>Random loot and hidden treasure awaits. Collect coins, find trash items to sell or random gear to wear.</li>
<li>Drop items for safekeeping in your Private Stash and share these between all of your characters. Visit shops where you can spend your hard-earned coins to upgrade your gear. Your private stash is even available to you after joining a hosted multiplayer game.</li>
<li>Solve quests and puzzles for a chance to gain magical rewards in return.</li>
<li>Unlock achievements.</li>
<li>Play as an archer or a warrior.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="container"><i>Go forth and claim your place among the heroes of legend, Murkwraith.</i></div>
