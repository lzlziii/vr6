COLLECTION OF INFORMATION

	When playing the arcade mode, upon completing the session player will be given an option to submit his score to a public leaderboard. It is entirely up to the user if he wishes to submit his data. Upon submission we will collect users chosen name, session data (dishes served, waves completed) and the name of the country that session was played from.
	
USE OF INFORMATION

	The data we collect is used exclusively for the purpose of displaying it on a public leaderboard. The game does not collect any other personal data.
	We intend to wipe our leaderboard data every time we release a major patch. Otherwise we intend to keep the data indefinitely.
