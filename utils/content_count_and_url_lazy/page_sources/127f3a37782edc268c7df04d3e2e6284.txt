class="max-w-2xl mx-auto"><h1>Privacy Policy</h1><article><div class="markdown-styles_markdown__h_8de"><p>RealFit aims to provide an inclusive service that promotes exercise in support
of a healthy body and mind. Our bodies and exercise habits are personal and we
place great importance in taking care of any data you share with us.</p>
<p>This privacy notice is designed to be readable so we can be as transparent as
possible about how we look after your data.</p>
<p>We always welcome questions and feedback about our privacy policy at
<a href="mailto:hello@realfit.co">hello@realfit.co</a></p>
<h1>The type of personal information we collect</h1>
<p>We currently collect and process the following information:</p>
<ul>
<li>Account details including a username and email address</li>
<li>Workout information including the time of workout, a score, and exercise metrics such as repetition counts.
We will inform you of updates to this as RealFit supports more features.</li>
</ul>
<h1>How we get the personal information and why we have it</h1>
<p>Personal information is provided to us directly by you when you create a RealFit
user profile. Creating a user profile enables:</p>
<ul>
<li>Cross-platform and cross-device access to your workout information</li>
<li>Sharing the RealFit application with multiple users while keeping personal information separated
If you use a RealFit user profile then workout information is recorded at the
end of each workout. This information lets you see your own workout history as
an overview and breakdown.</li>
</ul>
<h1>How we use your information</h1>
<p>We use the information that you have given us in order to uniquely identify you
for saving and retrieving your personal workout information.</p>
<p>We use Amazon AWS services for authenticating access to the RealFit service and
for storing our database of application information. Through our contract with
Amazon they are bound to adhere to EU model clauses meaning "personal data on
AWS will be given the same high level of protection it receives in the EEA" (
Please see here for more details:
<a href="https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/security/aws-gdpr-data-processing-addendum/">https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/security/aws-gdpr-data-processing-addendum/</a>)</p>
<h1>Legal Bases</h1>
<p>Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the lawful bases we rely on
for processing this information are:</p>
<ul>
<li>When the processing is necessary to perform a contract with you, like the Terms of Service; and</li>
<li>RealFit's legitimate interest, such as in improving, personalising, and developing the services</li>
</ul>
<h1>How we store your personal information</h1>
<p>Your information is securely stored using Amazon AWS services, currently in
Ireland.</p>
<p>We keep profile information until you delete your account, which you can do at
any time through the application.</p>
<p>You can also choose to delete specific workout information (for example custom
workouts) through the application at any time.</p>
<p>When deleting your account there may be a delay of up to 7 days before the
information is fully deleted from the AWS servers due to our database backup
policy.</p>
<h1>Your data protection rights</h1>
<p>Under EU and UK data protection law, you have rights including:</p>
<p>Your right of access - You have the right to ask us for copies of your personal
information.</p>
<p>Your right to rectification - You have the right to ask us to rectify personal
information you think is inaccurate. You also have the right to ask us to
complete information you think is incomplete.</p>
<p>Your right to erasure - You have the right to ask us to erase your personal
information in certain circumstances.</p>
<p>Your right to restriction of processing - You have the right to ask us to
restrict the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.</p>
<p>Your right to object to processing - You have the the right to object to the
processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.</p>
<p>Your right to data portability - You have the right to ask that we transfer the
personal information you gave us to another organisation, or to you, in certain
circumstances.</p>
<p>You are not required to pay any charge for exercising your rights. If you make a
request, we have one month to respond to you.</p>
<p>Please contact us at <a href="mailto:office@realfit.co">office@realfit.co</a> if you wish to make a request.</p>
<h1>How to complain</h1>
<p>If you have any concerns about our use of your personal information, you can
make a complaint to us at <a href="mailto:office@realfit.co">office@realfit.co</a>.</p>
<p>You can also complain to the ICO if you are unhappy with how we have used your
data.</p>
<p>ICO website: <a href="https://www.ico.org.uk">https://www.ico.org.uk</a></p>
