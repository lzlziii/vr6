<p><img class=" size-full wp-image-69 aligncenter" src="https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightCafe_logo02.png" alt="The Night Cafe" width="390" height="250"></p>
<p><em><img class=" wp-image-66 alignleft" src="https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightcafe041-372x450.jpg" alt="Sunflowers" width="217" height="263" srcset="https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightcafe041-372x450.jpg 372w, https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightcafe041-496x600.jpg 496w, https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightcafe041.jpg 1115w" sizes="(max-width: 217px) 100vw, 217px">The Night Cafe</em> is an immersive virtual reality environment that allows you to explore the world of Vincent van Gogh first hand. Take a moment to enjoy his iconic sunflowers in 3 dimensions or walk around the chair he painted in his bedroom to see it from another angle. Step into the vivid colors straight from his palette.</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-67 alignright" src="https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightcafe081-372x450.jpg" alt="Portrait of Madame Ginoux" width="186" height="225" srcset="https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightcafe081-372x450.jpg 372w, https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightcafe081-496x600.jpg 496w, https://www.borrowedlightvr.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/nightcafe081.jpg 1115w" sizes="(max-width: 186px) 100vw, 186px">While creating the environments of these paintings in 3D space We’ve had to expand on areas that can’t be seen; rooms behind doors, objects hidden from view, people turned away from the viewer. It’s been an interesting process in using reference material from Van Gogh and other impressionist&nbsp;painters but also imagining what might have been there, just off the edges of the canvas.</p>
<p>The experience won the Platinum and Community Choice awards at the Oculus Mobile VR Jam 2015. The Night Cafe: An Immersive Tribute to Vincent van Gogh is available now on&nbsp;Steam for HTC Vive and Oculus Rift.</p>
