              <h2 class="text-primary">Barnstormers: Determined to Win</h2>
            </div>
            <p>From the award winning creator of the VR experience <em><a href="http://iamamanvr.logicgrip.com/">“I Am A Man” </a></em>comes the next big history themed VR project, <em>“<strong>"Barnstormers: Determined to Win.</strong>”</em> This forthcoming interactive VR experience is set to the backdrop of the Negro Baseball League. The immersive experience is a historical fiction piece allowing one to experience people and places from the perspective of a Negro League baseball player.</p>
            <p> Through partnership with the <a href="https://nlbm.com">Negro League Baseball Museum</a>, funding from the <a href="https://www.unrealengine.com/en-US/megagrants">Epic Games Mega Grant</a> and partnership with <a href="https://www.reallusion.com/iclone/live-link/unreal-engine/pitch-and-produce.html">Reallusion</a> (the creators of <em>iClone</em> and <em>Character Creator</em>) we are bringing Negro League Baseball to life as they celebrate their 100th year anniversary.This project will be produced under the umbrella of the "Our Civil Rights" VR series. </p>
            <p>In this VR experience users will face certain struggles in playing the game of baseball during the time of racial segregation. This project is sure to be a hit for people of all ages as you face off with baseball legends such as Satchel Paige, Josh Gibson, and Buck O’Neil. </p>
