<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: small;">Jump in to the world of Harvest Simulator VR!<br>
</span><span style="font-size: small;">
       Harvest Simulator VR is a casual farming game built exclusively 
for VR Headsets.</span><br>
<span style="font-size: small;">It was inspired by our love of playing in the dirt and 
mucking out, as well as the sense of accomplishment that farming gives.</span><br>
<span style="font-size: small;">It was built directly for room-scale VR and allows you to dig up and 
plow your fields, plant your seeds, tend to them with care and watch 
them grow.</span><br>
<span style="font-size: small;">Sell your crop to grow your farm.</span><br>
<span style="font-size: small;">Tend to your chickens and 
collect their eggs too!</span><br>
<span style="font-size: small;">Experience farming life like never before.</span><br>
<span style="font-size: small;">Enter
 the world of the first harvest simulator for virtual reality.<br><br><b>Key features</b><br>Work in your own virtual reality farm, where you can use your farming tools and your skills to create the best farm possible. <br>Plant new crops and see them grow through full day and night cycles.<br>Prepare
 your land and grow produce, selling them on the market and earning 
money to expand your farm with new tools and other essentials.<br>You can buy new seeds and livestock and you can customize your farm and your home with additional furniture.<br>Use your own hands to cultivate your plot and turn your farm into the farm of your dreams.<br>The more your farm grows the more you can do: there is a lot more in this farm than you can even imagine.      </span><br>
<br>
