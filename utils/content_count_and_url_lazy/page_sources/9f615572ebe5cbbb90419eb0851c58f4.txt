		<p><img class="alignnone wp-image-465 size-full" src="https://kucki.eu/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ThiefN2560.png" alt="" width="2560" height="1440" srcset="https://kucki.eu/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ThiefN2560.png 2560w, https://kucki.eu/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ThiefN2560-300x169.png 300w, https://kucki.eu/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ThiefN2560-768x432.png 768w, https://kucki.eu/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ThiefN2560-1024x576.png 1024w, https://kucki.eu/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ThiefN2560-1x1.png 1w" sizes="(max-width: 2560px) 100vw, 2560px"></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Ever wanted to know how the life of a thief is? It’s like a Sale, but everything is for free! Jewelry, game consoles, VR-headsets, and even dogs! (Disclaimer: You can’t steal dogs inside the Game)<br>
While others are at work, you are in their house, doing some renovations and repairing their expensive electronic devices or cars. You may even need to take them with you – for store repair.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>There is a Thief in my House</strong><span style="font-family: sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: Sans serif, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;">is a fun level based stealth action VR game for Oculus Rift. Developed from the ground up and with passion for VR.<br>
</span></span></span>You are new to the company, which is an organization for thieves and you need to take the Thief training simulation which will take place inside VR.<br>
Your training takes you through different situations: You need to break into houses, rob banks and steal expensive cars.</p>
