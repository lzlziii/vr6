Finally we have the Skateboard, which is best used to travel in large open spaces. having multiple forms of movement allows you to explore the world the way you want to explore.</li>
					<li>Levels: RuneCaster contains many different ways to play across five diverse levels. Each of these levels is very unique both in theming and gameplay and will challenge you to use your full array of spells to succeed. The extreme difference in theme/style from level to level is meant to reinforce the idea that you are traveling to completely different worlds and allows the player to experience a variety of enviroments.</li>
