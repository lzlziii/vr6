																			<h1>为何要采用抢先体验这种模式？</h1>
										“There’s something here - we’ve seen it with our own eyes, but we need your help to find the full potential!<br>
<br>
We know that most VR users on Steam are pretty savvy by now and likely have some favorite genres already, but VR is still new and we haven’t discovered all use cases yet. Is a social game really what VR is for? Well, we think that’s one of the coolest use cases. For every intense shooter out there, there’s likely also room for a more casual experience. Especially for those occasions when you want to introduce new people to the magic of VR.<br>
<br>
We want to let people chime in and share their experiences with Puppet Fever as we move forward. We see new ways people interact with the game watch time a new player tries it and learn the language. There is much left to discover and improve upon the basic formula. We also want to see if the concept, novel as it is, can stand on its own legs in the VR marketplace. <br>
In short, we want the community help us to make the game better and try and see if people respond well to the concept!”

										<h1>这款游戏的抢先体验状态大约持续多久？</h1>
										“Until autumn 2018.”

										“More content! We have plenty of ideas for new content such as new game modes, puppets and theatrical special effects, but before we get ahead of ourselves, we want to run the vanilla experience by you to see what you’d like to see more of, based on your experience of having friends over to play.<br>
<br>
A truly interactive livestreaming experience is also on our roadmap where a streamer can play the game with the audience in real time. Right now we have some of the basics in place, because even the streaming platforms are early in development in terms of VR support.<br>
<br>
We would also like to include support for more languages.”
