<p>Red Matter 2 is an award-winning immersive puzzle-adventure game that takes you on a heart-pumping journey to the far reaches of space in a dystopian cold war setting. Step into the shoes of Sasha, an agent awakened in a lunar base by an undercover operative and tasked with uncovering the secrets of the mysterious Red Matter.</p>
<p>As you explore visually stunning Volgravian bases and experience a spine-chilling atmosphere, you’ll be challenged with mind-bending puzzles, fast-paced combat, and platforming mechanics that will keep you on the edge of your seat. With the help of your hacking tool and jetpack, you’ll have the freedom to soar through the skies and move without limits as you navigate your way through the world.</p>
<p>Uncover the secrets of this fully-realized world as you solve environmental, physics, and logic puzzles and hack into terminals to gain access to vital information. But be prepared for the unexpected, as Volgravia has taken great measures to protect the secrets you seek.</p>
<p>Explore a fully interactive world designed to enhance the feeling of immersion in virtual reality. Red Matter 2 is the ultimate VR adventure, and a must-play for anyone looking for a thrilling, impactful, and unforgettable experience.</p>
