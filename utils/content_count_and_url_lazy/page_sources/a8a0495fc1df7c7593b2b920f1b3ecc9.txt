Increased pressure on his shoulders made him behave like a lunatic and lastly, he has been kicked out of the national team. His career has taken a serious damage. 					  <h3>Description</h3>
		Header Goal VR is a physics based, action pumped, virtual reality sports game with a simulation like interaction dynamics. Through its story based levels you find yourself in the place of the character Axel Rix who is a young soccer player. Your main goal is to hit thrown balls by heading while targeting various objects in a soccer training field. Each stage has a unique level design. To achieve a better score, you should have a plan. Even hitting the targets in the right order makes a great difference. You will start feel like Axel within the immersive atmosphere of the game.
		<br>
		Being able to hit a virtual ball in the most natural and realistic way makes Header Goal VR the most entertaining way of heading a ball.
