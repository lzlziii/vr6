   <p style="text-align: left;"><strong>Furnie</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">This application collects some Personal Data from its Users.&nbsp;&nbsp;Personal Data collected for the following purposes and using the following services:</p>
<ul>
<li style="text-align: left;">Room scans used for 3D visualization: Personal Data: room scans are stored locally on user device for viewing at a later time</li>
<li style="text-align: left;">Microphone used for voice commands : Personal Data: voices and sounds are not stored or transmitted</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>Paint Arena</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">This application collects some Personal Data from its Users.&nbsp;&nbsp;Personal Data collected for the following purposes and using the following services:</p>
<ul style="text-align: left;">
<li>Live Broadcasting may occur during usage of the Paint Arena : Personal Data: drawings, voices, sounds, body language, and gestures streaming over third party server</li>
<li>Login form for authentication: Personal Data: email address, username, and password</li>
<li>Debugging, moderating, and/or marketing:&nbsp; Personal Data: Videos and screenshots of users’ drawings, avatar animations, name tag,&nbsp;voices and sounds</li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: left;">Parental supervision is encouraged for the online activities of their children to ensure no information is collected from a child without parental permission.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Third party audio broadcasting of copyright content is also discouraged from users who use the Paint Arena app</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">The publisher may use videos and screenshots&nbsp;of any user’s drawings, avatar animations, name tags,&nbsp;voices and sounds for marketing purposes without notifying the user, unless the videos or screenshots contained sufficient personal information for identifying and contacting the user, in which case, the publisher will contact the user for consent before releasing such media to the public.</p>
<hr>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>Room Scanner</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">This application collects some Personal Data from its Users.&nbsp;&nbsp;Personal Data collected for the following purposes and using the following services:</p>
<ul style="text-align: left;">
<li>Room scans with 3D geometric details and photo textures: Personal Data: room scans are stored locally on user device, and can be uploaded to a third party server for downloading by the same user and for sharing with other users, where access is restricted using a room-specific password.</li>
<li>Login form for authentication, ownership of room scans, and avatar appearance: Personal Data: email address, username, password and avatar gender.</li>
<li>Live Broadcasting may occur during usage of the Room Scanner : Personal Data: body language, gestures, drawings, voices and sounds streaming over third party server</li>
<li>Debugging, moderating and/or marketing:&nbsp;&nbsp;Personal Data: Videos and screenshots of users’ drawings, avatar animations, name tag, voices and sounds</li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: left;">Parental supervision is encouraged for the online activities of their children to ensure no information is collected from a child without parental permission.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Third party audio broadcasting of copyright content is also discouraged from users who use the Room Scanner app.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">The publisher may use videos and screenshots&nbsp;of any user’s room scans, avatar animations, name tags, drawings, voices and sounds for marketing purposes without notifying the user, unless the videos or screenshots contained sufficient personal information for identifying and contacting the user, in which case, the publisher will contact the user for consent before releasing such media to the public.</p>
