<h2 class="wp-block-heading">ABOUT THIS GAME</h2>



<p>An endless legion of alien monstrosities, the XenoHorde threatens mankind’s galactic empire. You are the last surviving member of the Hive Slayers, the galaxy’s best and last defense against alien threats. Equipped with your matrix gauntlet and plasma blaster your mission, designated B.A.B., is as simple as it is deadly; to survive the endless XenoHorde and bring mankind back from the brink you must Blast All Bugs.</p>



<p><strong>THREE GAME MODES</strong></p>



<ul>
<li>Standard Mode: clear waves to defeat bosses and minibosses</li>



<li>Arcade Mode: defeat columns of enemies before they get too close!</li>



<li>Endless Mode: see how long you can last against the endless horde</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>POWER-UPS</strong></p>



<ul>
<li>Rapid-Fire: overwhelm the XenoHorde with enhanced fire-rate</li>



<li>Shield Recharge: fully charge your matrix shield in a pinch</li>



<li>Bomb: destroy swarms in a fiery blast</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>MULTIPLE ENEMY TYPES</strong></p>



<ul>
<li>Standard: simple but deadly</li>



<li>Rapid-Fire: buffed with more bullets per bug</li>



<li>Armored: tougher to kill but satisfying to blast</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>THREE RATES OF FIRE</strong></p>



<ul>
<li>Punchy Rapid-Fire</li>



<li>Hair Trigger Sniper</li>



<li>Bold Shotgun</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>SLAY WITH STYLE</strong></p>



<ul>
<li>Streak Multiplier: embrace your inner sharpshooter and stay on target to increase your score</li>



<li>Reflect Projectiles: the best defense bites back, reflect projectiles into enemy lines with the matrix gauntlet</li>
</ul>



<p><strong>LEADERBOARDS</strong></p>



<ul>
<li>Local and Online to flex on friends</li>



<li>Leaderboard for every game mode</li>
</ul>

<li>Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system</li>



<li>OS: Win 7 or higher</li>



<li>Processor: Intel Core i5-4590 / AMD FX 8350</li>



<li>Memory: 8 GB RAM</li>



<li>Graphics: GTX 970</li>
