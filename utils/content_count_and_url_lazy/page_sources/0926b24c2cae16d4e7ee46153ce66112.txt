<ol>
<li>When purchasing or using Games through game devices, PCs and mobile devices such as cell phones with network functionality<br>IDs, game records, game results, and billing records provided by game platforms, OS information for game devices and terminals, model names, identifiers, location information that can be obtained from mobile devices, advertisement viewing information, etc.</li>

<li>When answering AMATA questionnaires<br>Customer name, residential address, email address, date of birth, age, gender, nickname, and other information entered into the questionnaire.</li>
<li>When responding to inquiries related to Games<br>Customer name, email address, inquiry content, the response provided, and other information required to deal with the inquiry.</li>
<li>AMATA may also acquire Personal Information posted to social media platforms such as Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and other platforms hosted by third parties, in cases where the customer has provided consent to said third party to provide their information to others.</li>
</ol>
