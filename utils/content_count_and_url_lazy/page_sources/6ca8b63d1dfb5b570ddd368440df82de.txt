<p data-adtags-visited="true">Planetary Space Simulator 3D is a simple, but very addictive physics simulation of a gravity systems in space.</p>
<p data-adtags-visited="true">There is no goal, other than to have fun by playing around with suns, planets and moons, observing the gravity forces between them and building up solar systems and even entire galaxies.</p>
<p data-adtags-visited="true">What would you like to have more? 3D? Also available!!</p>
