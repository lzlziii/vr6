<h1>Place Privacy Policy</h1>

<p class="MsoNormal">Mental Health Online, hereafter: “MHO”, ‘we’, ‘us’, or ‘our’
is a service run by Swinburne University of Technology, providing free digital
mental health services to the Australian community. MHO has developed a virtual
reality app called Place, hereafter: “PLACE app”, “app”. </p>

<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Definitions</h2>

<p class="MsoNormal">Mental Health Online&nbsp;(MHO):&nbsp;digital
mental health service run by&nbsp;Swinburne University of Technology that has
developed the PLACE app.&nbsp;</p>

<p class="MsoNormal">Users: members of the public who download and use the PLACE app. </p>

<p class="MsoNormal">&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Commitment to privacy</h2>

<p class="MsoNormal">Mental Health Online (MHO) is committed to protecting the
privacy of personal information which the service collects, holds and
administers. Personal information is information which directly or indirectly
identifies a person. MHO respects the privacy of online users and we recognise
the importance of providing a protected and secure online environment for
users. </p>

<p class="MsoNormal">The purpose of this policy is to provide a framework for
Mental Health Online, providers of the PLACE app, in managing privacy
considerations. This online privacy policy outlines how MHO collects, stores
and processes information for users of the PLACE app. This online privacy
policy specifically relates to the PLACE app developed by MHO. This policy only
safeguards the privacy of online users of the PLACE app and does not
automatically apply to data collected offline or by MHO in general. The privacy
values of MHO are reflected in <a href="https://www.mentalhealthonline.org.au/pages/privacy">Mental Health
Online’s Privacy Policy</a>, which is compliant with the Privacy Act 1988
(Cth). Further details regarding the <a href="https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/C2020C00025">Australian Privacy
Act 1988</a></p>
