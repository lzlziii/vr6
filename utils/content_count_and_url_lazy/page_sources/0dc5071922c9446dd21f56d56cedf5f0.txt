<li>Right to erasure: you can request for your personal data to be erased from our records.</li>
<li>Right to restrict processing – meaning&nbsp;where certain conditions apply, you have the right to restrict the Processing of your Personal Data.</li>
<li>Right to object to processing&nbsp;– meaning in certain cases you have the right to object to Processing of your Personal Data, for example in the case of direct marketing.</li>
<li>Right to object to automated Processing&nbsp;– meaning you have the right to object to automated Processing, including profiling; and not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated Processing.
