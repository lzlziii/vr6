<p>Your privacy is important to us. Instant 3D Builder&nbsp;uses the personal data you provide to us to notify you of important information about our products, upgrades and enhancements, and to send you information about other Instant 3D Builder products and services.</p>
<p>We share your personal data with your consent or as necessary to complete any transaction or provide any service you have requested. We also share data with Instant 3D Builder-controlled affiliates and subsidiaries; with vendors working on our behalf; when required by law or to respond to legal process; to protect our customers; to protect lives; to maintain the security of our services; and to protect the rights of property or Instant 3D Builder.</p>
<p>Instant 3D Builder is committed to protecting the security of your personal data. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Your personal data is never shared outside the company without your permission, except under conditions explained above.</p>
<p>If you believe that Instant 3D Builderhas not adhered to this statement, please contact us at <a href="mailto:Instant3DBuilder+Admin@gmail.com">Instant3DBuilder@gmail.com</a>. We will respond to questions within 30 days.</p>
