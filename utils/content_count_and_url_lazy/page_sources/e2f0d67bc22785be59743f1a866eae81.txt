By using the Platform, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Policy. 

1. Information that we collect on the platform: 

(a) User-Provided Information: When you use the Platform, we may collect information about you or you may provide information about you, and we may link that information to other information about you. You may provide information to us on the Platform in a variety of ways. For example, you provide us with information when you register for an account (we collect your email address and date of birth and allow you to create a username and password), subscribe to our newsletters, use the Platform, make a purchase on the Platform, or send us customer service inquiries. 

(b) Cookies and automatically collected information: When you use the Platform, we may send one or more cookies - small text files containing a series of alphanumeric characters - to your device. We may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and can be used by your browser on subsequent visits to the Platform. Please refer to your web browser's help file to learn how to change your cookie settings. Please note that if you delete or do not accept cookies from the Platform, you may not be able to use the full features of the Platform. We may also use third party cookies on our Platform. For example, we use Google Analytics to collect and process certain analytics data. To learn more about how Google Analytics uses your data and what your options are, please visit www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/. Nostraverse does not process or respond to "do not track" signals from web browsers or other similar transmissions indicating a request to disable online tracking from users using our Platform. We may also automatically record certain information from your device using various types of technologies, including "clear gifs" or "web beacons". This automatically collected information may include your IP address or other device address or ID, the web browser and/or device type, the web pages or websites you visit immediately before or after using the Platform, the pages or other content you view or otherwise interact with on the Platform, and the dates and times you visit, access or use the Platform. We may also use these technologies to collect information about your interaction with email messages, such as whether you opened, clicked on, or forwarded a message. This information is collected from all users and may be linked to other information about you. 

(c) Information Disclosed While Using the Platform: While using the Platform, including participating in various "rooms" made available to users of the virtual reality component of our Platform, you may disclose information about you to us and to other users of the Platform. This may be the case when you engage in conversations, chats, or other user-to-user interactions. We may collect and use this information to the extent permitted by law. 

(d) Payment Information: If you choose to access or use portions of the Platform that require payments or fees, we may require you to provide us with financial information to process those payments. We may share your payment information with third-party payment processors who process transactions on our behalf. We may also collect and retain information relating to the transactions you have made. 

(e) Location Information: We may use your IP address to obtain general information about the physical location from which you connect to the Internet. 

(f) Third Party Web Beacons and Buttons: We may display Third Party Content on the Platform. The Third Party Content may use cookies, web beacons or other mechanisms to collect data in connection with your viewing of the Third Party Content on the Platform. In addition, we may implement third-party buttons (e.g., Facebook "like" or "share" buttons) that may act as web beacons even if you do not interact with the button. Information collected by third-party web beacons and buttons is collected directly by those third parties, not by Nostraverse. Information collected by third parties in this manner is subject to their own data collection, use and disclosure policies. Please note that the privacy choices you make on Nostraverse  are not transferred to the third party websites or platforms (and vice versa). 

(g) Information from other sources: We may receive information from third parties and sources other than the Platform, such as analytics partners and advertisers.

We use the information we collect on the Platform (in both individual and aggregated format) in a variety of ways to provide the Platform and operate our business, including the following: 

(a) We use the information we collect on the Platform to operate, maintain, improve and provide the Platform, to provide services and information requested by you, to respond to comments and questions and to otherwise provide support to users, and to process and deliver entries and rewards in connection with promotions offered from time to time on the Platform. 

(b) We use the information we collect on the Platform to understand and analyze our users' usage trends and preferences, to improve the Platform, and to develop new products, services, features and functionality. 

(c) We may use your email address or other information we collect to contact you for administrative purposes, such as customer service or to send you communications, including required notices, marketing communications or updates on promotions and events relating to products and services offered by us and by third parties. 

(d) We may use information to: (i) personalize our Platform, such as to store information about you so that you do not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit the Platform; (ii) provide customized content and information; (iii) monitor and analyze the effectiveness of the Platform; (iv) monitor overall Site usage data, such as. the total number of visitors and pages viewed; (v) track your entries, submissions, and status in promotions or other activities on the Platform;(vi) detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, intellectual property infringement, violations of our Terms of Use, violations of law, or other misuse of the Platform; (vii) or as required by applicable laws and regulations or requested by a court or governmental authority. 

(e) We may also use information for other purposes, which we will expressly state at the time the information is collected.

Except as described in this Policy, we do not share information about you that we collect on the Platform with third parties without your consent. We may share information with third parties if you give us your consent to do so and in the following circumstances: 

(a) We work with third party providers who provide website, application development, hosting, payment, maintenance and other services to us. These third parties may have access to or process your data in the course of providing these services to us. 

(b) We may disclose information about you if required to do so by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws, in response to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena or search warrant, or to otherwise cooperate with law enforcement or other governmental authorities.

(c) We also reserve the right to disclose information about you that we believe in good faith is reasonable or necessary to (i) take precautions against liability, (ii) protect ourselves or others from fraudulent, abusive or unlawful uses or activities, (iii) investigate and defend against claims or allegations by third parties, (iv) protect the security or integrity of the Platform and any facilities or equipment used to provide the Platform; or (v) protect our property or other legal rights (including, without limitation, the enforcement of our agreements) or the rights, property or safety of others. 

(d) Information about our users may be disclosed and otherwise transferred to an acquirer, successor or assign in connection with a merger, acquisition, debt financing, sale of assets or similar transaction, or in the event of an insolvency, bankruptcy or receivership in which information is transferred to one or more third parties as part of our business assets.
