<div class="post-title-wrapper"><h1 class="post-title">Privacy Policy</h1></div><p>Effective Date: 4/20/2019</p>
<p>This Privacy Policy applies to the Metanaut Labs Inc. (“<strong>Metanaut</strong>” or “<strong>we</strong>”) software and services on which it is placed, including but not limited to metanautvr.com, gadgeteergame.com, and the <em>Gadgeteer</em> video game (collectively, the “<strong>Service</strong>”) and details data we collect, use and disclose about our users, including you.</p>
<p><strong>By clicking “I agree” or similar or by using the Service you are agreeing to be bound by the Privacy Policy.</strong></p>
<ol start="1">
<li>
<h5>How we obtain your data</h5>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The Service collects and processes personal data, which is information about you or that identifies you, as well as anonymous data, which does not identify you.&nbsp; You may choose not to provide Metanaut with certain data or request the deletion of certain data, which may impact Service features available to you or operation of the Service.</p>
<ol start="2">
<li>
<h5>Personal data we collect</h5>
</li>
</ol>
<p>We collect and process the following personal data:</p>
<ul>
<li>unique identifiers that identify you including your Gamer ID (and associated data and statistics), Internet Protocol address, MAC address and other unique device identifiers;</li>
<li>if you sign up for an email list, your email address;</li>
<li>if you use the voice features provided through the Service we may collect the details of your voice communications;</li>
<li>if you interact with us on social media platforms, such as Facebook and Youtube, we collect personal data provided to us by such platforms; and</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>if you contact us for customer support or for any other reason, we collect any personal data you choose to provide, such as your first and last name and email address.</li>
</ul>
<p>We collect anonymous data from you, which does not identify you unless associated with personal data, including but not limited to responses to anonymous surveys, metrics and other types of statistical data, which could be derived from use of the Service, and diagnostic data concerning the Service.&nbsp; For example, when playing <em>Gadgeteer</em>, we collect anonymous information on how many times you have used a gadget and how many tools you use.</p>
<ol start="3">
<li>
<h5>Processing of Your Personal and ANONYMOUS data</h5>
</li>
</ol>
<p>We process your personal and anonymous data primarily for our business purposes, such as providing the Service, to understand your needs and the needs of our users and to provide you with better service and information, particularly:</p>
<ul>
<li>to send Service updates, patches and related push notifications that may be of importance to you and if you do not wish to receive these types of updates, patches and notifications your use of the Service may be limited;</li>
<li>providing the Service to you, such as displaying and delivering content through the Service, authenticating users, facilitating voice communications with other users during multiplayer mode or performing services requested of us;</li>
<li>maintaining and analyzing Service functionality, such as backing up data, understanding how users use the Service and drawing trends and predictions based on this data;</li>
<li>improving the Service and future games we make</li>
<li>protecting the security and integrity of the Service and its users including but not limited to detecting and protecting Metanaut and other third parties against error, fraud, theft and other illegal activity;</li>
<li>legal or regulatory compliance;</li>
<li>providing customer and technical support and to resolve customer issues;</li>
<li>provide you with information or inform you of changes related to the Service such as confirmations, technical notices, updates, suspensions, outages or other material changes; and</li>
<li>contact you with surveys, marketing or promotional content or contests, if you previously consented to such contact or we have a basis for contacting you for such purpose under applicable law.</li>
</ul>
<p>We disclose your personal and anonymous data to our parent companies, affiliates, subsidiaries, employees and contractors for the same purposes described above.&nbsp; We also disclose your personal and anonymous data to third parties, who may be located in a foreign jurisdiction and subject to foreign laws, as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li>to process your data as described above and provide or enhance Service features;</li>
<li>for customer support and feedback, analyzing Service functionality and technical issues, tracking use of the Service and generating reports and data models that we use to improve the service;</li>
<li>to link or combine your information with other information from third parties to help understand your preferences and provide you with better services;</li>
<li>for payment processing and fraud prevention purposes; and</li>
<li>to law enforcement or a government that has asserted its lawful authority to obtain the data or where Metanaut has reasonable grounds to believe the data could be useful in the investigation of unlawful activity, or to comply with a subpoena or warrant or an order made by a court, person or body with jurisdiction to compel the production of data, or to comply with court rules regarding the production of records and data, or to Metanaut’s legal counsel.</li>
</ul>
<ol start="4">
<li>
<h5><strong>THIRD PARTY SERVICES</strong></h5>
</li>
</ol>
<p>The Privacy Policy only applies to personal data that Metanaut collects, processes and discloses and does not apply to the collection, processing and disclosure of data by third parties through third party services, which may be broader than set forth in this Privacy Policy, and that may be embedded into the Service. For example, we may share aggregate or anonymous information about you with hardware manufacturers, platform hosts, publishers, business partners, and other third parties, to the extent permitted by applicable law.&nbsp;&nbsp; Please contact us using the information below if you would like to learn more about third party data practices.</p>

</li>
