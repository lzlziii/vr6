		<h1 class="entry-title">The Unity Cube Privacy Policy</h1>	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	
	<div class="entry-content">
		<div class="mailmunch-forms-before-post" style="display: none !important;"></div>
<p>Last updated on: 2022.06.21</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-who-we-are">Who we are</h2>



<p>We are New Technology Walkers, a group of VR professionals that love to create VR experiences. The team is headed by Massimiliano Ariani and Antony Vitillo (previously at Immotionar) but includes many other freelancers and small companies (like MID). We create games and applications like the one that you are playing, The Unity Cube, which is aimed to be an absurd VR app.</p>



<p>Privacy is an important pillar of our offering: we engage daily with the VR community and we know how trust is an important value for all VR people, so we have built our game with transparency about data management in mind. This privacy policy details how we handle your data when you play The Unity Cube.</p>



<p>If anything in this document is not clear to you or you think that we are making some errors in the management of your personal data, please feel free to contact us at info@ntwalkers.it.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-what-personal-data-we-collect-and-why-we-collect-it">What personal data we collect and why we collect it</h2>



<p>The Unity Cube is a simple app and doesn’t need any kind of personal data. For this reason, it doesn’t collect any personal data.</p>



<p>The Unity Cube is on the Quest Store. You can find the Quest Store privacy policy at this link: <a href="https://store.facebook.com/it/en/legal/quest/privacy-policy/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">https://store.facebook.com/it/en/legal/quest/privacy-policy/</a></p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-who-we-share-your-data-with">Who we share your data with</h2>



<p>We don’t own your data, and so we are not sharing any of your data with third parties, either for free or as a paid service. We care about your privacy, and we don’t share unnecessary data with other entities.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-how-long-we-retain-your-data">How long we retain your data</h2>



<p>We don’t own your data, so we don’t retain it at all.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-what-rights-you-have-over-your-data">What rights you have over your data</h2>



<p>If you are a player of our applications, you are in full control of your data.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Your rights on your personal data include the following:</p>



<ul><li>Request access to your personal data;</li><li>Request correction or deletion of your personal data;</li><li>Object to our use and processing of your personal data;</li><li>Request that we limit our use and processing of your personal data; and</li><li>Request portability of your personal data.</li></ul>



<p>In case you don’t find an easy way to fulfill automatically your request, we will be happy of helping you with that.</p><div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;"></div>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-where-we-send-your-data">Where we send your data</h2>



<p>No personal data of yours exit your device while you use The Unity Cube.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-our-contact-information"><strong>Our contact information</strong></h2>



<p>In case of any problems with your personal data, you can contact New Technology Walkers’ founders Antony Vitillo or Massimiliano Ariani, and they will help you in solving any issue. You can contact us via e-mail or standard mail.</p>



<p>E-mail address: <a href="mailto:info@ntwalkers.it">info@ntwalkers.it</a></p>



<p>Standard mail address is:</p>



<p>Antony Vitillo (NTW)<br>&nbsp;c/o Associazione Culturale Lombroso 16<br>via Cesare Lombroso 16<br>10125 Torino (TO)<br>Italy</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-additional-information">Additional information</h2>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-how-we-protect-your-data"><strong>How we protect your data</strong></h3>



<p>For all our applications, we try to keep your data as secure as possible by:</p>



<ul><li>Using only high-quality trusted products to handle them;</li><li>Keeping our product always updated with the latest security patches;</li><li>Keeping ourselves informed about any data breaches that are happening in the world.</li></ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-what-data-breach-procedures-we-have-in-place"><strong>What data breach procedures we have in place</strong></h3>



<p>We continuously stay informed about possible data breaches on the services that we use. In case of possible data breaches we get notified about, we will inform all The Unity Cube’s community immediately. In case the breach is about a 3rd party service (e.g. Unity Analytics), we will work together with the providers of the services to understand what has been the impact of the breach, what are the risks for our users, and how we can solve the problem.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-what-third-parties-we-receive-data-from"><strong>What third parties we receive data from</strong></h3>



<p>We don’t receive any kind of data from third parties for this application.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-privacy-policy-changes">Privacy Policy Changes</h2>



<p>We of New Technology Walkers may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. If we make changes, Your continued use of The Unity Cube after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your consent to such change.</p>
