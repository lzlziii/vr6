<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Chesstar PRIVACY POLICY</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Last update: 17/5/2023</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ABOUT THIS PRIVACY
POLICY</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1.1&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Introduction</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">When you visit the Chesstar website (“Chesstar
Website”) or when you make use of the Chesstar products, software,
virtual/augmented reality application or mobile application (“Chesstar
Products”), Chesstar (“Chesstar”, “we”), who is the owner of this Chesstar
Website and the developer of the Chesstar Products, collects, stores, and uses
your personal information. This privacy policy sets out which personal
information we collect from you, for which purposes, how we use your personal
information and what measures we take to protect it.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Chesstar is committed to protecting your
privacy and personal data and would like to be as transparent as possible in
explaining how it processes your personal information. Therefore, we have tried
to keep this policy as simple as possible, and we advise you to take the time
to read it thoroughly. We will not process your personal data beyond what is
set out in this privacy policy.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1.2&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Who does this privacy
policy apply to?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">This privacy policy applies to every user
of the Chesstar Website and the Chesstar Products (“you”, “data subject”). This
privacy policy only applies to our Chesstar and Hyper Universe related products
and services and not to the other products, websites and services offered by Chesstar.
If you would like to receive more information on how Chesstar manages your
personal data when you use our other products, websites and services, please
visit https://chesstar.com/privacy.html</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1.3&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Who is responsible
for personal data processed in accordance with this privacy policy?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Chesstar, a private limited liability
company headquartered, is the controller of the personal data that is collected
and processed when making use of the Chesstar Website and Chesstar Products. If
you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us
by making use of the aforementioned address or send us an e-mail via the
following e-mail address:&nbsp; contact@chesstar.com.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">1.4&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Updates to this
privacy policy</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Chesstar can revise this privacy policy at
any time, for example, in the context of changes to our Chesstar Website or Chesstar
Products, regulatory updates or feedback from our users. The adapted conditions
apply from the moment they are published on this page, so we advise you to
regularly visit this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
If any significant changes are made to this privacy policy, we will notify you
personally when possible, and if the processing of personal data is based on
your consent, request your permission.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; WHICH PERSONAL DATA DO
WE COLLECT?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">When you use the Chesstar Website or the Chesstar
Products, we collect the following personal information from you:</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">•<span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Identification,
contact and location information such as your name, e-mail address, address,
user name, avatar and user ID;</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">•<span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Information
about your professional qualifications and/or academic background (should you
contact us with an application for employment);</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal">•<span lang="EN-US">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Information
about how you interact with our Chesstar Website and Chesstar Products such as
information regarding the length and frequency of use, crash reports, popular
items and features and your achievements; and Information associated with the
device you use when making use of the Chesstar Website or Chesstar Products
such as IP address, brand, type, operating system, hardware information and
screen resolution.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; HOW DO WE COLLECT YOUR
PERSONAL DATA?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3.1&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; You provide us with
your personal data by submitting a contact form, registering, creating an
account, a profile or an avatar</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">When you make use of our Chesstar Website
or Chesstar Products, it is possible that you create an account, profile, or
avatar. You can also contact us by e-mail or through our contact form on the Chesstar
Website. When you choose to do so, we may sometimes ask you to provide certain
personal data, such as your e-mail address, name, date of birth, sex and/or
postal code. After registration, we can also request additional information
that can help us provide more customized services.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3.2&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; We receive your
personal data from third parties</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">If you log into the Chesstar Products using
a social media account or make use of the social networking functions present
in the game, you grant permission for the social networking website to share
certain, clearly identified personal data with us. Usually, this includes the
basic data associated with your profile on the social networking website (name,
e-mail address, date of birth, postal code, and sex). Furthermore, it can also
include certain data related to your behaviour on the social networking
website, such as likes, for example. This can only include personal data that
you have shared publicly on the social networking website, and only to the
extent to which your personal settings on the social networking website allow.
If you do not wish such information to be shared, we advise you to adjust your
settings on the relevant social networking website.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Furthermore, we also receive information
from other reliable sources, including both online and offline data providers. Chesstar
shall only do so insofar as this data was collected by the third party legally
and insofar as it can be shared with us legally. If necessary, Chesstar will
also request your explicit permission to use this data.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Further, in order to analyse the Chesstar
Website and Chesstar Products and improve our products, we make use of
analytics tools and third parties, who help us get a better understanding of
the use of our Chesstar Website and Chesstar Products. These third parties may
collect information, for example by using applications that you have installed,
mobile websites you have visited.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Lastly, when you are making use of our Chesstar
Products via a platform provider such as Oculus, Steam, Viveport…, these
platform providers share certain personal data about you and your activities on
the platform with us. We advise you to review the privacy policies of these
platform providers to get a better insight in which information these platforms
collect and share with their partners.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3.3&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Personal data is
collected automatically when making use of the Chesstar Website and Chesstar
Products</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Regardless of whether you register, create
an account, a profile or an avatar, we collect user information when you make
use of the Chesstar Website and Chesstar Products. This may include, but is not
limited to, information regarding the length and frequency of use, crash
reports, popular items and features, your achievements… We use this information
to improve the performance of our Chesstar Website and Chesstar Products, adapt
it based on your requirements and interests, and better understand how our
products are being used.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">3.4&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Other sources</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Our Chesstar Website and Chesstar Products
are dynamic and innovative operating tools, which means that we are constantly
looking for ways to serve you better and meet your personal needs. This can
also mean that new applications are added that cause us to collect and process
your data differently. We will always communicate about this as transparently
as possible in this privacy policy.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; WHY DO WE COLLECT YOUR
PERSONAL DATA?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">We collect personal data in order to offer
and improve our products and to provide better services to our users. More in
particular, we collect and process personal data for the following purposes:</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4.1&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To make the Chesstar
Website and Chesstar Products available to you</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">We use your personal data for the provision
and maintenance of our services. For example, when making use of our Chesstar
Products, we use the user name you have provided to keep your scores and
identify you amongst other users. We also use your personal data to notify you
when there are changes to or planned maintenance of our Chesstar Website and Chesstar
Products or to process your feedback. Your personal data is likewise processed
to receive and answer any queries you may have about the Chesstar Website and Chesstar
Products.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4.2&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To recognize you as a
user</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">When you use a profile or account to log in
to our services, we apply technology that recognizes you as a registered user
and immediately grants access to the relevant service. This means that you
don’t need to provide your log-in information every time you visit the relevant
Chesstar online service(s) or wish to take part in interactive features of our
services, like contests, for example.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4.3&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To analyse and
improve the Chesstar Website and Chesstar Products</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">We use personal data to understand which Chesstar
Website or Chesstar Products features and items are most popular among our
users. To understand the preferences of our users better, we may also invite
you to take part in a survey. We do this to continually improve our Chesstar
Website and Chesstar Products and develop new products and services, to make
our offer as suitable as possible for our users.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4.4&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To personalize the Chesstar
Website and Chesstar Products to you</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Chesstar strives to make its Chesstar
Website and Chesstar Products as relevant as possible for every user. By using
personal data that indicates what a user is likely interested in, Chesstar can
work towards the provision of more personalized services. That way, Chesstar
could, for example, recommend content that matches your profile.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4.5&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To stay apprised of
new products and services</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Chesstar can use your contact details and
other data collected to inform you of news, upcoming activities or events,
promotions, new services, and so forth. We will only do this if you have
granted permission for us to do so. You can opt out of this type of communication
at any time. To do so, you can use the “unsubscribe” link that you see at the
bottom of all of our electronic communications.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">4.6&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To prevent and detect
abuse or fraud</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">We can also use personal data to prevent,
detect and investigate illegal or suspected illegal activity, such as fraud
(for example, with payment methods), and adherence to our Terms of Use , in the
interests of both Chesstar and our users.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; ON WHAT LEGAL BASIS DO
WE PROCESS YOUR PERSONAL DATA?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5.1&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Necessary for the
conclusion and performance of a contract with you</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Some of the personal data that we collect
(e.g. platform account, username, unique identifier) are necessary to be able
to make use of the Chesstar Products. We process the personal data that fall
within this category in order to perform the agreement we have with you.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5.2&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Based on our
legitimate interests of those of third parties</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Sometimes, we process your personal data
based on our legitimate interests, for example, our legitimate interests in
ensuring the integrity, safety and functionality of our Chesstar Website and Chesstar
Products. The legitimate interests that we pursue include also those of third
parties, such as platform providers, in order for these platform providers to
receive information on the behaviour of users on their platforms. If we process
your personal data based on our legitimate interests of those of a third party,
we have made sure that your fundamental rights and freedoms are not compromised
by pursuing these legitimate interests, in particular, when you are a minor.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5.3&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Based on a legal
obligation</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">We also process personal data if the law
requires us to do so, for example, to detect fraud or illegal activity.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5.4&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Based on your consent</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">We ask your consent before placing cookies
that are not strictly necessary for the functioning of the Chesstar Website
(e.g. for analytics or third-party cookies). We also ask your consent in order
to send you promotional updates about the Chesstar Products or for Chesstar
Products functions that interact with your device. Whenever the processing of
your personal data is based on your consent, you may opt out at any time.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; WHO ARE THE RECIPIENTS
OF MY PERSONAL DATA?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Chesstar shares your personal data with its
partners, financial and other advisors and service providers in order to
provide and optimize the Chesstar Website and Chesstar Products. We will never
sell your personal data&nbsp; or share your personal data with third parties if
we have no legal grounds to do so. In particular, we share your personal data
with the following categories of recipients:</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6.1&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Chesstar suppliers</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">In order to offer you the Chesstar Website
and Chesstar Products, we call on a number of independent companies and service
providers. Such third parties aid us, for example, in securing our
infrastructure and IT services, carrying out payment transactions through our
services, conducting surveys… It is possible that such suppliers gain access to
your personal data in the context of their work for Chesstar. Chesstar only
allows this to extent that access is necessary for carrying out the work that
such suppliers perform for Chesstar and only insofar as the suppliers provide
the necessary assurances regarding access and potential use of your personal
data and information.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6.2&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Social media networks</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Certain Chesstar online services integrate
social media “buttons” that interact with social media networking websites such
as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram… These buttons allow you to share information
about your achievements on these platforms. The buttons are provided and
managed by the social media websites. If you wish to receive more information
on how your personal data is shared when making use of such button, we advise
you to read the privacy policies of the relevant social media network.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6.3&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Authorities</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">In certain circumstances, we will be
required to share your data with the authorities. We may, for example, do so in
order to satisfy the law or a court order, to detect and address fraud, or to
protect Chesstar’s rights.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">6.4&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Other users</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">When you are making use of the Chesstar
Products, certain personal data, such as your user name, will be visible to the
other users playing in the game server (up to 10 players).</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">7.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; WILL MY DATA BE
TRANSFERRED OUTSIDE OF THE EEA?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Some of the recipients named above are
located outside the European Economic Area. If we transfer your personal data
to parties outside the EEA, we will make sure that such transfer is accompanied
by appropriate safeguards to ensure your personal data is adequately protected.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">8.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; HOW IS YOUR DATA
SECURED?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">It is important to Chesstar that your data
is handled securely. Therefore, we make use of various security technologies
and measures, to suitably protect your data against unauthorized access, use,
loss or publication. Unfortunately, the exchange of information over the
Internet is never 100% secure. Although we do our best to guarantee that your
data remains secure, we cannot guarantee this absolutely.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">9.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; HOW LONG IS YOUR DATA
RETAINED?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">We will retain your personal data for as
long as is needed to pursue the purposes as set out in this privacy policy. For
example, Chesstar will store your personal data for as long as it has an
ongoing relationship with you or for as long as needed to ensure compliance
with its legal or contractual obligations.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">10.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; HOW CAN YOU MANAGE
YOUR DATA?</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">You, as a data subject, have certain rights
with regard to your personal data. You can consult the personal data that
Chesstar collects about you at any time, and correct any incomplete or
incorrect information about you. You can request data portability, erasure and
object to the processing of your personal data. You also have the right not to
be subject to profiling or automated decision-making.</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Some of these rights can be exercised by
making use of the relevant features on the Chesstar Website or the Chesstar
Products. If you are unsure how to exercise your rights or you have a request
that is not covered on the Chesstar Website or the Chesstar Products, you can
contact Chesstar by sending an e-mail to contact@chesstar.com . If you want to
exercise your rights, we ask that you be as specific as possible, so that we
can address your request thoroughly and appropriately. We also remind you that
we must be able to reasonably verify your identity in order for you to exercise
your rights, so that we can prevent someone else doing so in your name.</span></p>
