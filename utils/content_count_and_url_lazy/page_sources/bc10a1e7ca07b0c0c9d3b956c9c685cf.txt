<p>We may collect the following personal information that you provide to us.</p>
<ul>

<li><strong>Purchases</strong>. We may collect aggregate information from Oculus or Steam (e.g., what has been purchased and refund metrics). <li><strong>Your Communications with Us</strong>. <li><strong>Business Development and Strategic Partnerships.</strong>We may collect personal information from individuals and third parties to assess and pursue potential business opportunities.</li>
