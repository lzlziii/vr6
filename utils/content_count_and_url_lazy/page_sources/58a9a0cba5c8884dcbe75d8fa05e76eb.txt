									<p>With a variety of upbeat music tracks, infinite levels, epic scale clockwork rooms and obstacles, you'll enjoy travelling at high speed and testing your reactions and focus. Keep your eyes ahead of the game, don't touch anything and survive the Mechanism as long as you can.</p>
