		<h1>BE THE HORROR</h1>
<p>Play as a demon in this story driven slasher horror game. Summoned from the depths of hell into our world, use your demonic abilities to gruesomely take down everything between you and your path back to the underworld.<br>
Massacre those who wish to orchestrate your destiny. Dismember any living being. Spawn your minions to help you in your quest for blood.</p>
<p>Available on:</p>
<p><a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/858250/Tainted_Fate/"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-2074 size-medium" src="http://www.misfitvillage.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/steam-logo-300x192.png" alt="steam-logo" width="300" height="192" srcset="http://www.misfitvillage.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/steam-logo-300x192.png 300w, http://www.misfitvillage.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/steam-logo.png 480w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px"></a></p>
