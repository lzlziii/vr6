					<h1 class="entry-title">Dreams of Dalí in Virtual Reality</h1>			<h2 class="subtitle"></h2>
						<p id="back_to_exhibits"><a href="https://thedali.org/exhibit/">&lt; All Exhibits</a></p>

	<div class="entry-content">
		<div class="container">
						<div class="">
				<h3>Travel Inside and Beyond the Art: <em>Dreams of Dalí</em> in Virtual Reality</h3>
<p>Enjoy the remarkable marriage of art and technology&nbsp;in <em>Dreams of Dalí</em>, a virtual reality&nbsp;experience, as you explore&nbsp;Dali’s painting&nbsp;<em>Archaeological&nbsp;Reminiscence of Millet’s “Angelus.” </em>Immerse yourself in&nbsp;the world of the Surrealist master like never before in this encounter, venturing into the towers, peering from them to distant&nbsp;lands and discovering surprises around every corner. We imagine Dalí himself, known in his lifetime as what we now call an “earlier adopter” of new technology, would applaud this inspiring homage to his 1935 painting.</p>
<p>This award-winning VR experience&nbsp;has garnered visitor acclaim, online praise and international recognition through a multitude of industry awards, including the prestigious Cannes Cyber Lion GOLD; a &nbsp;Webby People’s Voice award; and a Facebook Silver award for Innovation, among others.</p>
<p>Museums and educational institutions interested in licensing <em>Dreams of Dalí</em> may contact <a href="mailto:VRlicense@thedali.org">VRlicense@thedali.org</a>.</p>
<h3><hr class="">Download the Interactive Experience</h3>
<p><strong>For a fully immersive virtual reality experience from anywhere in the world, using your Oculus Rift or HTC Vive, download&nbsp;<em>Dreams of Dalí&nbsp;</em>from these leading VR platforms:&nbsp;</strong></p>

<p>When&nbsp;you can’t visit The Dalí in person, or if you don’t own a Rift, you may view a linear 360° video version of Dreams of Dalí from a variety of devices such as a Samsung Gear VR or Google Cardboard or Daydream.</p>
<div class=" dv1-column one-third"><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zQ2-oJOkTKc" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-6517 alignnone" src="https://thedali.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/DoD-360-image-with-button-1.png" alt="360" width="589" height="389" data-youtube="zQ2-oJOkTKc"></a></div>

For guests ages 13 and up. Disclaimer: Consult your physician prior to using the headsets if you are prone to blackouts, disorientation, seizures, or experience photosensitive epilepsy.</p>
