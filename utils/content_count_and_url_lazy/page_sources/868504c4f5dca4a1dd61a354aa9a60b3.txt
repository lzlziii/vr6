aria-label="">Your rights and how we use personal information.</h2><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>The British Film Institute (the “<span class="caps">BFI</span>”, “we”) is a registered charity (number 287780), Royal Charter Body, National Lottery distributor and non-departmental public body funded by the department of Digital, Culture, Media and Sport.</p>
<p>The <span class="caps">BFI</span> is committed to respecting and protecting your personal information.</p>
<p>This policy seeks to inform you&nbsp;about:</p>
<ul><li>how and why we collect, process and store your personal&nbsp;information</li>
<li>your&nbsp;Rights</li>
</ul><p>This policy applies to all the <span class="caps">BFI</span>’s activities, services, products and platforms.</p>
<p>The <span class="caps">BFI</span> is the Data Controller and Richard Brousson is the Data Protection Officer under the <span class="caps">UK</span>’s Data Protection Act 2018 and the <span class="caps">EU</span>’s General Data Protection Regulation 2016.</p>
<p>All questions and complaints about the <span class="caps">BFI</span>’s use and handling of personal information should be addressed to <a href="mailto:dpo@bfi.org.uk">dpo@bfi.org.uk</a>.</p>
<p>You also have the right of complaint to the <span class="caps">UK</span>’s Supervisory Authority for Data – the Information Commissioners Office (the “<span class="caps">ICO</span>”). See <a href="https://ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/">ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint</a>.</p>
<p>Information about personal data relating to volunteering, working for or applying to work for the <span class="caps">BFI</span> can be found here: <span class="caps">BFI</span> Privacy Notice: Workforce and&nbsp;Recruitment</p>
</div><h2 class="Headline__H2-sc-e1fxl5-2 hwajTk" aria-label="">Information we may collect about you</h2><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>We may collect and process the following information about&nbsp;you:</p>
</div><h3 class="Headline__H3-sc-e1fxl5-3 bdTzLA" aria-label="">Information you give us</h3><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><ul><li>Your prefix and name, billing and delivery address, postcode, email address, phone numbers, gender, date of birth, diversity monitoring information (anonymously unless stated otherwise), job title, personal identification documents, data relating to access or dietary requirements, payment card&nbsp;information</li>
<li>If you are making a grant application – your position within the company applying and relevant&nbsp;experience</li>
<li>If you are a student – your course details, relevant experience, school, college or&nbsp;university</li>
<li>You may provide us with this information when you set up an account (e.g. <span class="caps">BFI</span> Player, membership), apply for a grant (<span class="caps">BFI</span> Film Fund), apply for a course (<span class="caps">BFI</span> Education), apply to the <span class="caps">BFI</span>’s certification unit, buy tickets (<span class="caps">BFI</span> Southbank, <span class="caps">BFI</span> <span class="caps">IMAX</span>, <span class="caps">BFI</span> Festivals), fill in a form (competitions and surveys), complete an application form or otherwise apply for a job here either directly or via a third party, apply to volunteer, or correspond with us by phone, email or in&nbsp;writing</li>
</ul></div><h3 class="Headline__H3-sc-e1fxl5-3 bdTzLA" aria-label="">Information we collect automatically</h3><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><ul><li>Device <span class="caps">ID</span>s, <span class="caps">IP</span> addresses and other unique&nbsp;identifiers</li>
<li>Your activity on our services and platforms such as title selections and browsing&nbsp;history</li>
<li>Advertising identifiers, such as those on mobile devices, tablets and streaming media devices and information collected by cookies (please see our Cookies and internet advertising policy&nbsp;below)</li>
</ul></div><h3 class="Headline__H3-sc-e1fxl5-3 bdTzLA" aria-label="">Information we collect about you from other sources</h3><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><ul><li>Service providers that help us determine a location based on your <span class="caps">IP</span>&nbsp;address</li>
<li>Select partners that make our services available on their platform or with whom we offer co-branded services or engage in joint&nbsp;marketing</li>
<li>Publicly available information such as geo-demographic information and measures of capacity and propensity for giving to help us fundraise effectively and in line with our ethical policies from sites such as Companies&nbsp;House</li>
<li>Social media – depending on your settings you may have given us permission to access your personal information held by companies such as Facebook and&nbsp;Twitter</li>
</ul></div><h3 class="Headline__H3-sc-e1fxl5-3 bdTzLA" aria-label="">Third parties may collect information about you</h3><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>When using our websites and platforms third parties may collect information about you. This can be controlled by adjusting the settings in your internet browsers. There is more detail about adjusting your browser settings on the <a href="https://ico.org.uk/your-data-matters/online/cookies/">Information Commissioner’s Office&nbsp;website.</a></p>
</div><h2 class="Headline__H2-sc-e1fxl5-2 hwajTk" aria-label="">Why we collect and process your personal information</h2><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>We use your personal data for the following&nbsp;purposes:</p>
<ul><li>Carrying out research in the public&nbsp;interest</li>
<li>Maintaining, preserving and enhancing the <span class="caps">BFI</span> National&nbsp;Archive</li>
<li>Providing you with customer service when you visit, make use of or access any of the <span class="caps">BFI</span> premises, services or&nbsp;platforms</li>
<li>To administer any of your accounts, applications, contracts, enquiries or&nbsp;complaints</li>
<li>To process&nbsp;payments</li>
<li>Fulfil ticket, product, donation and membership&nbsp;requests</li>
<li>Contacting&nbsp;you</li>
<li>Providing you with information about things we think may interest&nbsp;you</li>
<li>To prevent, detect and investigate illegal activity such as fraud. The personal information we have collected from you will be shared with fraud prevention agencies who will use it to prevent fraud and money-laundering and to verify your identity. If fraud is detected, you could be refused certain services, finance, or employment. For further details of how your information will be used by us and these fraud prevention agencies, and your data protection rights, see <a href="https://www.cifas.org.uk/fpn">Fair Processing Notices for Cifas’s&nbsp;Databases</a></li>
<li>Analyse and understand our members, audiences and customers and improve our&nbsp;services</li>
<li>To protect and keep your personal information&nbsp;accurate</li>
<li>Using <span class="caps">CCTV</span> recording equipment in and around our premises for security&nbsp;purposes</li>
</ul></div><h2 class="Headline__H2-sc-e1fxl5-2 hwajTk" aria-label="">Special categories of personal information</h2><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>Data protection law recognises certain “special categories” of personal information, which include information revealing racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, trade union membership, genetic information, biometric information for uniquely identifying a person, information concerning health, and information concerning a person’s sex life or sexual orientation.</p>
<p>These special categories are considered particularly sensitive and so we will only collect and use this information where you have given us your explicit consent. For example, you may choose to tell us about your health condition before attending one of our events and we will only use this information for the particular event and not for any other&nbsp;purpose.</p>
</div><h2 class="Headline__H2-sc-e1fxl5-2 hwajTk" aria-label="">Lawful bases for processing your personal information</h2><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>Our legal basis for collecting and processing your personal information depends on the type of personal information and how we use it. There are 6 lawful&nbsp;bases:</p>
<ul><li>Consent – acquiring your agreement for us to process personal data for a precise purpose. You can withdraw consent for the storage and processing of data at any time (<a href="mailto:dpo@bfi.org.uk">dpo@bfi.org.uk</a>)</li>
<li>Public task – something we’re required to do as a public charity for example maintaining and enhancing the <span class="caps">BFI</span> National&nbsp;Archive</li>
<li>Legitimate interests – where we have a legitimate interest that isn’t overridden by your fundamental rights and freedoms for example something we think you’d be happy for us to do like contact you when you have actively shown an interest in our activities or&nbsp;services</li>
<li>Contract – fulfilment of a contract e.g. subscribing to <span class="caps">BFI</span> Player or becoming a <span class="caps">BFI</span>&nbsp;member</li>
<li>Legal obligation – where we have to use data as prescribed by&nbsp;law</li>
<li>Vital interests – protecting your health and personal&nbsp;safety</li>
</ul></div><h2 class="Headline__H2-sc-e1fxl5-2 hwajTk" aria-label="">Sharing of personal data</h2><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>We only share your personal data with third parties where we have a legal basis for doing so, such as with your consent or the fulfilment of a contract. For example, to invite you to an event and administer payments. We will never sell your personal&nbsp;information.</p>
</div><h2 class="Headline__H2-sc-e1fxl5-2 hwajTk" aria-label="">Transfers Outside of the European Economic Area (the “EEA”)</h2><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>Where we transfer personal information outside of the <span class="caps">EEA</span> for processing this is carried out in accordance with all applicable laws and with third parties we&nbsp;trust.</p>
</div><h2 class="Headline__H2-sc-e1fxl5-2 hwajTk" aria-label="">Retention Policies and Periods</h2><div class="Text__Outer-sc-1acyvkb-0 cjbegI text_block"><p>We only keep your personal information for as long as is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. This depends on the personal information and the purpose for which it is processed.
