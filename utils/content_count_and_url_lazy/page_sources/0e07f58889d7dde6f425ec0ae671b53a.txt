	<h2>BCITs website and personal information</h2>
<p>When you visit BCITs website, BCITs web servers automatically collect information that is essential to operating and maintaining the sites security. The information collected includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Browser type</li>
<li>IP address</li>
</ul>
<p>Some portions of the BCIT website may distribute small pieces of information (called cookies) to web browsers to assist you when you return to specific areas on the site. If you have concerns about cookies, you can change your web browser settings not to accept this information, or display warning messages.</p>
<p>BCITs website contains links to third party sites, and BCIT is not responsible for the privacy provisions of those other websites.</p>
<p>When you voluntarily send electronic mail to BCIT or complete an electronic form that includes identifying information about you, BCIT will keep a record of this information so that we can respond to you.</p>
<h2>BCIT and the protection of personal information</h2>
<p>As a public body, BCIT complies with privacy provisions of BCs&nbsp;<a href="https://www.bclaws.gov.bc.ca/civix/document/id/complete/statreg/96165_01"><em>Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act</em></a>&nbsp;(RSBC 1996 ch. 165):</p>
<ul>
<li>Personal information is collected directly from the individual about whom it relates</li>
<li>Personal information is collected, used, and disclosed for purposes consistent with BCITs program and service offerings</li>
<li>Individuals have the right of access to and a right to request correction of their own personal information</li>
<li>Personal information is retained for the time period to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected</li>
<li>Personal information is secured from unauthorized access</li>
<li>Individuals will be notified about the purposes for which information is collected, and when information is collected for an optional use, individuals will consent to the information collection</li>
</ul>
<p>You may refer to BCITs policy&nbsp;<a href="https://www.bcit.ca/files/pdf/policies/6700.pdf">6700 Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy [PDF]</a>, for more information.</p>
<p>For more information about BCITs privacy procedures you may contact:</p>

Associate Director, Information Access and Privacy<br>
British Columbia Institute of Technology<br>
3700 Willingdon Avenue<br>
Burnaby, B.C., Canada V5G 3H2</p>
