        <h2>Exterminate - Privacy Policy</h2>

        <p>
            Exterminate makes use of an analytical data gathering service ("Unity Analytics") provide by Unity Technologies and its subsidiaries and affiliates (collectively “Unity”) to measure and analyze how players progress through the game.
        </p>

        <p>
            By downloading, installing, or playing Exterminate you agree to the terms set forth in this document and those found in Unity's privacy policy, which can be found at the following URL: <a href="https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy" target="_blank">https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy</a>.
        </p>

        <p>
            EfficiencyLab, LLC manually submits the following information to Unity Analytics:
        </p>
            <ul>
                <li>Metadata about the play environment, such as how many walls were detected and how large they are;</li>
                <li>Data regarding level progression, such as how long it took to complete a given level, how many enemies were defeated, how many times player abilities were activated, etc.</li>
            </ul>

        <p>
            In addition, the Unity Analytics service automatically collects certain device information in an attempt to uniquely identify game installs and for statistical analysis.
            According to Unity's privacy policy, "the developer of the game has ongoing access to this data. Other Unity customers may have access to aggregated reports about game activity in general across a number of games."
            This information includes (but might not be limited to) the following:
        </p>
        <ul>
            <li>Unique device identifier generated from the device MAC/IMEI/MEID, which they alter to limit the ability to identify the relevant device in the future;</li>
            <li>IP address;</li>
            <li>Device manufacturer and model;</li>
            <li>The operating system and version running on your system or device;</li>
            <li>Language;</li>
            <li>The make of the CPU, and number of CPUs present;</li>
            <li>The graphics card information, such as type, vendor and driver name, version and graphics API;</li>
            <li>Amount of system and video RAM present;</li>
            <li>Current screen resolution;</li>
            <li>Operating system identifier (e.g., Mac, Windows, etc.);</li>
            <li>A checksum of all the data that gets sent to verify that it did transmit correctly; and</li>
            <li>App ID of the game installed.</li>
        </ul>

        <p>
            The Unity privacy policy states:
            "We primarily use the information we collect to provide, administer, operate and improve our Services. We may use your information for the following purposes:
        </p>
        <ul>
            <li>To perform analytics and research aimed at improving the accuracy, effectiveness, usability, or popularity of products and services that Unity and Developers provide;</li>
            <li>To create and share statistics and reports regarding aggregate usage of Unity-powered apps;"</li>
        </ul>

        <p>
            All information gathered is aggregated and anonymized. Employees and affiliates of EfficiencyLab, LLC and Unity have no way of using information collected from the game to personally identify you.
        </p>

        <p>
            EfficiencyLab, LLC and its affiliates use information gathered manually and automatically by Unity Analytics for the sole purpose of improving the Exterminate product by examining where players experience difficulty in progression. Information collected will never be sold to a third party, or used for any purpose that does not directly involve improving the Exterminate product.
        </p>
