<h3>Privacy Policy House Defender</h3>
<p>2Sync (Moritz Loos &amp; Julian Zietemann, hereafter always referred to as 2Sync) cares about the protection of your data! Accordingly, we have developed the following privacy policy to help you understand how we collect, use, share, and otherwise utilize personal information. In addition, 2Sync’s privacy policy at <a class="notion-link-token notion-enable-hover" href="https://2sync.io/dataprivacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" data-token-index="1" data-reactroot=""><span class="link-annotation-unknown-block-id--1109057339">https://2sync.io/dataprivacy</span></a> also applies.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Which kinds of data are collected?</strong><br>When choosing to use House Defender, we may collect certain information. This information includes both personal and usage data and may contain information such as:<br>–&nbsp;Room information; wall lengths and positions, object sizes and positions in the room (according to the user’s input)<br>– When using the multiplayer; user ID and IP address of the device (use of data by Photon Cloud Services).</p>
<p><strong>How does the app use user data?</strong><br>We use the collected data solely for the gaming experience! All data collected is clearly related to the game – without the data, playing would not be possible:<br>– Room data; the room data is entered manually by the user and is used to create the virtual world. Your room data is used by the 2Sync SDK for the optimal creation of the virtual objects. The data will not be passed on to third parties, it will only be stored on your device!<br>– Multiplayer data; this data is used to allow you to play with other players in individual lobbies and to be matched together. The game lobbies are provided by the Photon Cloud service, to which a data transfer of user ID and IP address takes place. Photon has also implemented many measures to ensure the protection of your data and a data processing agreement is in place. You can find Photon’s privacy policy here: <a class="notion-link-token notion-enable-hover" href="https://dashboard.photonengine.com/en-US/account/privacyandcookiepolicy" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" data-token-index="1" data-reactroot=""><span class="link-annotation-unknown-block-id-441037929">https://dashboard.photonengine.com/en-US/account/privacyandcookiepolicy</span></a></p>
<p><strong>How can users request or delete their data?</strong><br>Since we do not store any user data, deletion is possible by uninstalling the app from your device and the corresponding app folders. If you still have questions about data protection or data processing, you are welcome to contact us at <a class="notion-link-token notion-enable-hover" href="mailto:hello@2sync.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" data-token-index="1" data-reactroot=""><span class="link-annotation-unknown-block-id--1536068144">Hello@2Sync.io</span></a>!</p>
