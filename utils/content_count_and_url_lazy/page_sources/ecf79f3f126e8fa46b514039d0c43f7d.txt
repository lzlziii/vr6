<p>● operate and improve the Games and future games we may make;</p>

<p>● understand you and your preferences to enhance your experience and enjoyment using the Games;</p>

<p>● respond to your comments and questions and provide customer service;</p>

<p>● link or combine it with other information we get from third parties and other users of the Games, to help understand your preferences and provide you with better services.</p>

<p>The data and information that we collect will be stored and maintained by Oddly Satisfying Studios until you instruct us otherwise, or, in our sole discretion, for the longer of: (a) for so long as is necessary or appropriate to carry out the purpose(s) for which such information was collected and (b) for so long as we are required to maintain such information by law or other applicable rules or regulations and your consent to such purposes remains valid after termination of our relationship with you.</p>
