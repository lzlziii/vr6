					<h1 class="page-header"></h1>
										<div class="entry"><div id="pl-938" class="panel-layout"><div id="pg-938-0" class="panel-grid panel-no-style"><div id="pgc-938-0-0" class="panel-grid-cell"><div id="panel-938-0-0-0" class="so-panel widget widget_sow-editor panel-first-child panel-last-child" data-index="0"><div class="so-widget-sow-editor so-widget-sow-editor-base">
<div class="siteorigin-widget-tinymce textwidget">
	<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;">The <strong>DEMoCap</strong> is a Free Software Application allowing to do <strong>Motion Capture</strong> using <strong>VR equipment</strong>.</span></p>
</div>
</div></div></div></div><div id="pg-938-1" class="panel-grid panel-no-style"><div id="pgc-938-1-0" class="panel-grid-cell"><div id="panel-938-1-0-0" class="so-panel widget widget_sow-image panel-first-child" data-index="1"><div class="so-widget-sow-image so-widget-sow-image-default-c67d20f9f743-938">

<div class="sow-image-container">
		<img src="https://dragondreams.ch/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/democap-1.0-768x461.png" width="768" height="461" srcset="https://dragondreams.ch/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/democap-1.0-768x461.png 768w, https://dragondreams.ch/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/democap-1.0-300x180.png 300w, https://dragondreams.ch/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/democap-1.0-150x90.png 150w, https://dragondreams.ch/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/democap-1.0.png 1007w" sizes="(max-width: 768px) 100vw, 768px" alt="" loading="lazy" class="so-widget-image">
	</div>

</div></div><div id="panel-938-1-0-1" class="so-panel widget widget_sow-editor" data-index="2"><div class="so-widget-sow-editor so-widget-sow-editor-base">
<div class="siteorigin-widget-tinymce textwidget">
	<p style="text-align: center;">There are various advantages using VR compared to other techniques:</p>
<ul>
<li style="text-align: center;">Less expensive equipment</li>
<li style="text-align: center;">Quick to equip actor for performing</li>
<li style="text-align: center;">Does not require large rooms build especially for motion capture</li>
<li style="text-align: center;">Allows actors to see the scene in which they have to perform</li>
<li style="text-align: center;">Capture motion in real time without post processing</li>
<li style="text-align: center;">Capture any count of characters no matter if a single person or a group</li>
<li style="text-align: center;">Easily re-take captures of individual characters without re-taking all</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div></div><div id="panel-938-1-0-2" class="so-panel widget widget_sow-editor panel-last-child" data-index="3"><div class="so-widget-sow-editor so-widget-sow-editor-base">
<div class="siteorigin-widget-tinymce textwidget">
	<p style="text-align: center;">DEMoCap is build using the Drag[en]gine game engine. The resulting captured animations are usable directly in the Drag[en]gine game engine or can be imported into Blender for editing. Using Blender the captured animations can be used for all other purposes Blender can be used for.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">DEMoCap uses the <strong>original armature without modification</strong>. The captured animation is directly usable.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Using <strong>Motion Transfers</strong> DEMoCap supports various kinds of armatures. You can capture humanoid characters, beastly characters, tails, wings, extra arms, whatever you want. You can also capture <strong>First Person Hands</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Using VR you can recreate the relevant parts of your game scene. This allows your actor to perform more natural and animations are better fitting to your game scene.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">In short DEMoCap accelerates the animation production allowing for higher quality animations to be done in less time.</p>
</div>
