<h1 class="wp-block-heading">About</h1>



<p>You are immersed in your VR game and you hear a message notification but you are too immersed to stop.&nbsp; What should you do?</p>



<p>Daily OVR brings your VR World closer to reality.&nbsp; It enables you to check your messages even while wearing your VR headset.&nbsp; You no longer have to be interrupted by taking your hmd off to catch your calls.&nbsp; By opening mulitple windows, you can see your messages in real time!</p>



<p></p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator">



<h1 class="wp-block-heading">Features</h1>



<ul><li>The most user-friendly OVR UI.</li><li>Mirror your favorite Windows applications into VR game.</li><li>Mirror the desktop or monitors.</li><li>Support up to 5 multiple overlays. </li><li>QuickSwitch let you quickly switch among programs.</li><li>Save the frequently used settings to “My Favorites.”</li><li>Adjust the size, transparency, and placement of overlays and keep your  settings for next time with user-friendly Interface and amazing  experience.</li><li>Easily launch the virtual keyboard on the overlay toolbar.</li><li>Mode change on the overlay toolbar.</li><li>Overlay FPS adjustment on the overlay toolbar.</li><li>Bind the overlay to different controllers or the HMD.</li><li>Support Oculus &amp; Windows MR (through SteamVR)</li><li>Different controller button setting for Vive &amp; Windows MR</li><li>Show/Hide Daily OVR icon </li><li>VR Phone Notification Pop-up.<br>Daily OVR allows you to bring your Android phone notification into the VR view; you won’t miss important messages anymore.<br>    – Phone calling notification<br>    – SMS<br>    – WhatsApp<br>    – LINE</li></ul>
