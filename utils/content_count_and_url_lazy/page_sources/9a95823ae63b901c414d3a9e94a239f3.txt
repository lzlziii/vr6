&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; - Motion Controllers (tested on Oculus Rift and HTC Vive)<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; - Your VR headset<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; - An XBox 360/One controller.&nbsp; Equivalent layout controllers *may* work.</p>

 </div><div class="n module-type-header diyfeLiveArea "> <h2><span class="diyfeDecoration">What bugs and issues are there?</span></h2> </div><div class="n module-type-text diyfeLiveArea "> <p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>- Satellite photography is best experienced with a bit of height.&nbsp; The illusion will not work when you're near the ground.&nbsp; I hope to address this in the future using World
Creator.<br>
- If you look behind you, the game will try and adjust your view so you look down your side.&nbsp; However, ultimately, if you intentionally move your head to defeat that compensation, you can look
through yourself!&nbsp; I can't stop you.<br>
- You can't currently 'trim' in pitch, although you can fly just fine without.<br>
- Observer mode does not output sound to your normal pc speakers, if you are using the Oculus&nbsp;Rift headphones.&nbsp; You need to change your settings on the Oculus software to enable sound
mirroring, or whichever output option you wish.</p>
<p>- Oculus Touch and HTC Vive are prone to problems with bright UV light, and requires a solid sensor setup.&nbsp; The flapping motion required with Oculus Touch requires your sensors to have a good
view of your complete motion, or it may not work correctly.</p>

<p>- Unity personal edition 2019.4.30f1, with the Oculus SDK and the current SteamVR release.</p>
<p>- Mirror Networking suite (<a href="https://mirror-networking.com/">https://mirror-networking.com/</a>).<br>
- World Composer, Terrain Composer and World Creator Pro.</p>
<p>- Bing Satellite photography.<br>
- Flight physics developed in-house.<br>
- Enviro for the day/night cycle.<br>
- Eagle models by All-Animation and Junnichi Suko.</p>
<p>- Icon/Cover photo by Neil Davies, featuring his white tailed sea eagle called Snatch.</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p> </div><div class="n module-type-header diyfeLiveArea "> <h2><span class="diyfeDecoration">Planned features:</span></h2> </div><div class="n module-type-text diyfeLiveArea "> <p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>- (PARTWAY THERE) More dynamic landscape with things to do of course (challenges).<br>
- Trophy/achievement support (the Steam version has some already).<br>
- (PARTWAY THERE) Maybe one large landscape, or many smaller ones. Next on the list is to add more ground detail.<br>
- Possible survival simulation including weather, defending your territory, nest provider, etc.<br>
- Special needs access (head steering is the start of this).</p>
