<h4 class="has-text-align-center has-white-color has-black-background-color has-text-color has-background has-large-font-size" style="font-style:normal;font-weight:200">ElectroSwing Privacy Policy</h4>



<div style="height:36px" aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer"></div>



<p class="has-text-align-center has-white-color has-black-background-color has-text-color has-background">What Data do we Collect?</p>



<h1 class="has-text-align-center has-white-color has-black-background-color has-text-color has-background has-medium-font-size" style="font-style:normal;font-weight:100">ElectroSwing never directly collects data from users. We only collect the automatic data provided by the Oculus Store, this includes analytics for downloads, playtime, game performance and reviews.</h1>



<p class="has-text-align-center has-white-color has-black-background-color has-text-color has-background">How do we Use The Data?</p>



<h1 class="has-text-align-center has-white-color has-black-background-color has-text-color has-background has-medium-font-size" style="font-style:normal;font-weight:100">We may use analytical data to gain insight on ways to improve player experience or game performance.</h1>



<p class="has-text-align-center has-white-color has-black-background-color has-text-color has-background">What Data Is Shared?</p>



<h1 class="has-text-align-center has-white-color has-black-background-color has-text-color has-background has-medium-font-size" style="font-style:normal;font-weight:100">We never sell data to third parties, ever! The only data we may share would be download count or reviews.</h1>
