						    <div class="entry-content">
							    <p>Minesweeper has finally come to Augmented and Virtual Reality! Flag, count, and open cells in this fully 3D reinvention of the classic game.</p>
<h2 class="bb_tag">Features</h2>
<ul class="bb_ul">
<li>View the entire minefield in cube view, or highlight an individual plane</li>
<li>Fully customizable minefield spawning parameters, with difficulty presets</li>
<li>Drag, drop, scale, and rotate the minefield to locate mines from any angle</li>
<li>Standard game mechanics and timer</li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: left;">Professional mine-defusers and newbies alike will be able to enjoy the challenges and gameplay of SweeperXR!</p>
