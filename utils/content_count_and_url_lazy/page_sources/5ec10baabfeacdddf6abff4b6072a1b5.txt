					<p><strong>DESCRIPTION</strong></p>
<p>An adventure and exploration game about wildlife in a dreamlike universe.</p>
<p>Somewhere in the abyssal depths of the internet, life has blossomed. Be the first to explore this virgin land where nature takes center stage. An unknown power seems to upset its balance. Join forces with intriguing and endearing creatures to solve the puzzles of Paper Beast.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><strong>GAME EXPERIENCE</strong></p>
<p><strong>The game exists in VR and in Flat screen.</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;">Explore a colorful and whimsical universe born from the imagination of Eric Chahi, creator of </span><em style="font-size: 14px;">Another World</em><span style="font-size: 14px;">.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;">&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size: 14px;">Two game modes: the adventure and the sandbox, an immersive playground.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Explore a world populated by majestic and intelligent creatures. Grab them, help them: their behaviour adapts to your actions.</span></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Control the environment. The gameplay is based on a unique physics engine. ivert rivers, melt the ice, withstand storms to solve puzzles… And fly away!&nbsp;</span></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Experiment! In the sandbox, live an experience close to a god game. Terraform mountains, dig lakes, populate your ecosystem, unleash tornadoes. Have fun with the physics of the game.</span></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">An original soundtrack combining the Japanese punk rock of TsuShiMaMire and the ambient style of Roly Porter.</span></li>
</ul>
