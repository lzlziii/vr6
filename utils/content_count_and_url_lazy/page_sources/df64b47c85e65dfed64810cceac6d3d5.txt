<h3 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;">FOR VR BEGINNERS AND VETERANS ALIKE</span></h3>
<p><span id="docs-internal-guid-2ed6eca3-7fff-2d50-1ba1-fa579cc4a4f8" style="font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; white-space: pre-wrap;">Bizarre Barber’s core foundation is simple, intuitive, and responsive. The single-step mechanics with humorous twists feel pleasant and rewarding in virtual reality. There is no better way to replicate this level of physicality, accuracy, and immersion than with 6 degrees of freedom VR. The experience is intuitively clear for players of all ages and backgrounds. The gameplay encourages players to employ various strategies, whether it is calculating the angle for the perfect move or to just be fast, physical, and chaotic.</span></span></p>

<h3 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 24px;">THE GAMEPLAY</span></h3>
<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 2.057142857142857; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: Arial; color: #707070; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">The approximate time players spend on each level is 3 minutes. A player can master a level by playing it 2-3 times before the next level is unlocked.&nbsp; There are 13 levels with different mechanics. Playing through the entire game will take 60-80 minutes and there’s a high potential of replayability.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p dir="ltr" style="line-height: 2.057142857142857; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 10.5pt; font-family: Arial; color: #707070; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 400; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: none; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">We emphasize accessibility by utilizing very intuitive motions for controllers. Most of the time, the controllers in the game work like hands in real life – by holding tools. No button, trigger, grip, or joystick presses are required for the main gameplay in Bizarre Barber. Players can’t drop tools accidentally — the tools are permanently attached to their hands. All the interactions feel natural — by wiggling, juggling, and rotating, the player can manipulate the tools properties and fulfill the job quickly and easily. Virtual reality gives the player a lot of agency over how to manipulate these tools and the design makes them feel powerful.</span></p>
