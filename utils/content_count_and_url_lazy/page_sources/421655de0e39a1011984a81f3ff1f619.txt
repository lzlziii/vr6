<p align="center"><b>“Your privacy is not a game”</b></p>
<h2></h2>
<p align="justify">Ubisoft teams create original and memorable experiences so that players can have fun, interact with others and take pleasure through our games, applications, websites and online services (collectively hereinafter referred to as our “<b>Services</b>”).</p>
<p align="justify">We collect your personal data so that you can benefit from our Services and we make it our duty to adhere to the rules of the game!</p>
<p align="justify">This privacy policy is designed to help you understand how your data is used, inform you of your rights and how to exercise your rights.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<h2 class="ubi_mceLegalContentIndex" id="ubi_mceLegalContentIndex_0"><b>1. What is personal data?</b></h2>
<p align="justify">“<b>Personal Data</b>” (or “<b>Data</b>”), is all information that identifies you:</p>
<ul>
<li>either directly, for example, your last name, first name, email address or telephone number;</li>
<li>or indirectly, for example your account ID, IP address, cookie identifier or game data.</li>
</ul>
<p align="justify">For more information about the Data that Ubisoft collects, go to Section&nbsp;3 “When does Ubisoft collect your Data and for what purpose?”.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<h2 class="ubi_mceLegalContentIndex" id="ubi_mceLegalContentIndex_1"><b>2. Can minors access our Services?</b></h2>
<p align="justify">The age at which minors can create a Ubisoft account or have access to some of our Services depends on the personal data protection laws of each country. For more information, please see the article on the access and terms specific to minors in our <a href="https://legal.ubi.com/termsofuse/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Terms of Use</a> of the Ubisoft Services.</p>

<p align="justify">When you create your Account, you can manage your subscription to receive the Ubisoft <i>newsletter </i>by email. You can unsubscribe at any time by:</p>
<ul>
<li>clicking on the unsubscribe link at the end of each <i>newsletter,</i></li>
