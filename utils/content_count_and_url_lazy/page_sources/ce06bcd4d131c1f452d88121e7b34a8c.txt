<h2>Supported platforms</h2>



<p>The game can be controlled by all supported VR controllers or by hand tracking, which is supported via <a href="https://www.ultraleap.com/product/leap-motion-controller/#whatsincluded" data-type="URL" data-id="https://www.ultraleap.com/product/leap-motion-controller/#whatsincluded">Leap Motion controller</a> mounted on the headset or via <a href="https://support.oculus.com/2720524538265875/#faq_192737238414957" data-type="URL" data-id="https://support.oculus.com/2720524538265875/#faq_192737238414957">Oculus Quest hand tracking feature</a>. </p>

<h2>Who is this game for?</h2>



<p class=".entry-meta { display: none; }">When playing this game, you <strong>don’t need to be a piano player</strong> in real life. Musical hearing is certainly good but not necessary. Big plus for you are fast hands and a portion of patience. This game is also great for piano players – but you shouldn’t imagine that it will be the same as a real piano. It is not and it does not trying to be. The goal of the game is to be colourful musical fun, where you can become part of some pretty good music.</p>



<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="aligncenter size-large is-resized"><img loading="lazy" src="https://vrpianist.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/vive_pro.png" alt="" class="wp-image-240" width="214" height="301" srcset="https://vrpianist.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/vive_pro.png 569w, https://vrpianist.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/vive_pro-214x300.png 214w" sizes="(max-width: 214px) 100vw, 214px"></figure></div>



<p>You can sit or stand during game, you don’t need to walk at all. You will need only <strong>small space for playing </strong>and you also don’t need to worry of motion sickness. You <strong>don’t need to have a piano at home</strong>, you can play on a normal table or simply in the air.</p>
