class="post-title">sphereFACE</h1></div> </header><div class="post-content"><div class="video-container"><iframe style="border:0" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/GJ2Rt6A-JHw" allowfullscreen=""></iframe></div><p><em>sphereFACE is a retro-styled 3D vector shooter, wrapped around the inside of a sphere.</em></p><p>An unashamedly abstract shooter that ranges from slow, strategic sniping to frantic rock-dodging action, with roguelike elements of exploration, progression, discovery and permadeath.</p><h4 id="gameplay">GAMEPLAY</h4><ul><li>Destroy the “asteroids” in each sphere to unlock the hyperspace portals leading to other spheres, and navigate through a vast procedurally generated 3D space, homing in on the disturbance that woke you from slumber.</li><li>Sophisticated inertial flight dynamics on the inner surface of a sphere are difficult to master - agility, awareness and good reflexes are essential to survive.</li><li>Look freely around each sphere using mouselook, a controller, your keyboard, or even your VR headset.</li><li>Roguelike single-save system featuring permadeath.</li><li>Dark and twisted science fiction storyline.</li><li>Arcade style top-10 high score system.</li><li>Determine your own difficulty curve - progress cautiously and slowly, or charge in with guns blazing.</li><li>Stray too deep into the hyperspace tunnel network and it won’t be long before you meet something that shoots back…</li></ul><h4 id="weapons--powerups">WEAPONS &amp; POWERUPS</h4><ul><li>Collect and upgrade a variety of destructive and unusual weapons, such as the spreadGUN, gaussRIFLE, guidedROCKETS, spaceMINES, flameTHROWER and singularityCANNON.</li><li>Destroy “enemy” ships and catch their “powerups” to improve your weapons. That is what they are, right?</li><li>Fashion armour from smashed asteroid rock, and find movement augmentation devices such as reverseTHRUSTERS to improve your maneuverability.</li><li>Each weapon has its own strategy and learning curve, but the true sphereFACE master must learn to use them in concert with each other.</li></ul><h4 id="technical">TECHNICAL</h4><ul><li>The game engine is completely custom built, which allows sphereFACE to have exactly the gameplay experience it needs with no limitations. The game is highly optimised and will run smoothly - even on that old notebook.</li><li>Fully configurable controls - bind any control to any key, mouse button, gamepad button or stick, joystick axis or even foot pedal; any combination of devices can be used to control the game.</li><li>Theme support - create and share a customised look for the game.</li><li>Simultaneous updates on Windows, Linux and macOS.</li><li>Ongoing development - new features and improvements added continuously, professional and highly responsive development team.</li></ul><h3 id="get-sphereface">Get sphereFACE</h3>
