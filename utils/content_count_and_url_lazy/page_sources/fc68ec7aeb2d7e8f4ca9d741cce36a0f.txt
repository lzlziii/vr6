											<h1 class="entry-title">Star-Stuff Privacy Policy</h1>
					
					<div class="entry-content">
						<p>This pri­vacy policy explains how we use the per­sonal data we col­lect from you as a user of Star-Stuff. To use this expe­ri­ence, you must con­sent to the data col­lec­tion terms described here. In addi­tion to this pri­vacy policy, we also use the third-party ser­vices includ­ing Oculus and Photon Engine. We use Oculus to dis­trib­ute and run the soft­ware, and Photon Cloud to handle game server, match­mak­ing, and net­work­ing ser­vices. You can read them&nbsp;here:</p>
<p><strong>Oculus</strong>: <a href="https://www.oculus.com/legal/privacy-policy/" style="outline: none;">https://www.oculus.com/legal/privacy-policy/</a><br>
<strong>Photon</strong>: <a href="https://dashboard.photonengine.com/en-US/Account/PrivacyAndCookiePolicy" style="outline: none;">https://dashboard.photonengine.com/en-US/Account/PrivacyAndCookiePolicy</a></p>
<h2 class="first">What data do we collect?</h2>
<p>We col­lect the fol­low­ing&nbsp;data:</p>
<ul>
<li>Photon Cloud col­lects the min­i­mal data required to pro­vide their ser­vice includ­ing the IP address of your device. See <a href="https://dashboard.photonengine.com/en-US/account/gdpr" style="outline: none;">https://dashboard.photonengine.com/en-US/account/gdpr</a> for details.</li>
<li>Aggregate anony­mous server usage data including:
<ul>
<li>Concurrent Users</li>
<li>Number of rooms in&nbsp;use</li>
<li>Server band­width</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>With your con­sent we may col­lect game­play data. This includes the following:
<ul>
<li>Hand/controller/headset posi­tion</li>
<li>Whether you are con­nected with another player.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>All other game data is col­lected to syn­chro­nize your expe­ri­ence with another remote user. This data is sent to a Photon Cloud server to be relayed to the other player. This data is not stored beyond the time required to com­mu­ni­cate the infor­ma­tion to the other player.</li>
</ul>
<h2>How do we collect your data?</h2>
<p>We col­lect data and process data auto­mat­i­cally when&nbsp;you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Run the expe­ri­ence and con­nect to the Photon Cloud server.</li>
<li>If you have con­sented to have game­play data recorded, we will auto­mat­i­cally record it until you dis­able game­play data record­ing (accessed in the intro screen), rein­stall, or unin­stall the experience.</li>
</ul>
<h2>How will we use your data?</h2>
<p>We col­lect your data so that we&nbsp;can:</p>
<ul>
<li>Connect you with other users for mul­ti­player networking.</li>
<li>Gameplay data is con­nected to gather insights into how you use the expe­ri­ence so that we can make improve­ments to the expe­ri­ence and develop new social expe­ri­ences for human con­nec­tion. This anony­mous data is only col­lected with your con­sent and may be included in aca­d­e­mic pub­li­ca­tions regard­ing the experience.</li>
</ul>
<h2>How do we store your data?</h2>
<p>Most of your data will only be stored on Photon Cloud servers. That data is only kept long enough to pro­vide you with the net­work­ing ser­vice for this expe­ri­ence. Aggregate data (e.g. number of users and band­width) for up to 24&nbsp;hours.</p>
<p>Anonymous game­play data is kept indef­i­nitely and may be shared on an open access data­base to facil­i­tate research by other researchers.</p>
<h2>What are your data protection rights?</h2>
<p>We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all your data pro­tec­tion rights.</p>
<p>Every user is enti­tled to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>The right to access – You have the right to request our copies of your per­sonal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.</li>
<li>The right to rec­ti­fi­ca­tion – You have the right to request that we cor­rect any infor­ma­tion you believe is inac­cu­rate. You also have the right to request us to com­plete the infor­ma­tion you believe is incomplete.</li>
<li>The right to era­sure – You have the right to request that we erase your per­sonal data, under cer­tain con­di­tions. Note that it may not be pos­si­ble to delete anony­mous data once it has been incor­po­rated into the dataset.</li>
<li>The right to restrict pro­cess­ing – You have the right to request that we restrict the pro­cess­ing of your per­sonal data, under cer­tain conditions.</li>
<li>The right to object to pro­cess­ing – You have the right to object to our pro­cess­ing of your per­sonal data, under cer­tain conditions.</li>
<li>The right to data porta­bil­ity – You have the right to request that we trans­fer the data that we have col­lected to another orga­ni­za­tion, or directly to you, under cer­tain conditions.</li>
</ul>
<p>If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exer­cise any of these rights, please con­tact us at our email&nbsp;below.</p>
<h2>Changes to our privacy policy</h2>
<p>We’ll keep this pri­vacy policy under reg­u­lar review and place any updates in this doc­u­ment. This pri­vacy policy was last updated on November 8,&nbsp;2021.</p>
<h2>How to contact us</h2>
<p>If you have any ques­tions about our pri­vacy policy, the data that’s col­lected, or you would like to exer­cise one of your data pro­tec­tion rights, please con­tact us at <a href="mailto:privacy@desnoyers.ca" style="outline: none;">privacy@desnoyers.ca</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
