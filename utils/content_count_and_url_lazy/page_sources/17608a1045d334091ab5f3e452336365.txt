<span class="collapseomatic " id="id1514" tabindex="0" title="Read more">Read more</span><span id="swap-id1514" class="colomat-swap" style="display:none;">Read less</span><div id="target-id1514" class="collapseomatic_content " style="display: none;">
<p><strong>Background<br>
</strong><br>
When we‚Äôve decided to include PSVR in our supported platforms, we knew it was going to be difficult‚Ä¶ and we were right, it is a huge task for a small indie studio like us.<br>
We‚Äôve never done anything like this, we have to learn the limitations of the platform, then shaping and forming A-Tech Cybernetic to fit in those limitations.</p>

<p>We‚Äôre planning to release the full version on PC (<em>Steam, Oculus Store and Viveport</em>) in 2019, probably late Q1, early Q2.<br>

<p>We won‚Äôt give ground to any difficulties, we‚Äôre going to work as hard as we can to bring A-Tech Cybernetic VR to PSVR as soon as possible!</p>
