"Hardware ID" an amount of numbers specifically for a device or system

A. Hardware ID
 Your Hardware ID is collected to be sent to Playfab and used to login to your Playfab/MonkeBuddy's VR Account

B. IP Address
 Your IP address is saved in your Playfab/MonkeBuddy's VR account

 Remembering the mention we made in 2A, your IP address is saved in your Playfab/MonkeBuddy's VR account, and is only used for for IP bans in-game
