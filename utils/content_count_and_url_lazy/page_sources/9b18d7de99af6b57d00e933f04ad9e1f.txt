  <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Soulace (Work in progress)</strong></p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Soulace is a visionary VR journey depicting the after death realm in a poetic way. The project was initially Inspired by the Tibetan Book of the Dead, an ancient manuscript used in the Tibetan tradition. It is read at the bedside of the deceased, guiding him through the several stages of the afterlife. In the process of creating this work, I tried to avoid a literal interpretation of the manuscript and, instead, recreate the atmosphere I envision for the dying process. This process, in my mind’s eye, is a journey towards the source of life. </p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Soulace is a meditative and contemplative Virtual Reality point and click experience, inspired by adventure games from the 1990’s like Myst and Atlantis. The project is raising questions about the purpose of life and the interconnectedness of all living creatures. </p>
