		<h1 class="entry-title">About</h1>	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><strong>Albino Moose Games</strong> is a game studio founded by two best friends and video game industry veterans, Sheena Perez and Travis Rupp-Greene. They decided to join forces and create games that they feel passionate about and enjoy. From RPGs, MOBAs, RTS, and so on, they both enjoy playing all types of games and wanted to take their favorite games they enjoy and make their own.</p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#585aad;">Sheena Perez –&nbsp;<span style="color:#000000;">Co-Founder</span></span></strong></p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;">Sheena Perez is one-half and Co-Founder of Albino Moose Games. She started her career in the industry 8 years ago as a sound designer and a freelance composer. After creating a Pong game in Unity, she decided that being a game developer was up her alley. She released a game titled <em>Spooky’s Jump Scare Mansion</em>&nbsp;formerly known as&nbsp;<em>Spooky’s House of Jump Scares </em>on Steam. She decided to partner up with her best friend Travis Rupp-Greene and create passion projects under their own game studio. Sheena is the PR manager, composer, business manager and game developer for Albino Moose.</p>
<p><strong><span style="color:#585aad;">Travis Rupp-Greene</span></strong><span style="color:#000000;"> –&nbsp;<strong>Co-Founder</strong></span></p>
<p style="padding-left:30px;">Travis Rupp-Greene is the other Co-Founder and half of Albino Moose Games. He&nbsp;knew he wanted to be a game developer when he got his first computer with QBASIC on it in fourth grade – he wasn’t allowed to play games during the week so instead he programmed them (to the detriment of schoolwork).&nbsp;While he was burning out after around a decade in the traditional industry he met Sheena Perez and became best friends, and eventually they struck out to found Albino Moose Games. He is a severe&nbsp;engineering generalist being able to do anything from engineer systems for millions of users to UI/UX design to writing shaders and modelling brains.</p>
