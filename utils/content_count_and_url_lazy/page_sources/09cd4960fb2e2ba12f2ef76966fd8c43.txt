</header><div class="entry-content"><p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>A stickman reality</strong> is an immersive VR experience which uses 2D and 3D animations in a 3D VR environment to engage the player in an interactive story. By presenting an interesting mix of meaningful interactions in a linear story, we get players to be an active part of the story and project their experiences on the storyline. We also show how 2D animations can play a meaningful role in a VR experience.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>A Stickman Reality was demonstrated to a large audience at the Indigo 2018 edition. Visitors were positively suprised by the experience.</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The experience works on different devices: HTC Vive, Oculus Rift, and Windows Mixed Reality headsets.&nbsp;Here is the <a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/889220/A_Stickman_Reality/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Steam download page</a>.</span></p>
<p>Find the <a href="https://improvive.com/en/presskit/">Press Kit here</a></p>
<p><em><strong>ImproVive and VR</strong></em></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Starting 2017 ImproVive has been developing and investigating the power of storytelling in VR. We believe that VR can be a powerful tool in immersing people in stories and creating empathy. VR is a unique media which allows users to experience a sense of physical space and interaction that feels much more natural, not just through the Headset, but especially by enabling interaction using your arm and hand movement. With VR we can create meaningful experiences between people where the quality of interaction goes beyond picking up a gun and shooting someone or something. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">ImproVive has worked on several pilot VR projects to explore these possibilities. “A stickman Reality” is one of those projects.</span></p>
