<p class="home-a-details text-center"><a class="button heading-y background-y hover-light" href="#about">About Us</a><a class="button heading-x background-x border-hard-x hover-light" href="#projects">Our Work</a></p>
</div></div> </div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="view x40-widget widget   hide-on-small-devices" id="layers-widget-skrollex-section-16">
<div class="fg colors-b  no-top-padding no-bottom-padding">
<div class="layout-boxed section-top"><p class="lead"><span style="color:null;">NatureSims is a virtual reality design team creating new nature simulations that are part game, part documentary and all kinds of educational and entertaining. We build VR experiences that allow users to see the world from the viewpoint of diverse living creatures—highlighting their beauty and the challenges they face as climate change and other factors affect their ecosystems on an ever-increasing basis.</span></p>
</div> </div>
</div>
<div class="view x40-widget widget text-bg player" id="layers-widget-skrollex-section-17" data-text-effect-selector="h1,h2,h3,h4" data-text-effect="effect-a-animated">
<div id="about" class="fg colors-c ">
<div class="layout-boxed section-top"><h3 class="heading-section-title text-bg-svg svg-effect" style="height: 103px;"><svg width="100%" height="100%" viewBox="0 0 896.4 103"><desc>Created with Snap</desc><defs><pattern x="0" y="0" width="262.9178598766508" height="86" patternUnits="userSpaceOnUse" id="patternSlmcx63mu7" viewBox="0 0 300 86" preserveAspectRatio="none"><g><rect width="100%" height="86" class="fill-highlight"></rect><rect width="90%" height="86" class="fill-heading"></rect><rect width="50%" height="86" class="fill-highlight"></rect><rect width="20%" height="86" class="fill-heading"></rect></g></pattern></defs><text x="0" y="0" fill="url('#patternSlmcx63mu7')"><tspan dy="86" x="448" style="text-anchor: middle;">What we do</tspan></text></svg></h3>
<p class="header-details">A Documentary<span> in VR</span></p>
<p>NatureSims combines biological, psychological and ecological research with inventive virtual and augmented reality technology to create enjoyable and educational VR and AR experiences.</p>
<p><strong><span>The Goal</span>:</strong> to allow players to defy the limitations of their own bodies, to experience the world from the vantage of other living creatures—a vastly different and mind-expanding reality. Step outside your perspective and look at the world in an entirely new way.</p>
</div> <div class="section-cols layout-boxed">
<div class="pure-g"> <div class="layers-widget-skrollex-section-55c8d1dfc0f70041705364 pure-u-1 pure-u-lg-12-24  col-padding">
<div class="col-icon color-heading">
<i class="fa fa-eye"></i>
</div>
<div class="col-content">
<h5 class="heading-col-title">First Person<span> Experiences</span></h5>
<p>See the world through the eyes of our animal cousins. Confront the challenges they face as a matter of course. Experience their highs and their lows as they wind their way from one challenging stage of life to the next.</p>
</div></div> <div class="layers-widget-skrollex-section-55c8d1dfc0f7c247758097 pure-u-1 pure-u-lg-12-24  col-padding">
<div class="col-icon color-heading">
<i class="fa fa-paw"></i>
</div>
<div class="col-content">
<h5 class="heading-col-title">Building Empathy <span>For Nature</span></h5>
<p>Connect with non-human living beings in a way not heretofore possible in a format that is part simulation, part video game. Now, you can live the nature documentary as an active participant.</p>
</div></div></div> </div>
</div>
</div>

<div id="how-we-work" class="fg colors-d ">
<div class="layout-boxed section-top"><h4 class="heading-subsection-title"><span>Our</span> Process</h4>
<p class="narrow">The key to our work is translating academic research into immersive nature simulations suitable for all ages.</p>
</div> <div class="section-cols layout-boxed">
<div class="pure-g"> <div class="layers-widget-skrollex-section-55caa2c2d9be3056910152 pure-u-12-24 pure-u-md-6-24  col-padding circle-icon">
<p class="text-center"><i class="fa fa-file-text-o border-lite circle scroll-in-animation player" data-animation="fadeInUp"></i></p><p class="circle-caption">research</p>
<p class="text-center">Before development begins on a project, we work with some of the top researchers in the world to build a deep understanding of the flora and fauna involved. This can take six months or more, but it is absolutely necessary to plan and develop thoughtful and accurate simulations.</p>
</div> <div class="layers-widget-skrollex-section-55caa59f4525f360669205 pure-u-12-24 pure-u-md-6-24  col-padding circle-icon">
<p class="text-center"><i class="fa fa-handshake-o border-lite circle scroll-in-animation player" data-animation="fadeInUp"></i></p><p class="circle-caption">partnerships</p>
<p class="text-center">In addition to partnering with individual researchers, we also look for nonprofit organizations with a similar focus to partner with, such as wildlife conservation organizations like <a href="https://monarchjointventure.org/"><span>Monarch Joint Venture</span></a>. We value the feedback and support we receive from our partners and want our experiences to contribute to their work.</p>
</div> <div class="layers-widget-skrollex-section-55caa5b8021c8599175159 pure-u-12-24 pure-u-md-6-24  col-padding circle-icon">
<p class="text-center"><i class="fa fa-laptop border-lite circle scroll-in-animation player" data-animation="fadeInUp"></i></p><p class="circle-caption">technology</p>
<p class="text-center">We make use of a wide variety of technologies to develop our simulations, ranging from cutting-edge software and hardware to custom camera rigs to simulate some of the unique attributes of animal visual systems, like the monarch butterfly’s ability to perceive ultraviolet light.</p>
</div> <div class="layers-widget-skrollex-section-55caa5bcc8666509544289 pure-u-12-24 pure-u-md-6-24  col-padding circle-icon">
<p class="text-center"><i class="li_lab border-lite circle scroll-in-animation player" data-animation="fadeInUp"></i></p><p class="circle-caption">Experimentation</p>
<p class="text-center">Our development process involves a lot of testing and experimentation. While the core of our process is to simulate the lived experience of a particular animal as faithfully to the research as possible, the gameplay also needs to be intuitive, engaging and fun to capture the imagination of our players.</p>
</div> <div class="layers-widget-skrollex-section-61cc7c7151fa9664302932 pure-u-1 pure-u-lg-24-24  col-padding">
<h3 class="text-center">In the end, our work is only successful if the simulations result in players feeling more of a connection to nature</h3>

<p class="header-details">What is it like <span>to be a bat</span>?</p>
<p class="lead">This is the famous question posed by American philosopher Thomas Nagel regarding the limitations of consciousness and the mind-body problem: the relationship between thought and consciousness in the human mind, and the brain as part of the physical body.</p>
<p class="lead">MonarchVR and our other nature simulations explore this issue in an entirely unique way, combining academic research with virtual and augmented reality technologies to help players experience the world as other animals do.</p>
<p class="lead">Every animal possesses unique abilities and survival methodologies, such as the monarch butterfly’s ability to see ultraviolet light or a bat’s ability to hunt and navigate a twilight world using echolocation. Every animal also faces unique challenges, from the quotidian—daily searches for food and shelter—to the remarkable—continent-spanning migrations and life-or-death encounters with predators.</p>
<p class="lead"> Our simulations are designed to explore both, providing players with the highs and the lows of an animal’s experience, and highlighting the ways in which human activities can worsen the challenges they face through habitat destruction and climate change.</p>
<hr>
<p class="lead">We currently have one public prototype, <a href="https://www.naturesims.com/frogworld/">Frogworld</a>, and are in the process of creating our first free public demo, <a href="https://www.naturesims.com/monarchvr/">MonarchVR </a>featuring the Monarch butterfly.</p>
<p class="lead">We are eager for feedback from the VR gamer community, as well as educators and others who share an interest in our work. You can <a href="https://www.naturesims.com/betaapplication/">join our beta program for MonarchVR</a> or contact us below!</p>
