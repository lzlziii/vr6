It works by rendering the 3D scene an X percentage higher than the physical resolution of the display and then resizing/downsampling the image back to your display. This results in an expensive form of high quality anti-aliasing that delivers a cleaner looking rendered image.<br> <img src="https://cdn.akamai.steamstatic.com/steam/apps/500500/extras/supersampling_550.jpg?t=1469479011"><br>You can fine tune super sampling settings in real-time.<h2 class="bb_tag">Introducing Super Enhanced Mode</h2>Simple VR Video Player is excited to introduce Super Enhanced Mode! Sharpen your video and drastically improve image fidelity by enabling "Super Enhance" in the options. The Super Enhance technique utilizes an intelligent sharpening algorithm to enhance contrast and details while maintaining image quality.<br><br> <img src="https://cdn.akamai.steamstatic.com/steam/apps/500500/extras/super_enhanced_550.jpg?t=1469479011"><br><br>This feature when combined with high Super Sampling effectively upscales, sharpens, and downscales the video for greatly improved clarity especially on virtual reality headsets.<h2 class="bb_tag">Multiple Video Decoding Options</h2>Supports Microsoft Media Foundation, DirectShow, LibVLC, and PopMovie for multiple video decoding solutions.<h2 class="bb_tag">What Can It Play?</h2>Simple VR Video Player was built to take advantage of multiple video decoding engines in the backend to offer the greatest compatibility.  As a general rule of thumb, if your Windows Media Player can play the movie, so can Simple VR Video Player.<br><br>Media Foundation (Win 8/10) is a super optimized DX11 hardware accelerated decoder built into the latest versions of Windows with full h264 decoding support.<br><br>For more advanced users, you can change the video decoding engine to use another video backend such as DirectShow which allows you to use DirectShow decoders, filters, and splitters such as LAV and ffdshow.
