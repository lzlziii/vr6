<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">ABOUT THIS SOFTWARE&nbsp; &nbsp;Notice! This program is made for boaters - not gamers!</span><br><br><span style="font-weight: bold;">This program is an advanced radar simulator, mainly intended to be used in Screen Mode. The PC screen is divided into 3 windows. Radar window,&nbsp; Map window and a&nbsp; Visual window. The program is operated with a mouse in Screen Mode.</span><br><br><span style="font-weight: bold;">The radar simulates a rotating antenna that "scans" a 3-dimensional map (the surroundings you see in the Visual window), and creates a realistic radar image".</span><br><br><span style="font-weight: bold;">Then it is necessary to interpret what you see on the radar screen. Are there land, boats buoys or other objects shown? This interpretation comes with experience and this is what you can get by using this simulator.</span><br><br><span style="font-weight: bold;">Your position is shown on the Map window, and in the Visual window you see the surroundings. Compare this with what you see on the radar screen, and eventually you will interpret the radar image correctly and trust what you see there. Then you will feel safer sailing at night and in poor visibility.</span><br><br><span style="font-weight: bold;">With this simulator, you can also practice night sailing where you have to identify beacons and light buoys (characteristics and sectors) so that you can verify your position. You will also see the other boats' navigation lanterns.</span><br><br><span style="font-weight: bold;">This program is a useful aid for those who want to practice basic navigation, and for those who participate in courses to obtain a certificate for boats less than 24m (D5L).</span><br><br><span style="font-weight: bold;">VR Mode is an add-on for those who have a VR headset and would like to see how it works.
