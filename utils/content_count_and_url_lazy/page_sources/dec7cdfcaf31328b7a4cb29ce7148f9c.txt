                    <div class="features__description"><h2>GAME-WORLD &amp; ZONES</h2>
<ul class="bb_ul">
<li>Get transported into a seamless world comprised of rich, detailed environments.</li>
<li>Move between zones to discover new locations and find different places to bomb graffiti.</li>
<li>No loading or saving screens, everything streams in real-time, offering better immersion.</li>
<li>Multiple locations for doing graffiti in the same game level / world. You can paint on walls, buildings, tunnels, trains, containers and more.. and this is just the beginning!</li>
<li>Massive, long walls in some locations, provide ample room for those mega burners.</li>
<li>Realistic environments, with time of day, nature, animals, spatial sound FX and more.</li>
<li>Current game-world and zone mechanics have been designed with the longer-term features road-map in mind.</li>
</ul>
</div>

                <div class="features__gallery feature__gallery-1">
                    <div class="features__description"><h2>Realistic Paint System</h2>
<ul>
<li>Created by a graffiti artist, modeled from experience, the system looks and feels realistic.</li>
<li>Spray on walls and surfaces with different paints: FT-ACTION 71 (Regular / Multi-purpose), Slick Rick! (Gloss), Rack-a-lak (Matt), Zero2Hero (Metallic).</li>
<li>Areas are separated into Paint Zones that load and save automatically as you graffiti.</li>
<li>Check your Smart Watch to see Paint Zones status, discover new bombing locations.</li>
<li>Paint cans have lids and support different grab types for added realism.</li>
<li>Select different cap types, to utilize different spray, line and paint effects.</li>
<li>Intelligent drips system detects paint build-up and spawns realistic drips.</li>
<li>Undo / redo system to make sure those outlines and details are perfect.</li>
<li>Additional spray settings give you control over line and spray dynamics.</li>
</ul>
</div>
        </div>

<ul class="bb_ul">
<li>Actions Assistant is a tablet-like device that you use to manage various aspects and settings related to graffiti, locomotion, social, interactions and game settings.</li>
<li>The Graffiti Manager allows you to easily select color palettes and create quick color swatches using drag and drop.</li>
<li>Spray can Quick Actions menu system offers context access to various settings and panels, such as: Color Palette, Color Swatches, Caps Selector, Color Dropper, Spray Size Lock and more.</li>
<li>Use a Clipboard and related actions to import images from your local drive, you can use imported images for reference when doing pieces in-game.</li>
<li>Refer to your Smart Watch to get info on current health, paint zone and time of day.</li>
<li>Access the GRAF-CORP computer system in-game, to get help and play tips.</li>
<li>Choose from three different types of locomotion, depending on comfort, and preference.</li>
<li>Game and Quality settings give you fine control over setup, tweak things to your liking.</li>
</ul>
</div>
        </div>

                    <div class="features__description"><h2>Interactive Environments</h2>

<li>Use your backpack inventory system to store paints, clipboard and more.</li>
<li>Play music on your retro Boombox, so you can listen to tunes whilst bombing.</li>
<li>Rack up at the Bombastic – Graffiti Supplies vending machine, to top-up paint supplies.</li>
<li>Discover new ways to interact with the game-world and environments around you.</li>
<li>You can pick up and use objects, open doors, operate lifts and even move vehicles.</li>
<li>To get up to those hard to reach graffiti spots, you’ll need to climb or get creative.</li>
<li>Look for objects that you can stand on, or stack, ladders to climb and a mobile scissor lift.</li>
<li>Be careful though, some areas can be dangerous, and if you fall from a height it hurts!</li>
<li>Also be on the lookout for danger, security systems, and dogs… they do bite you know!!</li>
</ul>
