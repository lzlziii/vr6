<h1>The Story</h1><br>
<p>Please, take a seat. You’ll be here for a while. <br class="hide-for-xsmall" style="visibility: hidden;">Don’t worry, you won’t be alone. Let these creatures welcome you…</p>
<p>They’ll keep you company. Give you a taste of a dark surreal experience. <br class="hide-for-xsmall" style="visibility: hidden;">Don’t be afraid just yet… Perhaps they will spare you this time around. <br class="hide-for-xsmall" style="visibility: hidden;">After all… the nastiest thing they can do to you will only be virtual.</p>
<p>But… then again… You will probably be wishing you were in a real dream.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>DreadEye VR is a virtual reality experience that allows you to become an Indonesian shaman (dukun). <br class="hide-for-xsmall" style="visibility: hidden;">Craft and combine ingredients as you practice an ancient ritual, opening the gate to the spirit realm to commune with the dead. <br class="hide-for-xsmall" style="visibility: hidden;">Immerse yourself in terrifying experiences set in haunted rural Indonesia and discover the gruesome horrors lurking in the shadows!</strong></p>

<section id="info">
<img src="assets/dreadeye-vive-oculus.png" alt="DreadEye VR for HTC Vive and Oculus CV"><br><br>
<p>Design and developed from the ground for fully immersive VR experiences and gameplay.<br>
DreadEye VR is our attempt to make a unique approach to get a fully terrifying horror experience on VR.</p>

<p><span class="red">1. Follow instructions</span><br>
Consult the book for step-by-step methods to summon spirits.</p>
<p><span class="red">2. Cook ingredients</span><br>
Use the bowl to prepare ritual ingredients that require it.</p>
<p><span class="red">3. Reset when necessary</span><br>
If things get messy, use the hourglass to think it over and start again from scratch.</p>
<p><span class="red">4. Summon the ghost</span><br>
Complete the ritual and call upon the restless dead.</p>
<p><span class="red">5. Find the ghost</span><br>
Use your flashlight to illuminate the ghost.</p>
<p><span class="red">6. Commune with the dead</span><br>
Enter the spiritual world and endure the horror.</p>

<br>
<p><span class="red">Combine, Craft, and Commune</span><br>
Combine ingredients to commune with the dead.</p>

Overcome numerous puzzles in a unique Indonesian rural setting.</p>
<p><span class="red">Battle Spirits</span><br>
