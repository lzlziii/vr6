class="wp-block-heading"><strong>HitMotion: Reloaded Privacy Policy</strong>&nbsp;</h2><p><em><sup>Updated on 2021.07.24</sup></em></p><hr class="wp-block-separator"><h2 class="wp-block-heading">Who we are</h2><p>We are New Technology Walkers, a group of VR professionals that love to create VR experiences. The team is headed by Massimiliano Ariani and Antony Vitillo but includes many other freelancers and small companies (like MID). We create games like the one that you are playing, HitMotion: Reloaded, that is aimed to let you stay fit while having fun.</p><p>Privacy is an important pillar of our offering: we engage daily with the VR community and we know how trust is an important value for all VR people, so we have built our game with transparency about data management in mind. This privacy policy details how we handle your data when you play HitMotion: Reloaded.</p><p>If anything in this document is not clear to you or you think that we are making some errors in the management of your personal data, please feel free to contact us at info@ntwalkers.it.</p><hr class="wp-block-separator"><h2 class="wp-block-heading">What personal data we collect and why we collect it</h2><p>HitMotion: Reloaded is a single-player game. Since at the moment the game doesn’t require an account and doesn’t offer multiplayer features, we have been able to develop it so that almost all the data about the game just stay on the device and is never transmitted outside.&nbsp;</p><p>Your only personal data we may collect is related to:</p><ul><li>Game progresses</li><li>Game preferences</li><li>Game analytics</li></ul><p>These items are explained in detail here below.</p><h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Game Progresses</strong></h3><p>While you proceed in the game, the system may record information about your progress into the game like for instance:</p><ul><li>Highscores</li><li>Stars unlocked in a level</li><li>Achievements unlocked</li><li>Dailies won</li></ul><p>and information about how you performed in the game from a fitness standpoint, that is, for instance:</p><ul><li>Number of calories burn</li><li>Number and types of punches given</li><li>Number of dodges performed</li></ul><p>This data is necessary to provide you a satisfying user experience, with features like gamification that are proven to be effective to let people be motivated in playing a game. Since ours is a game about fitness, we believe that these tactics may entice you to return playing HitMotion, so that you can stay fit every day, with a noticeable benefit for your health.</p><p>We just store all this data locally on your device, because there is no need to transmit it outside. Your data is never communicated to us, and you have full control over it.</p><h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Game Preferences</strong></h3><p>For the sake of offering a more coherent user experience, we also save locally some of your choices about how you do prefer playing the game, like for instance:</p><ul><li>What is your preferred language</li><li>What has been your choice for what concerns the game analytics</li><li>The position and the orientation of your play space area</li><li>What are the modifiers you have selected for each level</li></ul><p>This data too is stored only locally on your device and never leaves it. It is used so that you can have a more pleasant user experience: for instance, saving the preferred language lets you select your own native language only once, without the need of selecting it every time you start the game.</p><h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Game Analytics</strong></h3><p>Analytics is the only data of yours that may be sent to the cloud. The game may record information about your game progress and send it to our Unity Analytics account to let us understand how to improve the game in the future.</p><p>The data that is sent to Unity Analytics includes:</p><ul><li>The levels you have played and their outcome (win/lose)</li><li>The score you are reaching for each level</li><li>The punches you are giving</li><li>The enemies you are destroying</li><li>The enemies you are dodging</li></ul><p>This data is very useful for us, which are a little game studio, to analyze the general performances of the players and be able to adjust the game accordingly. So, for instance, if we notice that no player wins Level 4, we understand that we made it too difficult, and we can publish an update making it easier to win. Feedback is very important for us, and this data makes us improve our game for all the VR community.</p><p>Since privacy is overly important for us at New Technology Walkers, we want to underline about our analytics data collection that:</p><ul><li>We are relying on a very famous and trustworthy service like Unity Analytics</li><li>The data we can access is completely anonymized, and we can only see the aggregated values</li><li>We only collect the data we need and in no way this includes sensitive data about yourself</li><li>We use your data only to understand how to improve the game and for no other purpose</li><li>You can opt-out very easily from it.
