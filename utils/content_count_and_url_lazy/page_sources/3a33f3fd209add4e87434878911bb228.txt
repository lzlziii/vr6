	  <div id="Text" class="content">
		   <p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:18px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px">Horrors of Space is a 4-player co-op survival horror game. You and 3 other crew members awaken from cryo sleep to find the Aries in a state of emergency, invaded by an alien creature. It’s up to you and your teammates to work together to get out alive.</p>
			<img src="images/HoS_Still_08.jpg" width="100%" height="auto" alt="">
		<p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:18px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px"> Your goal is to safely get off the spaceship without falling prey to the AI controlled alien creature, which has dynamic behaviors and reactions to its surroundings.</p>
			<p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:18px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px"><strong>Your escape plan:</strong> search the ship for the key cards you need to access the locked areas and then hack the workstations to reroute power to the escape pods. On top of that, the ship has been damaged so you might have to make unexpected repairs at any moment.</p>
<p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:18px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px">There is more than one way of hacking the workstations: You can brute-force your way in and hack them or you can explore the ship to find the passwords.</p>
<p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:18px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px">Each crew member can be customized and has one item that only they can use, so choose wisely and combine your strengths to survive.</p>
<p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:20px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px"><strong>Key features</strong> 

    </p><blockquote>
      <p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:18px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px">- Over 800,000 square feet of explorable spaceship.</p>
      <p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:18px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px">- AI controlled alien creature with dynamic behaviors and reactions.</p>
      <p style="font-family:Coda;text-align:left;font-size:18px;color:aliceblue;text-indent:0px">- Every game is unique: passwords, clues and items are generated randomly at the beginning of every new game.</p>
    </blockquote>
