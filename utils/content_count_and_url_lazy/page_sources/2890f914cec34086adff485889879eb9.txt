                                <p>A unique twist on survival by combining action, adventure, and strategy in virtual reality. Apocalyptic Adventure is a game where players must manage resources, plan defenses, and explore to survive.</p>
                                <p>Episodes will challenge the player to overcome hordes of enemies using the environment to their advantage. Specifically crafted for room-scale and teleportation. Episode 1 includes all the game's core mechanics listed below.</p>

                                <h4>Key Features:</h4>
                                <h5>Defense:</h5>
                                <p>Planning and strategizing a defense is crucial to surviving. Barricade yourself in and place traps as the last line of defense between you and the enemy.</p>
                                <h5>Crafting:</h5>
                                <p>As you scavenge through the apocalyptic world you will acquire unique items. These items can be crafted together to create weapons, tools, traps, and more.</p>
                                <h5>Combat:</h5>
                                <p>Combat choices include firearms, melee weapons, throwing explosives, and traps. Be aware, with limited resources you will resort to anything around you to get the job done!</p>
                                <h5>Adventure:</h5>
                                <p>Each episode is set in a unique location. The first is a mountainous region centered around a water mill. The only way to survive is to figure out how to get off the mountain.</p>
                                <h5>Movement:</h5>
                                <p>Designed specifically for room scale and teleportation for a comfortable game experience.</p>
