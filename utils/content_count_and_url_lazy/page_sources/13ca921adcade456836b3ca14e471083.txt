  </div>
  
  <div class="node__content clearfix">
    
            <div property="schema:text" class="field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><p><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">This privacy policy governs your use of the software application SINGULIVE. This privacy policy was last updated on October 4, 2022. Our privacy policy may change from time to time for any reason. If we make any material changes to our policies, we will place a prominent notice on our website or application. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policies, feel free to contact us at any time at info@singulive.com</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<h2 style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 27px; margin-bottom: 8px;"><span style="font-size:20pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">What information does SINGULIVE obtain and how is it used?</span></span></span></span></span></span></h2>

<p style="line-height:1.38"><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">SINGULIVE requires a user account in order to access the platform. The data required to create the account is the Oculus ID, device ID, an email and a password. The purpose of storing this information is to be able to verify users to later facilitate cross-player multiplayer functionalitites between other platforms, such as smarthones or tablets. Singulive has also builtin social features so users can follow other users in order to acces multiplayer funcionatlities together. </span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">To improve the quality of Singulive, both venues and concerts record the number of visits they receive (anonymously).</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">In future updates, Singulive will store the history of concerts that users have attended with the aim of both offering the user's own history and offering personalized recommendations based on their interests.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">However, before implementing said updates, users will receive a notification with the information relevant to the processing of user data.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<h2 style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 27px; margin-bottom: 8px;"><span style="font-size:20pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">How do you handle location data?</span></span></span></span></span></span></h2>

<p style="line-height:1.38"><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">SINGULIVE does not use or collect any location data related to your geographic location.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<h2 style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 27px; margin-bottom: 8px;"><span style="font-size:20pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">Do you share personal information?</span></span></span></span></span></span></h2>

<p style="line-height:1.38"><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">We do not share personal information with anyone.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<h2 style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 27px; margin-bottom: 8px;"><span style="font-size:20pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">Do advertising companies collect data?</span></span></span></span></span></span></h2>

<p style="line-height:1.38"><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">Singulive SL does not share any data with advertising companies.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<h2 style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 27px; margin-bottom: 8px;"><span style="font-size:20pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">Do you use vendors or analytics providers?</span></span></span></span></span></span></h2>

<p style="line-height:1.38"><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">No data is shared with vendors or analytics providers.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<h2 style="line-height: 1.38; margin-top: 27px; margin-bottom: 8px;"><span style="font-size:20pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">Do you comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)?</span></span></span></span></span></span></h2>

<p style="line-height:1.38"><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">Yes. We do not solicit nor gather any data from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at info@singulive.com.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p style="line-height:1.38"><span style="font-size:20pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">How can I request to delete my user data on the platform?</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p style="line-height:1.38"><span style="font-size:11pt; font-variant:normal; white-space:pre-wrap"><span style="font-family:Arial"><span style="color:#000000"><span style="font-weight:400"><span style="font-style:normal"><span style="text-decoration:none">If you wish to request that we delete your user data in Singulive, contact us at info@singulive.com indicating your username and email address used in Singulive.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
