<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
<p>I, Jarosław Ciupiński of void room (full company name void room Jarosław Ciupiński), the developer of Tea For God respect Your privacy. The purpose of this page is to outline our privacy policy, what data and why do I collect and what are Your rights. By choosing to use this Service, You agree with the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.</p>
<h2>Information Collection and Use</h2>
<p>Tea For God collects information only in case of a crash. The information is collected to improve the game.</p>
<p>This data may include basic information about the device and installed OS, size of Your play area, settings, including setup height and other information that might be required to reproduce the reported bug.</p>
<p>The collected data is not shared with any third parties.</p>
<p>If any other information is required, this shall happen by other platforms and you will be notified of the purposes for which information is being collected.</p>
<h2>Your Rights to Your Personal Data</h2>
<p>Your rights provided under the GDPR allow you to access, rectify and erase Your Personal Data. In case of inquiry, please contact me by email: contact@voidroom.com.</p>
<h2>Security</h2>
<p>I strive to protect any collected information. Note, that no method of transmission or electronic storage guarantees 100% security.</p>
<h2>Children’s Privacy</h2>
<p>Tea For God is not addressed to anyone under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under the age of 13. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Personal Data, please contact me.</p>
<h2>Contact Us</h2>
<p>If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, You can contact me by email: contact@voidroom.com</p>
