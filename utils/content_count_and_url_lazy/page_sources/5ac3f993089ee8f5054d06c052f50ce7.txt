<p>Absolute Territory includes an in-game Level Editor for creating&nbsp;<em>custom missions</em>. Challenge your piloting skills, develop unique scenarios you only previously imagined. You decide the challenge by adjusting the environment and opponents. Fight with the most devastating weapons against lighter opponents or truly test your skills against-all-odds.</p>
