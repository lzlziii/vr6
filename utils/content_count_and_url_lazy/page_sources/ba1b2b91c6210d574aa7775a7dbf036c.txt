		  					<div class="row row-parent style-dark limit-width double-top-padding double-bottom-padding">
									<h2>Our upcoming game Cave Digger 2: Dig Harder is going to be on Steam Next Fest.</h2>
<p>VR players can get a taste of its core mechanics and alternative, weird west dieselpunk world for the first time, with the option of a cross-platform co-op multiplayer. The demo is downloadable on the game’s <a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/1523510/Cave_Digger_2_Dig_Harder/">Steam page</a> throughout the event, 16th – 22nd of June.</p>
<p><iframe title="&quot;Cave Digger 2: Dig Harder&quot; Demo Game Play Trailer | Steam Next Fest 2021" width="840" height="473" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/3vns7b6s6UU?feature=oembed&amp;width=840&amp;height=1000&amp;discover=1&amp;enablejsapi=1&amp;origin=https://vrkiwi.org" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen="" data-lf-form-tracking-inspected-kn9eq4rdo1j8rlvp="true" data-lf-yt-playback-inspected-kn9eq4rdo1j8rlvp="true" data-lf-vimeo-playback-inspected-kn9eq4rdo1j8rlvp="true"></iframe></p>
<h2></h2>
<h2>Key Features in Cave Digger 2: Dig Harder</h2>
<ul>
<li>Mining, looting, capitalizing</li>
<li>Exploration in procedurally generated levels</li>
<li>Close-quarters combat</li>
<li>Cross-platform co-op multiplayer</li>
<li>Character customization</li>
<li>Tool and weapon upgrades (not in the Steam Next Fest build)</li>
<li>An unraveling story in a form of collectibles (not in the Steam Next Fest build)</li>
</ul>
<p><img decoding="async" fetchpriority="high" class="alignnone wp-image-10328 size-full" src="https://vrkiwi.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/imagearticle_1_cavedigger2.png" alt="" width="1920" height="1080"></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Cave Digger 2: Dig Harder is the sequel to Cave Digger VR released on Steam, PSVR, and Oculus Quest back in 2018-19. The sequel is the biggest VR game production from Finland to date and is planned for an early access release on Steam in the 3rd quarter this year.</p>
<p>“We have worked hard to present you a taste of what is coming later in the year. Now you can form your squad, or go solo, to face the challenges of the first biome in the game. Be among the first to play Cave Digger 2”, says Jani Kaipainen, the Head of VR Publishing of VRKiwi.</p>
<p>“Also, here’s a useful tip for those who haven’t played Cave Digger 1. To celebrate Cave Digger 2, we will run gigantic discount sales on Cave Digger 1 in major game stores this summer. If you’d like to support us, probably the best way would be to spread the word about us”, concludes Jani Kaipainen.</p>
<p>During the fest, the game will be streamed on Steam and on the publisher’s Twitch channel at <a href="https://twitch.tv/vrkiwigaming">twitch.tv/vrkiwigaming</a>. Cave Digger Discord channel is at <a href="https://discord.gg/cavedigger">discord.gg/cavedigger</a> and to install Cave Digger 2 Steam Next Fest build, go to its Steam page <a href="https://bit.ly/3chIMtP">here</a>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>About Cave Digger 2: Dig Harder</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><img decoding="async" class="aligncenter wp-image-10326 size-full" src="https://vrkiwi.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/CD2-logo-v6.png" alt="" width="448" height="200"></p>
<p>Cave Digger 2: Dig Harder is a VR single/multiplayer co-op adventure game taking place in an alternative, weird west dieselpunk world. The player is a penniless but adventurous prospector in the wild northern region known as “The New Frontier”. As the player excavates the mountains of their claim, they encounter many mysteries along the way, and only two things remain constant: the North is cold and the Frontier guards its secrets well.</p>
<h3>About VRKiwi</h3>
<p>VRKiwi is the game publisher brand of MeKiwi, a full-stack digital agency based in Oulu, Finland. Their first game Cave Digger VR released on Steam back in 2018, followed by multiple other VR and flat-screen platforms. Their second game ArtPulse came in 2020, and there are more coming through their publishing pipeline, namely Crystal Raiders VR, Cave Digger 2: Dig Harder (VR), and more. VRKiwi website is vrkiwi.org, and MeKiwi mekiwi.org.</p>
