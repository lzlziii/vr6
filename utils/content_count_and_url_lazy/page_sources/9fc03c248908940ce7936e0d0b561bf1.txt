<p>
Potioneer: a virtual reality game where players explore the wilderness in search of unique plants, hoping to bring them home to grow and fashion them into powerful potions. Using the 3d visuals and room-scale tracking that HTC Vive provides, players explore the wilderness looking for plants and craft their home using wood, hammers, and nails. In their workshop, players can use their mortar and pestle to crush plant seeds down to dust which will then be used to make potions!
</p>

<p>
Released onto Windows PCs via <a href="http://store.steampowered.com/app/544410" class="urlextern" title="http://store.steampowered.com/app/544410" rel="ugc nofollow">Steam Early Access</a>, <a href="https://focusonfungames.itch.io/potioneer-the-vr-gardening-simulator" class="urlextern" title="https://focusonfungames.itch.io/potioneer-the-vr-gardening-simulator" rel="ugc nofollow">itch.io</a>, and <a href="https://www.viveport.com/apps/cedd87ec-ead0-4e27-9008-710d33af7300" class="urlextern" title="https://www.viveport.com/apps/cedd87ec-ead0-4e27-9008-710d33af7300" rel="ugc nofollow">Viveport</a> on October 20, 2016, the game has seen many updates where new game features, plants/wildlife, and areas to explore are added.
</p>
