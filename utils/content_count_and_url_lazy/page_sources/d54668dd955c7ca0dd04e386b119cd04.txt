								<div id="devnotes_expander">
																			<h1>为何要采用抢先体验这种模式？</h1>
										“NeosVR is a project with high ambitions and currently being developed by a single developer with the help of a business partner to handle the runnings of our startup and all the things around it.<br>
<br>
As such, many of its features aren’t yet fully developed, others are still in prototyping stage and difficult to use and many of its subsystems aren’t yet accessible at all. A good chunk of the time is currently being devoted to creating some content for the system as well.”

										<h1>这款游戏的抢先体验状态大约持续多久？</h1>
										“We expect the project to continue maturing within the following year, especially with the help of the community. As we continue to work on it, it'll gradually keep becoming more stable and well rounded, usable for more and more applications.<br>
<br>
Once we feel that a newcomers don't need the "This might have some basic usability issues that I need to be aware of" mentality when working with Neos, we'll put it out of the Early Access. However  we expect this to be a very gradual process.”

										<h1>计划中的完整版本和抢先体验版本到底有多少不同？</h1>
										“While Neos doesn't have a well defined "Full Version", as there will always be more features, content and systems to add, there are several major features that will be added to the system.<br>
<br>
The primary one is powerful dynamic loading and synchronization of assemblies, instead of preloading them explicitly as mods. However due to security reasons, we have postponed this feature until we integrate Chromium’s sandbox.<br>
<br>
We're also working on many other subsystems that will make Neos usable as a VR art and music studio, such as editing audio, animation, integration with more hardware, as well as interfacing with external languages, scripts and devices to widen the possible applications of the system.<br>
<br>
There will also be many more performance and bandwidth optimizations and extensions to Neos' novel data model, which will make it easier to build a wider variety of networking applications with ease, increase their potential complexity and run them on wider variety of hardware.”

										<h1>抢先体验版本的现状如何？</h1>
										“Neos is already a very powerful system, allowing users to build, edit and experience in implicit multiplayer VR with a wide range of features (see the description section) that make it applicable to many scenarios, from entertainment to professional use.<br>
<br>
The core has also been stabilized over the years, being able to run for hours without a crashes or synchronization errors, with only a few rare bugs remaining to hunt down.”

										<h1>在抢先体验期间和结束之后，游戏价格会有所不同吗？</h1>
										“Neos itself will remain free for non-commercial use in the future, however the pricing of the extra cloud services (like storage, networking or data processing) will be subject to change, as we figure out the usage patterns and how much these services cost on our end.”

										<h1>在开发过程中，你们是如何计划让玩家社区参与进来的？</h1>
										“Community involvement is crucial to Neos. While we try to provide a variety of features and content ourselves, it is ultimately made to enable you to build new VR worlds, virtual tools and applications and share them with other users.<br>
<br>
Whether you are an artist, casual user, scientist, hobbyist builder or seasoned developer, your experience with the system and feedback will help us shape its features, improve ease of use and set priorities for further development.<br>
<br>
We want to support and feature your creations and contributions to Neos and use whatever resources we'll have at our disposal to reward the best and most promising creators among you.”

					如果你有一套VR那么这个游戏真的很好玩，虽然没有VR也能启动，但是没有VR设备的话是没有R键的，导致不能做一些操作，比如自身的变大变小，飞行，奔跑，物品操控也不会很方便，好了，我说一下这个游戏的优点和缺点吧。<br><br>优点：<br>1.这个游戏有着其他VR游戏没有的创造性，可以编辑建造自己的世界，甚至一些物品，比如在游戏内就可以创建一个剑一样的模型出来，然后我可以给这个模型加上材质光效以及一些特效，让这个剑看起来更加的帅气，之后我可以给这个剑加上功能，让这个剑可以御剑飞行满足你的仙侠梦。<br>2.游戏的社交性，游戏全程联网，可以和世界玩家一起交流。（有几个世界房间玩家竟然用来跳早操和下午操，大屏幕播放着健身视频，玩家模仿着一起跳操，游戏有全身追踪真的很强）<br>3.游戏有一定的汉化，虽然没有做到全部，但是汉化的部分已经够一些基础的游玩了。<br>4.游戏内可以调用外部的其他功能，我是不会外语的，日本玩家那里调用了Google翻译在游戏内制作了语音翻译器，有些玩家在自己的世界做了屏幕或者音乐播放器，播放着喜欢的视频和音乐。<br>5.游戏也有很强的娱乐性，玩家定时开放的游戏厅，在VR游戏里和好多人一起玩水果机，21点（当然姓赶河官也是玩家）。或者大家一起排排坐在木筏上钓鱼，以及滑雪等其他游戏。<br><br>缺点：<br>1.游戏网络连接不佳的问题导致加载房间会需要一段时间，我很少见到国内玩家不知道和国内玩家对话时怎样，但是和国外玩家对话会有一卡—卡的情况，有的时候也会掉线，并且我现在没有找到这个游戏的加速器。<br>2.游戏社交性是可以的，但是现在游戏内很少见到说汉语的玩家，以及玩家数量的稀少，导致交流起来有一定困难，虽然游戏内有语音翻译器，但是因为调用的是谷歌翻译，导致我这里使用不了。<br>3.汉化并不全面，只是主要菜单汉化了，创造工具还不是汉语内置输入法还不能输入汉字，不过内置输入法如果有大佬是可以在游戏内创造一个汉语输入法出来的，现在汉语只能在空中用笔写字了。<br>4.创造性在这个游戏内很突出，但是需要一定门槛，说白了我不会，游戏创造的界面大多数为英语，这是一方面。另一方面是，要做出酷炫的东西需要具备一定的逻辑知识。<br><br>总结一下，游戏真的不错，如果有更多的国内玩家加入进来，游戏的好多缺点会逐渐消失，如果看到这个评论的玩家想来试试，也可以一起交流。					<div class="gradient"></div>
