				<h2 class="entry-title">Buggy Blasters – HoloLens Prototype Download and Manual</h2>
				<div class="entry-meta">
					<time class="published" datetime="2017-12-11" pubdate="">December 11, 2017</time>, written by <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="https://previewlabs.com/author/kasper/" title="View all posts by Kasper Geeroms">Kasper Geeroms</a></span>
				</div>
				<div class="entry-content">
					<p><a href="https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/buggyBlasters_logo_version2_black_1x.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-4982];player=img;"><img decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5002" src="https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/buggyBlasters_logo_version2_black_1x.png" alt="" width="878" height="292" srcset="https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/buggyBlasters_logo_version2_black_1x.png 878w, https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/buggyBlasters_logo_version2_black_1x-300x100.png 300w, https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/buggyBlasters_logo_version2_black_1x-400x133.png 400w, https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/buggyBlasters_logo_version2_black_1x-300x100@2x.png 600w, https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/buggyBlasters_logo_version2_black_1x-400x133@2x.png 800w" sizes="(max-width: 878px) 100vw, 878px"></a></p>
<p>One of the prototypes we’ve been working on for a while now can finally be revealed!</p>
<p>We developed the Buggy Blasters concept on request by our parent company <a href="https://cronos-groep.be/en/">Cronos Groep</a>, who gave us carte blanche to come up with an interesting case for the HoloLens.</p>
<p>This is the premise of the prototype:</p>
<ul>
<li>In Buggy Blasters, players battle each other with remote-controlled buggies in the first online multiplayer game on HoloLens!</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Control your squad of buggies to capture the enemy flag, but be sure to retreat in time to heal. Use that one muscular Air tap trained finger or an Xbox One S controller, whichever you prefer.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Employ crazy tactical skills, lightning speed and efficient buggy management to crush your opponent, who’ll give you that ‘sad Robocop’ look.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>No friends with HoloLenses? That’s all right, because there’s a sandbox-style single player mode to lazily drive around in and practice target shooting.</li>
</ul>
<h5><strong>Download</strong></h5>
<p>&nbsp;<br>
Buggy Blasters is available for download on the Microsoft Store. </p>
<p><a class="button" href="https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/store/p/buggy-blasters/9p2h9srj1dmc?rtc=1">Get Buggy Blasters</a></p>
<p>Alternatively, it can be downloaded and installed manually (download <a href="https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/buggyBlasters_iteration1_2017_12_10.zip">here</a>, and installation using <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/hololens/hololens-install-apps">these instructions</a>).</p>
<h5><strong>Using the Xbox One S Controller</strong></h5>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>Make sure Buggy Blasters is not running. Shut down every instance of the app by removing all Buggy Blasters windows in the HoloLens main menu.</li>
<li>Bring up the menu with your hand (bloom) and select (air tap) the Settings button. Place the Settings window, and select ‘Devices’</li>
<li>Turn on the Xbox One S controller and press the Bluetooth pairing button (see below) until the Xbox logo on the controller starts blinking quickly.</li>
<li>The controller will come up on the HoloLens’s devices list. Select ‘Pair’.</li>
<li>Note that if you connected a paired controller to another device after playing, you will need to remove the controller from the HoloLens device list and re-pair it to use it again for Buggy Blasters.</li>
</ol>
<p><a href="https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/xbox_one_s_bluetooth_pairing.png" rel="shadowbox[sbpost-4982];player=img;"><img decoding="async" loading="lazy" class="wp-image-5009 size-medium aligncenter" src="https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/xbox_one_s_bluetooth_pairing-300x169.png" alt="" width="300" height="169" srcset="https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/xbox_one_s_bluetooth_pairing-300x169.png 300w, https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/xbox_one_s_bluetooth_pairing-400x225.png 400w, https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/xbox_one_s_bluetooth_pairing.png 755w, https://previewlabs.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/10/xbox_one_s_bluetooth_pairing-300x169@2x.png 600w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px"></a></p>
