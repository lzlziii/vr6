					<h4>Last updated: May 17, 2018</h4>
<p class="fs18 blklvel3">The Scope AR Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices of Scope AR’s apps and websites. Scope Technologies US Inc. is a United States company, and the laws of California and the laws of the United States apply.</p>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">Please note that in order to use our apps and websites, you authorize Scope AR to transfer your personal information across national borders to Canada, where Scope AR also operates. We will only transfer your personal information to these countries where permitted to do so by law and we will take steps intended to ensure that your personal information continues to receive appropriate protections.</p>

<h4>What does this privacy policy cover?</h4>
<p class="fs18 blklvel3">This privacy policy describes the privacy practices of Scope AR’s websites, as well as our desktop apps and mobile apps that include a link to this policy (in this policy, we refer to our desktop apps and mobile apps together as “apps”). Note that “websites” include web-based services such as the Remote AR and WorkLink CMS. This privacy policy also applies to Scope AR’s marketing and advertising practices. Please also see any additional Terms of Use or Product License Agreements that may apply to the app or website you are using. This policy does not cover the processing of personal information where we are acting as a data processor for one of our customers; in that case our customer will be the data controller.</p>

<h4>What information does Scope AR collect about me?</h4>
<h5>Scope AR ID, registration, and customer support</h5>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">When you register to use a Scope AR app or website, create a Scope AR user ID, or contact us for support or other offerings, Scope AR collects information that identifies you. This includes information such as your name, company name, and email address. We may also sometimes collect other information that does not identify you, such as which types of apps you’re interested in. Some of this information is required and other information is optional. To help keep our databases current and to provide you the most relevant content and experiences, we may combine information provided by you with information from other sources, in accordance with applicable law. For example, from these sources, we may learn about the size, industry, and other information about the company you work for.</p>

<h4>Scope AR apps and websites</h4>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">We collect information about how you use our apps and websites. We may collect information sent by your browser or device to our servers whenever you visit a Scope AR website or use a Scope AR app. For example, your browser or device may tell us your IP address (which may tell us generally where you are located) and the type of browser and device you used. When you visit a Scope AR website, your browser may also tell us information such as the page that led you to our website and, if applicable, the search terms you typed into a search engine that led you to our website. Scope AR may collect information about how you use our websites and apps by using cookies and similar technologies, and our servers may collect similar information when you are logged in to the app or website. Depending on the app or website, this information may be anonymous (for example, see the ScopeAR.com) or it may be associated with you (for example, the Remote AR CMS).If you use the service to make a recording, that recording may include personal information such as images of you. Also, we use automated systems to analyze your content using techniques such as machine learning in order to improve our apps and websites. This analysis may occur as the content is sent or received using an online feature of a Scope AR app or website, or when the content is stored on Scope AR servers. During app use, the CMS gathers information such as physical location, screenshots, videos, notes, checklists, annotations, call data, and crash reports.</p>

<h4>Scope AR app activation and automatic updates</h4>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">When you activate your Scope AR app or when you install updates to the app, we collect certain information about your device (including your IP address), the Scope AR app, and your product serial number (learn more). Some of our apps include features that connect to our servers and offer you the ability to install updates to the app. We may keep track of information such as whether the update was successful or not. We may use the information collected through activation or app updates to validate your copy of the app and confirm that it is genuine and properly licensed.</p>

<h4>Scope AR emails</h4>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">Emails we send you may include a technology (called a web beacon) that tells Scope AR whether you have received or opened the email, or clicked a link in the email. If you do not want us to collect this information from Scope AR marketing emails, you can opt out of receiving Scope AR marketing emails.</p>

Buttons, tools, and content from other companies

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">Scope AR apps and websites may include buttons, tools, or content that link to other companies’ services (for example, a Facebook “Like” button). We may collect information about your use of these features. In addition, when you see or interact with these buttons, tools, or content, or view a Scope AR web page or app containing them, some information from your browser may automatically be sent to the other company. Please read that company’s privacy policy for more information.</p>

<h4>Scope AR social networking pages and social sign-on services</h4>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">Scope AR has its own pages on many social networking sites (for example, the Scope AR® team’s Facebook page). We may collect information when you interact with our social networking pages.</p>

<h4>How does Scope AR use the information it collects about me?</h4>
<p class="fs18 blklvel3">We will only use your personal information when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:
</p><ul>
<li>Where we need to perform the contract we are about to enter into or have entered into with you.</li>
<li>Where it is necessary for our legitimate interests (or those of a third party) and your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.</li>
<li>Where we need to comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.</li>
</ul>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">See below to find out more about the types of lawful basis that we will rely on to process your personal data.</p>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">Generally we do not rely on consent as a legal basis for processing your personal information other than in relation to sending third party direct marketing communications to you via email or text message. You have the right to withdraw consent to marketing at any time by contacting us using the contact details set out below</p>

<h4>What are cookies and how does Scope AR use them?</h4>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">As with most websites, Scope AR uses cookies and similar technologies to make our websites work as well as to learn more about our users and their likely interests.</p>
 
<h4>Does Scope AR share my personal information?</h4>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">Scope AR works with companies that help us run our business. These companies provide services such as delivering customer support and sending emails on our behalf. In some cases, these companies have access to some of your personal information in order to provide services to you on our behalf. They are not permitted to use your information for their own purposes.</p>

<h4>Scope AR may also share your personal information:</h4>

<ul>
<li>When you agreed to the sharing</li>
<li>Within the Scope AR family of companies for purposes described in this privacy policy (Scope Technologies US Inc. and Scope Technologies )</li>
<li>With our resellers and other sales partners if you are a business customer and agreed to receive Scope AR marketing communications. Note that we do not share personal information about consumers with third parties for their own marketing purposes unless the consumer agreed to that sharing</li>
<li>When we are required to provide information in response to a subpoena, court order, or other applicable law or legal process</li>
<li>When we have a good faith belief that the disclosure is necessary to prevent or respond to fraud, defend our apps or websites against attacks, or protect the property and safety of Scope AR, our customers and users, or the public</li>
<li>If we merge with or are acquired by another company, sell a Scope AR website, app, or business unit, or if all or a substantial portion of our assets are acquired by another In those cases your information will likely be one of the assets that is transferred</li>

<h4>Is my personal information displayed anywhere on Scope AR’s websites or applications?</h4>

<p class="fs18 blklvel3">There are several places within Scope AR’s websites and apps that allow you to submit content for others to see. <p class="fs18 blklvel3">Please be careful when you share your personal information. Do not share anything you wouldn’t want publicly known unless you are sure you are posting it within an app or website that allows you to control who sees what you post.</p>
