<p>Last updated: July 24, 2022</p>

<p>This privacy policy describes our policies and procedures on collecting, using, and disclosing your information when you use Stunt Track Builder, our Oculus Software Application. If you have any suggestions, questions or concerns related to our privacy policies, please get in touch with us at: <a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection">trackbuildervr@gmail.com</a>.</p>

<h1 id="what-information-is-obtained-and-how-is-it-used">What information is obtained, and how is it used?</h1>

<p>Stunt Tracks Builder does not collect, store, use or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, address, location data related to your geographic location, phone number, email address or Oculus ID. We also do comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not solicit nor gather any data from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with information without their consent, they should contact us at <a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection">trackbuildervr@gmail.com</a>.</p>

<h1 id="share-of-personal-information">Share of personal information</h1>

<p>We do not collect, use, transmit, or maintain any personal information, therefore, we do not share personal information with anyone. We also do not share any data with advertising agencies/companies or any other companies. Also, Stunt Tracks Builder confirms that no data is shared with analytics providers or vendors and any other third parties.</p>

<h1 id="third-party-service-that-may-collect-information-used-to-identify-you">Third party service that may collect information used to identify you</h1>

<p>Stunt track builder is created using Unity engine and is running on Oculus Platform and use Oculus service. As such, Unity and Oculus/Meta themselves mights collect data from you.</p>

<p>Unity privacy policy: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy</p>

<p>Oculus privacy policy: https://www.oculus.com/legal/privacy-policy/</p>

<h1 id="how-can-you-delete-your-data">How can you delete your data</h1>

<p>We do not store your data. There’s nothing to delete. However, if you suspect your data has been wrongfuly collected or you wish to delete your data from our third party service such as Unity and/or Oculus, you can contact us by sending an email to <a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection">trackbuildervr@gmail.com</a> and we can work this out.</p>

<h1 id="privacy-policy-updates-notification">Privacy Policy Updates Notification</h1>
<p>This privacy policy may change for any reason from time to time in the future. If we make changes to this privacy policy that may impact you, we will notify you of such changes as appropriate by providing information through updating this document. In this case, we will change the “Last Updated” date at the top of this privacy policy, a prominent notice on our website or application will also appear</p>
