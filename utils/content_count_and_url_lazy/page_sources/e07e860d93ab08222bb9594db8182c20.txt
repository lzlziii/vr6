<div class="sqs-html-content">
  <h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>What stage of development is the game currently in?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Helios is currently in the Early Access stage and is available for purchase on the <a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/1047640/Helios/">Steam store</a>. <br><br>The game is not finished by a long shot but we have decided that the game cannot progress further without a larger community behind it than we have been able to attract with a Closed Alpha.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Why are you using Unreal Engine?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Unreal Engine will allow Helios to provide a more immersive, interactive, and creator friendly offering than anything currently on the market today.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Do you have dedicated servers?</strong> </h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">We have two dedicated servers (US and EU) but players are also able to host sessions themselves which their friends can join. We plan to offer more dedicated servers as we can afford them.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Can I stream Helios or make YouTube videos?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Yes. If you are going to stream Helios, we ask that you add a watermark to the footage that says Helios is an Early Beta. If you let <a href="https://discord.com/invite/98upZDm" target="_blank">Horatio</a> know ahead of time he can hang out in the chat to provide info for your viewers who ask questions.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">For making YouTube videos, please announce at the start of the video that Helios is in an Early Access stage of development and include that information as well as a link to this website in the description.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Do I need a VR Headset to play Helios?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">A desktop mode has already been added to Helios. At this time, desktop mode is somewhat limited in how you can interact with your environment but we have added Tobii tracking support for Eyes and Head tracking.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Does Helios support Full Body Tracking?</strong> </h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Yes! Click <a href="https://www.helios-vr.com/setting-up-full-body-tracking " target="_blank">here</a> to learn more.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Will I be able to make my own avatar and use it in game?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Yes! Helios is being designed from the ground up to be as creator friendly as possible. Functionality has already been added to allow you to do this. We have also added tools to make the upload process as painless as possible through automation of most of the major steps. Keep in mind we are still in Early Access so pls no buly.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Can I make stuff for Helios?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">If you want to you are welcome to do so and we appreciate it. Check out our <a href="https://www.helios-vr.com/documentation" target="_blank">Documentation</a> page to learn more</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Will I be able to sell what I make in a Marketplace?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Yes! We want to make it easy for all of us to benefit from user creativity. You will be able to sell your creations and a small portion of the proceeds will go towards paying for the upkeep of the servers.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>What else can I do to help?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;"> Right now the best thing you can do to help us is spread the word. You can also help by following <a href="https://twitter.com/Helios_Game" target="_blank">@Helios_Game</a> on Twitter and subscribing to our <a href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCUNuQEGrgSjTftOkzcXZNmw">YouTube channel</a>.</p><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><strong>Do you have a patreon?</strong></h2><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">We do indeed! If you want to help us make the game by supporting us financially, please visit https://www.patreon.com/HelmBoyStudios.</p>
