<div class="text-center"><h4 id="section-1">About</h4></div>
<div class="text-start">
<p>Odania Sports Arena is a sci-fi VR game that allows players to hit targets coming from outer space. The game features different types of games where you can hit these targets.</p>
<p>This first-person VR game is controlled with motion controllers and offers the following games:</p>
<h2>Boxing</h2>
<p>Players take up avatars in a ring and must fight their way to victory in various rounds. Training on speed and punches is crucial, and the immersive experience allows players to feel the punches with the effects on their avatar. Players can start random rounds with different settings.</p>
<h2>SciFi Stick</h2>
<p>Baseball fans will enjoy hitting incoming SciFi balls with a stick. Can you hit the surrounding targets?</p>
<h2>Space Ball</h2>
<p>NBA fans get to play Space Ball, throwing balls in space and watching targets explode.</p>
<h2>Golf</h2>
<p>Ever played golf in space? Now you can! Golf enthusiasts can try playing golf in space.</p>
<h2>Key Elements</h2>
<p>Odania Sports Arena offers:</p>
<ul>
  <li>A wide variety of games</li>
  <li>Easy-to-learn gameplay</li>
  <li>Realistic feel</li>
  <li>Immersive experience</li>
</ul>
<p>Players have an initial collection of three ball games and one boxing match but there’s a chance to work on skills and improve gameplay. Get ready for this once-in-a-lifetime gaming experience!</p>
