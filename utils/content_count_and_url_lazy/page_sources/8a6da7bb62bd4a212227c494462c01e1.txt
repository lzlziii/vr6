<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #d5d0be;">Set on a remote island training facility for cold-war super-spies, Omega Agent sees the player strap on a prototype nuclear-powered jetpack and blast around an open world city filled with daring missions and breath-taking events in an attempt to graduate as the ultimate elite spy, the ‘Omega Agent’.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #d5d0be;">At first contact we found VR an awe-inspiring tech, so in designing our first game for it we wanted to stay true to the spellbinding nature of Virtual Reality yet&nbsp;simultaneously prove that VR and excitement can go together very well indeed. We took a lump of Pilot Wings playability, a spoonful of classic Bond-movie attitude, added a dash of The Jetsons for visual flair and created a game which we hope celebrates old-school values like great controls and simple yet striking visuals in a playful 1960’s environment. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #d5d0be;">Omega Agent is also available&nbsp;for HTC Vive,&nbsp;Oculus Rift&nbsp;&amp; Samsung Gear VR.&nbsp;</span></p>
