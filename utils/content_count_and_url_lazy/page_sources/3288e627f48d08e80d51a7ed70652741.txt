<h1><a id="policy-covers" name="policy-covers">What this policy covers</a></h1>

<p>Your privacy is important to us, and so is being transparent about how we collect, use and share information about you. This policy is intended to help you understand the following:</p>

<ul>
	<li>What information we collect about you</li>
	<li>How we use the information we collect</li>
	<li>How we share the information we collect</li>
	<li>How we store and secure the information we collect</li>
	<li>How to access and control your information</li>
	<li>How we transfer the information we collect internationally</li>
	<li>Other important privacy information</li>
</ul>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>This privacy policy covers the information we collect about you when you use our products or services, or otherwise interact with us (for example, by attending our events), unless a different policy is displayed.</p>

<p>We refer to all our games together with our websites as “Services” in this policy.</p>

<p>This policy also explains your rights about how the information we hold on you is used. Your rights include how you can object to certain uses of information about you and how you can access and update certain information about you.</p>

<p><strong>IF YOU DO NOT AGREE WITH THIS POLICY, DO NOT ACCESS OR USE OUR SERVICES OR INTERACT WITH ANY OTHER ASPECT OF OUR BUSINESS.</strong></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<h1><a id="information-we-collect">What information we collect on you</a></h1>

<h3><a id="overview">Overview</a></h3>

<p>We collect information about you when:</p>

<ul>
	<li>You provide it to us.</li>
	<li>Automatically when you use the Services.</li>
	<li>Data is provided to us by third parties.</li>
</ul>

<p>The information we collect includes account and contact information, device and connection information, Service information and logs.</p>

<h3><a id="information-you-provide">Information you provide to us</a></h3>

<p>We collect information about you when you input it into the Services or otherwise provide it directly to us.</p>

<p><strong><em>Account and Profile Information</em></strong>:</p>

<p>We collect information about you when you do one or more of the following on our online store:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Register for an account</li>
	<li>Modify your account</li>
	<li>Make a purchase through the Services</li>
</ul>

<p>The personal information we collect is the following:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Name</li>
	<li>Email</li>
	<li>Billing Address</li>
	<li>Delivery Address</li>
	<li>Telephone Number</li>
</ul>

<p>If you do not wish to supply the above information, then you will have limited access to our Services.</p>

<p><strong><em>Content you provide through our games</em></strong></p>

<p>When you play our games, we collect information on how you use the game and certain events which you encounter when you play them.</p>

<p>Some of our games require to have a registered account on Frontier’s online store. When you play these games on console, you still require a Frontier Developments account. The personal information we collect when you do this is:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Email</li>
</ul>

<p>Some of our games will require you to create an in-game profile. The personal information we collect when you do this is:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Username</li>
</ul>

<p>Your username is shown to other users who play the game. We suggest that you do not use your real name and you use a pseudonym instead.</p>

<p>Not all our games require you to have a registered account.</p>

<p>Should you experience any issues with the games, we provide tools which allow you to send us the following information:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Crash Dumps</li>
	<li>Dxdiag</li>
</ul>

<p>These will contain information about your computer.</p>

<p><strong><em>Content you provide through our websites</em></strong></p>

<p>We collect any content you submit to the websites owned and operated by us. The personal information we collect on these websites are:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Name</li>
	<li>Email Address</li>
</ul>

<p><strong><em>Information you provide through our support channels</em></strong></p>

<p>The Services also include our customer support, where you can submit information about a problem you experience with a Service. When you engage with our support website and/or team you will be asked to provide contact information, a summary of the problem and any other documentation.</p>

<p>The contact information we ask you to provide is one or more of the following:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Email Address</li>
	<li>Name</li>
	<li>Username</li>
</ul>

<p>If you do not provide an email address, our support team will not be able to resolve your problem.</p>

<p><strong><em>Information you provide outside our Services</em></strong></p>

<p>Information you provide to us is not always done via our Services but via third-party services, e.g. social media. The information we receive this way is one or more of the following:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Name</li>
	<li>Email</li>
	<li>Address</li>
</ul>

<p>For example, when we are running a Facebook competition for some physical merchandise, if you win, we will ask you to contact us via Facebook to provide contact details.</p>

<h3><a id="automatic-information-collected">Information we collect automatically when you use the Services</a></h3>

<p>We collect information about you when you use our Services, including browsing our websites and taking certain actions within the Services.</p>

<p><strong><em>Your use of the Services</em></strong></p>

<p>The information we collect automatically when you use our Services are:</p>

<ul>
	<li>How you interact with the Services.</li>
	<li>Events you trigger during the use of our Services.</li>
</ul>

<p>For example, when you log into Elite Dangerous we will record that you have logged into the game.&nbsp;</p>

<p>We also automatically keep track of your Internet Protocol (IP) Address when you interact with our Services in one or more of the following ways:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Creating and/or modifying your account</li>
	<li>Playing our games</li>
	<li>Interacting with our website</li>
</ul>

<p><strong><em>Device and Connection Information</em></strong></p>

<p>We collect information about your computer, phone, tablet or other devices you use to access the Services, which is anonymised and aggregated. The device information includes:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Operating system</li>
	<li>Browser type</li>
	<li>URLs or referring/exit pages</li>
	<li>Device identifiers</li>
</ul>

<p>How much of the information we can collect depends on the settings and/or plugins on your device.</p>

<p><strong><em>Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies</em></strong></p>

<p>Frontier Developments and our third-party partners, such as our advertising and analytics partners, use cookies and other tracking technologies (e.g. web beacons, device identifiers and pixels) to provide functionality and to recognise you across different Services and devices.</p>

<h3><a id="third-party-information">Information from third parties</a></h3>

<p>We receive information about you from third-party services when you interact with certain Services or information made public by other third-party services.</p>

<p><strong><em>Other services you link to your account</em></strong></p>

<p>We receive information about you when you link your account with a third party account. The information we collect this way is</p>

<ul>
	<li>Third-party user id</li>
</ul>

<p>For example, when you link your Steam account to a Frontier account, we receive your Steam ID.</p>

<p>The information we receive when you link our Services with a third-party service depends on the settings, permissions and privacy policy controlled by that third-party service. You should always check the privacy settings and notices in these third-party services to understand what data may be disclosed to us or shared with our Services.</p>

<p><strong><em>Information made public by third parties</em></strong></p>

<p>We receive information about you from information made public by third-parties via a website and/or API. The information we collect this way is</p>

<ul>
	<li>Third-party user id</li>
	<li>Posts</li>
	<li>Reviews</li>
</ul>

<p>For example, Steam makes all game reviews accessible via an API.</p>

<p>The information we receive depends on the settings, permissions and privacy policy controlled by that third-party service. You should always check the privacy settings and notices in these third-party services to understand what data may be disclosed to us or shared.</p>

<p>How we use the information we collect depends, in part, on the Services you use, how you use them and any preferences you have communicated to us. <p><strong><em>To provide the Services</em></strong></p>

<h1><a id="how-we-share-your-information" name="how-we-share-your-information">How we share the information we collect</a></h1>

<p>As we make online games, this means sharing information through the Services. We share information we collect about you in the ways discussed below. We do not sell your information to third parties.</p>

<h3><a id="share-with-other-service-users">Sharing with other Service users</a></h3>

<p>When you use the Service, we share certain information about you with other Service users.</p>

<p><strong><em>For game interactions</em></strong></p>

<p>We publish online games and these require users of the Service to have a username. This username is shared with other players for the following purposes:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Add other players as friends</li>
	<li>Allow for in game interaction via chat functionality</li>
	<li>Reward players who have made some in-game achievement, for example visited certain areas of the game first</li>
</ul>

<p>There are certain features within our games that make game data public on other Services. The information made public varies between game and feature, however players will be notified of this information before they start using the feature. In some cases, players may prevent this information being made public by not using the specific feature. Otherwise, players will need to stop using the game in its entirety.</p>

<p>Within our Services, certain games allow for players to play the game their own way and create their own story. Some of our games therefore generate logs which show certain player interactions. This allows players to build up their own story outside the game. When a player interacts with a second player under certain circumstances, the second player’s username will appear in the original player’s logs. The information which is in these logs is information which has already been made public by players interacting with our Services.</p>

<p>How this information is used is up to the player and Frontier Developments cannot control how this is used. This feature is fundamental to using certain Services. If you do not wish your username to appear in these logs, then do not interact with the Services. If you terminate your game account, your username will still remain on players' computers within these logs.</p>

<p><strong><em>Community Forums</em></strong></p>

<p>Our websites offer publicly accessible blogs, forums and issue trackers, e.g.&nbsp;<a href="https://forums.frontier.co.uk/" rel="nofollow">Frontier Forums</a>. You should be aware that any information you provide on these websites – including profile information associated with the account you use to post the information, may be read, collected and used by any member of the public who accesses these websites. Your posts and certain profile information may remain even after your terminate your account. We strongly recommend you to consider the sensitivity of any information you input into these Services. To request removal of your information from publicly accessible websites operated by us, please contact us as detailed below. In some cases, we may not be able to remove your information, in which case we will let you know if we are unable to and why.</p>
