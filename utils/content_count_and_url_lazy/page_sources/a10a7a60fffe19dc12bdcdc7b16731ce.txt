		<p>A Athletics Games 2020 VR for Oculus Rift/HTC Vive/Steam VR just arrived !</p>
<p>You will feel inside a stadium where you can practice and compete in different athletics sports. The public will encourage you to achieve your goals, improve the times and distances of the World and Olympic Records</p>
<p><span id="more-384"></span></p>
<p>Oculus:&nbsp;<a href="https://www.oculus.com/experiences/rift/1959819677374519" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://www.oculus.com/experiences/rift/1959819677374519</a></p>
<p>Steam:&nbsp;<a href="https://store.steampowered.com/app/1029660/Athletics_Games_VR/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://store.steampowered.com/app/1029660/Athletics_Games_VR/</a></p>
<p>First game in which you can participate in&nbsp;Athletics through Virtual Reality.</p>
<p>Athletism proves like:<br>
– 100 meters flat<br>
– Long jump<br>
– Shot of weight<br>
– High jump<br>
– 400 meters flat<br>
– 110 meters hurdles<br>
– Disk throw<br>
– Pole vault<br>
– Javelin throw<br>
– 1500 meters flat</p>
<p>There are instructions with videos within the game but it is very easy to manage.<br>
For running you have to move the controls with natural movement of your hands as if you were running. Hold the trigger button down while moving. As speeddy as you could.<br>
For jumping you have to press face button one.<br>
For throwing you have to grab the object and launch it with natural movement.<br>
In javelin throw you have to grab, run after line limit and throw it.<br>
In shot put or discus you have to grab, move a little with point ray an throw it.<br>
In long jump you have to run and press face button one before arrive to line limit for jumping.</p>
<p>and many more…</p>
