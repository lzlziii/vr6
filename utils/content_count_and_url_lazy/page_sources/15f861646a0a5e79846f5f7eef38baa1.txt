		<h1 class="entry-title">Indie games. Angry llama attitude.</h1>	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Why indie games?</h3>



<p>There is something special about indie games. They are unique. They are labors of love. They take risks and explore new stories and new ways to tell stories.</p>



<p>At Lama glama, we want to tell some unique stories that come from our lives and we have some unique ideas. And we kinda like being our own bosses.</p>



<p></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Why the attitude?</h3>



<p>Angry llama don’t care what everyone else is doing. </p>



<p>We are going to do our thing. We make games because it’s our passion to try new things. To make things that are innovative and a little edgy. We’re not afraid to fall flat on our face…we’ll just laugh at ourselves, get up and try something else. Or, we’ll say “screw it” and go fishing.</p>



<p>Unlike the “big boys” in the game development world, we don’t answer to board members, shareholders and CEO’s…we answer to you, the player. <a href="https://discord.gg/WuUDfbeu7r">And we actually care what you say</a>. At the end of the day, we’re going to have fun making games that you have fun playing. So we hope you’ll join us on this journey.</p>
