                                                        return;

                                                    var xdiff = g_TouchEventStart.originalEvent.changedTouches[0].screenX - eventEnd.originalEvent.changedTouches[0].screenX;
                                                    var ydiff = g_TouchEventStart.originalEvent.changedTouches[0].screenY - eventEnd.originalEvent.changedTouches[0].screenY;

                                                    g_TouchEventStart = null;

																			<h1>为何要采用抢先体验这种模式？</h1>
										“Welcome to our Steam Early Access page! We've chosen to release our game in this state for a few key reasons:<br>
<br>
1. Your Feedback Matters: We believe that player feedback is essential to making a great game, and by releasing our game in Early Access, we can get your thoughts and opinions on the game's mechanics, balance, and overall feel. Your feedback will help us to improve the game and make it the best it can be.<br>
2. Continued Development: Early Access also helps us to secure funding to continue the game's development. By releasing the game in an unfinished state, we can generate revenue that we can use to add more content, features, and polish to the game.<br>
3. Community Building: Early Access allows us to build a community around the game and connect with players who are passionate about what we're creating. We're excited to work with our community to create a game that everyone will love.<br>
<br>
Thank you for joining us on this journey, and we look forward to hearing your feedback!”

										<h1>这款游戏的抢先体验状态大约持续多久？</h1>
										“Q4 2023”

										<h1>计划中的完整版本和抢先体验版本到底有多少不同？</h1>
										“We plan on making this game competitive, fun, and replayable. In the game’s current state, while *really* fun, it has yet to provide some of the key features that players have come to expect from modern games. These are:<br>
<br>
1. Ranking systems - Competitive game modes<br>
2. Player hangout maps - minigames and player arenas<br>
3. Player customization - Purchaseable and unlockable<br>
4. Alternative game modes<br>
5. Player progression - Levelling and achievements<br>
6. More maps <br>
7. More heroes and abilities<br>
<br>
We feel that once we have all of these, that player will enjoy copious amounts of replayability and satisfactory climbs through the rankings.”

										<h1>抢先体验版本的现状如何？</h1>
										“- Fully playable in its current state, in our search-and-destroy game mode, with 6 heroes and their unique abilities, with or without bots in their games. As players join bot games, they take over the spot of a bot, up to 6 players in a game.<br>
- Our tutorial is fully implemented, and we have a practice shooting range for players to learn what abilities do, and how weapons and hero items work.<br>
- Players can team up with their friends from their Steam friends list and join games together on the same team.”

										<h1>在抢先体验期间和结束之后，游戏价格会有所不同吗？</h1>
										“There will be external paid DLCs packs very close to post-launch as well as IAP skin system, and the game will be pay-to-play upon full release. We’re still discussing the price range depending on the size of the player base, how much we can get into the game by the release date, and how much demand there will be for our player customization system. Since we’re making this game very player-driven, to the point where we’re co-developing with the community, it will be up to players how the game turns out. Going into early access will give us a better idea of what that will be.”

										<h1>在开发过程中，你们是如何计划让玩家社区参与进来的？</h1>
										“We have been working directly with our Discord community and with high-level Esports teams, for the past 6 months. We have done so through our Beta 1 and 2 release rounds which have been very popular with our players.<br>
<br>
Also, we have conducted polls, surveys, and answered all questions and feedback posts our community’s had. We have applied over 70% of user feedback into our game. This ranged from big UI changes, QoL changes to full-fledged features, and we recorded an extremely positive response from the player base. Moving forward, we will continue to provide:<br>
Regular updates and communication:<br>
- We will keep our community informed about the game's progress through regular updates on our social media channels and Discord server. This will include concept art, gameplay footage, and developer insights.<br>
- Open communication: We value our community's feedback and suggestions, and we will actively seek their input throughout the development process. This will include regular Q&amp;A sessions, polls, surveys, and other interactive activities on our Discord server.<br>
- Community events and contests: We plan on hosting events and contests on our Discord server to encourage community involvement and reward their participation. This could include things like fan art contests, trivia contests, and more.<br>
<br>
Overall, we strongly believe that by involving the community in the development process, that we will achieve a great meta balance, and have the QoL, social and ranking systems players expect from competitive shooters.”
