description: An extension of the iconic LIFE brand, LIFE VR brings you immersive experiences from some of today’s most beloved Time Inc. brands, including TIME, People, Sports Illustrated, Travel + Leisure, InStyle, Essence and many more.  LIFE VR elevates Time Inc.’s acclaimed storytelling capabilities to new levels through 360° video and interactive virtual reality experiences to engage every passion, including news, entertainment, sports, fashion, history, food, and travel—all with new content curated every month.  This Windows Mixed Reality app is one of many platforms from which you can explore LIFE VR’s growing slate of world-class stories and original programming. Others include Steam for the HTC Vive; Samsung Gear VR; and Oculus Rift.  A short list of LIFE VR’s most recent experiences:  * Take Every Wave: Laird in VR * Capturing Everest * The Solar Eclipse in 360 * Travel + Leisure’s World’s Best City * Dunkirk VR: Save Every Breath * The Crystal Reef * Rogue One: A Star Wars Story in 360  Visit Time.com/lifevr for more information and a sneak peek at upcoming content.
