name: International Space Station Tour VR
description: Educational tour of the International Space Station (ISS). Incredible 360º technology allows you to feel like an astronaut. Visit International Space Station in VR. With high-quality photos created by European Space Agency and videos recorded by astronaut Samantha Cristoforetti you can feel like you are in space. Lure at the Earth from 400 km and check how is to levitate in weightlessness.  INTERNATIONAL SPACE STATION TOUR VR – MAIN FEATURES • The ability to watch International Space Station movies while virtually inside the Station • An amazing, 3D model of ISS that can be placed in Microsoft’s Mixed Reality Cliff House • A completely free, immersive experience of being an astronaut working on ISS   This experience is created by The House of Fables. ESA is not an author of the application. ESA holds all rights for photos and videos used.
