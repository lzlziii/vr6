name: Vending Machine - I can do it
description: This is an entertainment app that allows the user to have the experience of buying drinks from a vending machine.  After starting the app, a vending machine will move according to where you are looking. Tap to place the vending machine. Once placed, the vending machine can be moved or rotated by grasping one of the square objects to the left. Tap a button to eject a drink. There is no limit to how many will come out. Tapping a drink that has come out of the machine will display it in the air in front of you. Tapping the garbage can will clean up the ejected drinks.
