name: Visualization Experience
description: Theorem Solutions Visualization Experience - Free Trial  Theorem Solutions Visualization Experience shows how you can visualize, prepare and present your CAD data for visualization in HoloLens.  Taking direct input from all major CAD systems, Theorem's Visualization Experience prepares and optimizes the data for use in a Mixed Reality Environment and allows the user to navigate and interrogate the data using HoloLens gestures and voice controls.  The In-App tutorial will show you how to make best use of the Experience, including how to load and manipulate CAD data, explode the assembly structure and interrogate individual assembly components.  By downloading and registering to use the Visualization Experience, you will be provided with a free 90 day trial providing access to data hosted on Theorem's web based Experience Server.
