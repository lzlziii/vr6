name: Bricks VR
description: Relax and enjoy immersive Virtual Reality classic puzzle game. Move and rotate the bricks as they fall, attempting to fit them together. When you completely fill one horizontal line with colored bricks, that line disappears and all bricks above move down. If the bricks land on top of the game field, the game is over.  To move, rotate and drop bricks use Motion Controller, Keyboard or Xbox One controller.  Please ensure that you have Windows Mixed Reality headset to play this game.
