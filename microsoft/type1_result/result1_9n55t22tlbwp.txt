name: Orinox Model Review Holographic
description: Why don't you use the latest visualization technology available to enhance your engineering and design data ?   ORINOX is proud to introduce its new Windows Holographic app, Orinox Model Review (OMR) application now available for download. OMR is designed to be connected to our cloud based platform OCWS, for storage and management of Digital Asset. The Digital Asset is the Digital Twin of your physical asset. Our platform capture the Tags and Technical Information (1D/2D/3D) of your asset. After creating a new project you can easily drop on this platform AVEVA rvm or AUTODESK fbx file, directly from your favourite design software.   This solution for Digital asset visualisation is also compliant with BIM, Building Information Modelling or Better Information Management. But also PLM, Plant Lifecycle Management or ALM, Asset LifeCycle Management.
