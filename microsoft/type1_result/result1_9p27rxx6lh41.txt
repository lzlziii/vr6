name: Window Breaker
description: On this app, you can put a window on the wall, and break it.  * After a while it starts, you can put a window with tap gesture. * When you tap the window, it will break. * When you tap the broken window, it will be removed. * If you lower your line of sight, the menu will come up. * All windows will be deleted by pressing the reset button.   You can also use voice command.
