name: MultiMeasure
description: The future of measurement is here. MultiMeasure takes accurate, hands-free measurements of the real world (including people). The process is simple. First, generate a holographic mesh by scanning an object. Second, add points to specify measurements. Points can be manipulated using gaze, gestures, and voice commands. Easily add multiple measurements and calculate unit conversions right in the app.  MultiMeasure is a free application designed and developed by Mondo Robot, a digital agency leading exploration into mixed and virtual reality. A full library of features and voice commands are provided in the release notes.
