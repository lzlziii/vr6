name: Holo F(X)
description: Holo F(X) is an educational application designed to help students learn math better. It aims to deliver a whole new level of visual and interactive experience to learning. Utilizing HoloLens's AR technology, Holo F(X)  allow students to get a more direct view of mathematical graphs generated from any math function and equation. By inputing your formula into the Holo F(X) calculator interface, mathematical graphs are then generated in full virtual 2D and 3D forms, and projected directly in front of the user for a more thralling and awesome experience, giving the user a much more direct idea of how the math function work. For any parent, teacher or high schooler eager to find new and fun approach to math learning, Holo F(X) is just the thing you need.
