name: MR: Move out of the Room
description: MR: Move out of the Room is a short Escape the Room game based on VR: Vacate the Room.  It is a VR Experience where you need to find clues to solve puzzles and find your way out of the Room. There are some items that can be grabbed and carried around. With some items you can interact (such as switches and numpads).  Please notice: - You will need a Mixed Reality Headset - Motion Controllers are required - A room size of at least 2m x 2m is recommended. - Please make sure that you can reach at least a height of 2.10 meters. - Hint: There are 2 Endings!
