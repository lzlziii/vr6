name: HoloRail
description: HoloRail is a game for HoloLens that lets you build a simple railroad in your living room. You can use a builder to add different kind of rails, including turns, bridges and tunnels. You can rotate and position your scene according to your liking and then pick one of the two train types and watch and hear it roll on your new tracks. Game allows you to save you scene for later play without having to build it from scratch. Please rate our application. Enjoy exploring Microsoft HoloLens. Voice commands you can use are the following: Menu – shows or hides a menu in a game scene  Move – switching state into movement on main track  Reset – train returns to the beginning of the track  Play – starts the train, but only if track ends with a finish rail piece (FINISH) Undo – deletes the last built rail piece Rail – adds a straight rail piece  Bend – builds a regular bend rail piece  Bend Tiny – builds a tiny rail piece  Bend Small – builds a small rail piece  Bridge – builds a bridge rail piece  Finish – builds a finish end rail piece  Stop – stops a moving train  Continue – resumes train movement Change – changes a train type deployed on the rail  Clear – delete all built rail pieces, except for the start position (clean table) Builder – shows or hides a builder menu used for building of rails Load – shows a menu for loading a game from a saved position  Save – shows a menu for saving a game position  Exit – exits to the start menu
