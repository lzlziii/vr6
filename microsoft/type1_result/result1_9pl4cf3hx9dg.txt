name: Apples Crusher
description: Fun game where you can crush some apples by drawing lines to cut them using touch, mouse, pen. If you've got a Windows Mixed Reality headset, you can even use light swords or banana guns in VR! :)  For VR users: when you'll connect your VR headset, the UI/start button will change its text from "Play" to "Play in VR". Clicking on this button will enter the immersive mode to let you play using either with light swords or banana guns.  Technical details:  - built on top of WebGL/WebVR/WebAudio thanks to babylon.js - built as a PWA.
