name: Holographic Dream Car
description: How do you imagine your dream car? Many people picture it as a luxury red sports car with a charismatically roaring powerful engine. Sounds exciting? With Holographic Dream Car application for Hololens you have an opportunity to see the car of your dreams in a holographic mode.  The application supports several functions, such as:  Rotate: enables horizontal rotating of the model. Scale: enables scaling mode to change the size of the model. Sound: turns on the sound of the engine.  Now you have an opportunity to look at this amazing sports car any time you want. Just run the app on your Hololens and see the holographic magic. Oh yes, and don't forget to ask someone to take a photo of you next to a cool car!
