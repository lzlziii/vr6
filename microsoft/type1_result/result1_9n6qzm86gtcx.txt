name: V8 Engine
description: A four-stroke engine has eight cylinder in V configuration. The cylinders are mounted on the crankcase in two sets of four, with all the pistons moving a common crankshaft. It is also known as a V8 engine.  In this app you will be able to:  - Explore the design of a V8 engine. - Explain each part of a V8 engine. - Describe the four strokes which help in working of the V8 engine.
