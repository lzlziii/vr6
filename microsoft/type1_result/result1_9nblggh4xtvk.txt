name: Ghost Invasion
description: The latest game from Appetizer Mobile is here!  Ghost Invasion is an exciting first person shooter game where you battle ghosts as they shoot fireballs and explode near you! Defeat ghosts, collect points, and buy new weapons.  Start the game by picking your weapon and then selecting the difficulty level from the menu. Then, enter the game and watch for portals that can spawn anywhere around you. To use your weapon, perform an ‘air tap’ gesture with your hand.  Ghost Invasion uses the incredible technology of the Microsoft Hololens to make a 3D map of your room and intelligently spawn portals onto your walls, doors, tables, ceiling, and more! Ghosts bounce off of walls, and the slime splashes when it hits a surface. The gesture controls are very simple to learn, and the ability to walk around your room or house while playing makes each and every person have a unique ghost hunting experience.
