name: bitnamic CONNECT
description: Master service challenges with bitnamic CONNECT!  The app for mobile and worldwide collaboration in service and maintenance offers you live video and voice communication from on-site technicians (with hololens) to one or more remote experts. The image quality is especially optimized for service and maintenance. Additional features include chat and whiteboard collaboration as well as spatial laserpointer and annotations. To keep full control of all content, bitnamic CONNECT is secured with latest encryption technologies.  Requirements to establish a service session: - valid account on vios.bitnamic.net including a hololens-license - communicate the service information to the expert  Time limited test accounts and licenses for your company can be created upon request. Contact us for demos, accounts, licenses or further questions: - write an email to hololens@bitnamic.net - or visit www.bitnamic.net
