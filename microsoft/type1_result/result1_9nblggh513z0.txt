name: Holocule
description: Holocule is a DirectX Hololens app that dynamically renders molecules. It is integrated with 3rd party services to dynamically load molecules from the Internet. Holocule supports manipulation of molecules including zoom and rotate. When you load a molecule, first place the molecule in the right location (such as on a table) then begin your interactions. You can also change how the molecule is rendered. Use voice commands to invoke interactions.
