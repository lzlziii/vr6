name: Holographic Academy: Interactions
description: Holographic Academy course Holograms 210, 211, 212. Use your gaze to navigate the cursor on holograms. Find your holograms using the directional indicator around the cursor off holograms. Highlight different astronaut components using your gaze. Gaze at the center chest component and perform the air tap gesture and observe the tag along object.   Rotate the astronaut using your gestures. Say "Move Astronaut" voice command and move the astronaut to where it matters to you!  Gaze at the astronaut's wrist panel and say "Open Communicator". Record a message using voice dictation and then play it back. When ready send that message to the astronaut.
