name: Mixed Reality Concepts by Actimage
description: This bundle of 5 applications has been developed to explore different fields and sets of functionalities. With different interactions in every application you discover new ways to interact with the HoloLens technology.   • Museum: Usage of image recognition to display information about the painting Liberty Leading the People. We wanted to explore to future of museum visit with augmented reality. • BIM Visualisation: Shows the possibilities of using BIM (Building Information Modeling) with HoloLens and using two handed gestures. • Factory Layout: Presents how to plan the implementation of new machines in a factory. They can be placed anywhere and a warning will be displayed if the machine enters in collision with virtual or real elements. • Data Visualisation: HoloLens application which communicate with a PC, you can use the computer mouse in both application. This application also shows 3D charts and how we could work in a near future. • E-Commerce: Imagine how could be the future of e-shopping with HoloLens ?   These concepts have been realized in a laps of a week to provide visibility of a fraction of the mixed reality possibilities.
