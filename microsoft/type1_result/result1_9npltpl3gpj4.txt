name: Holo Plane
description: Fly with a holographic plane and drop your bombs on the anti aircraft guns!  Control the plane with the "Holo Plane Remote Control" companion app for your android smartphone: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.danielreichert.HoloPlaneRemoteControl  To connect this app with its companion app follow this steps:  1. Connect your HoloLens and your Smartphone to the same wifi. 2. Open HoloLens Settings > Wifi > Advanced Options and note your HoloLens IP Adress 3. Open HoloPlane on your HoloLens 4. Open this app "Holo Plane Remote Control" on your Smartphone and enter the IP of your HoloLens. 5. Press "Start" Button. Your holographic plane should now fly  (If you have no wifi available you can open an ad-hoc wifi with your android device)
