name: KADO MEP Layout
description: KADO Demo is a limited version of the application aimed to demonstrate architects, engineers, construction teams and facility managers the capabilities of KADO framework and what can AR/MR do for construction industry. The demo was designed as a tool to help MEP engineers to do point layout on site.  KADO Demo is only a showcase of what can be built on top of KADO framework. Every Construction company has unique requirements for AR Software solutions, which is why KADO supports multiple tools that give the companies the flexibility to pick the right building blocks for your next AR tool or product without additional hassle and coding from scratch.
