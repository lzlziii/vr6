name: Holo-Machine
description: Holo-Machine lets you create your scenic vision using holograms of shapes, scenery, people and lights.  With Holo-Machine creatives from any industry, but mainly theatre and entertainment, can start playing around with shapes, colours and ideas at the beginning of your design journey.
