name: DC Motor
description: A DC motor or direct current motor is a device that converts electrical energy (direct current system) into mechanical energy. DC motor works on the principle, when a current carrying conductor is placed in a magnetic field, it experiences a torque and has a tendency to rotate.  In this app you will be able to:  1. Explore the internal view of a DC motor. 2. Describe the parts used in construction of a DC motor. 3. Explore the effect on the rotation speed of DC motor by:  -varying the current  -varying the number of turns in the coil
