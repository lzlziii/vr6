name: TPC Scottsdale Experience
description: Experience the PGA TOUR like never before on Windows HoloLens!   Amaze yourself and your friends by placing a 3D map of TPC Scottsdale on your coffee table and seeing the course from a birds eye view.   With the TPC Scottsdale experience, you will be able to:   * Show a map of the course in 3D in your living room or any home or office space.   * Zoom in to view details of any hole on the course, with special attention to the Clubhouse and the most exciting hole in golf - #16 at TPC Scottsdale.    * View all of your favorite players by hole and see their shot trails as they battled towards victory during the Waste Management Phoenix Open.   * Compare two players on each hole to see how they performed and the distance they achieved with each shot.   * View heat maps of each hole to see where most ball traffic occurred during the tournament.   * Look at real time weather in Scottsdale, AZ.   The TPC Scottsdale experience is the first holographic golf application designed to enhance your enjoyment of the sport, and to enhance your perspective of a tournament. Download it today.
