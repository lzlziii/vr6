name: HoloIndEqp
description: This is an app to show lathe machine in HoloLens. It can be used to experience machine operation, view introductions of the main parts of lathe machine. Also, you can see the internal structure of the machine.  Instructions: 1.Air tap the red button to turn on the machine. 2.Tap and hold the carriage handwheel, then drag from right to left or from left to right. 3.Gaze to view the introduction of the main parts.
