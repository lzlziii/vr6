description: The Program-Ace's most featured hero - Archy, the Rabbit - is now ready to feel the power of Microsoft HoloLens. He is excited to help children and adults explore the world of holographic computing. Specially designed to showcase all of the mixed reality capacities, the application will be a perfect match for children's natural curiosity and adult's passion for new technologies.   Using this application is a piece of a cake. It is fully responsive to all standard HoloLens gestures and is sensitive to voice commands as well. To help you adapt using it, we have designed a tutorial that will show you how to control and manage the app, what voice command to use, and how to interact with the rabbit. You may enjoy playing with Archy, this fluffy little cutie, without the necessity to be connected to the Internet. All you need to have fun are HoloLens and a good mood!
