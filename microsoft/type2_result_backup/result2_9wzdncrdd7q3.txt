name: MobileHMI
description: MobileHMI is a real-time, business and visual intelligence (BI) mobile solution for manufacturing, industrial, building and energy applications. This secure mobile app allows executives, managers, engineers, operators and technicians to remain informed from anywhere and to have instant, one-touch access to key operational performance indicators (KPIs). Download to instantly view critical information on one of hundreds of smartphones, tablets, Web browsers or Web-enabled devices.    For increased efficiency users can access real-time and historical KPI's, trends and charts. For responsiveness alarms can be acknowledged and monitored remotely. Users can also navigate and drill-down to any asset, view and control operations through interactive graphics. Providing ground-breaking Smart Tile overviews, AppHub™ consistency and simple configuration MobileHMI makes things fast and easy to deploy. Quickly view remote mission critical information on any Microsoft, Apple or Android device through innovative HTML5 and Windows RT technology.  MobileHMI reduces the time it takes resolve issues by allowing users to create rich, mobile dashboards for any application. Designed for security, reliability and simplicity MobileHMI is a must-have mobile software app for anyone with a need to monitor critical information remotely from their favorite smart phone, tablet or web browser.
