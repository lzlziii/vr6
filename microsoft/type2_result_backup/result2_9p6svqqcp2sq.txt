name: Walk the World
description: View ANY PLACE in the world in 3D, either with HoloLens or your immersive headset! Shows OpenStreetMap and 4 other Bing and Google Maps on the floor. You can navigate *by walking over it* (or teleport if your are using Mixed Reality). The map will recenter on you if you walk too far from the center. Zoom in, zoom out switch between 2D and 3D, and move to other places by voice commands or via your controller. Also includes 10 predefined vistas of famous places.   Tap the map anywhere to get the address of the location you tapped on. Press Controller pad up for zoom in, down for zoom out. Vista menu is under menu button. Grab button will show a keyboard with speech recognition to navigate around. In-app help screen for available speech commands.  Demo movie for immersive headsets: https://youtu.be/YvLUVL1f-A0 Demo movie for HoloLens: https://youtu.be/xKUcntR5TLY
