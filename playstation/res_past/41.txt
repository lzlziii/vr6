tag_name THE KNIGHT OF QUEEN (Simplified Chinese, English, Japanese)
tag_jj “THE KNIGHT OF QUEEN” is a Role-Playing Game which a player can experience the world of the RPGs played around 1990 which is fully playable in VR. The game has a medieval world and a nostalgic one-on-one turn-based command input battle system.  You play as a knight of a medieval queen and explore a world ruled by the Devil King who threatens peace by defeating various monsters and collecting various information and items.
