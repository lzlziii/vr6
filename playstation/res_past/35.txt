Fight. Fall. Rise Again. Prepare for the fight of your life. Move your arms and body to the synthwave soundtrack as you attack, block, cast spells, and dodge your way deeper into the heart of a neon fantasy world filled with magic-infused monsters. Are you the Champion that Rokar needs?
